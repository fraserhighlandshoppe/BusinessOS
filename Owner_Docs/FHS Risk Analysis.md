# FHS Risk Analysis

## Overview
Risk assessment to identify, evaluate, and manage risks that could impact Fraser Highland Shoppe.

## Risk Management Strategies
1. **Avoidance** - Eliminate, withdraw, or not become involved
2. **Reduction** - Optimize and mitigate
3. **Sharing** - Transfer (outsourcing or insurance)
4. **Retention** - Accept and budget

## Identified Risks

### Not Enough Demand for Products
**Risk**: Building customer base takes time

**Strategy**: Retention
- Retain risk - don't hold no product
- As items sell, judge turnover rate
- Order replacement to cover turnover

### Difficulty Acquiring New Customers
**Risk**: Slow customer acquisition

**Strategies**:
- Reduction - Optimize marketing efforts
- Sharing - Outsource or use insurance
- Retention - Accept and budget

### Difficulty Acquiring Product
**Risk**: Suppliers out of stock or slow to replenish

**Strategies**:
- **Reduction**: Order with lots of lead time
- **Avoidance**: Find alternative suppliers for equivalent products

### Difficulty Acquiring Accounts with Suppliers
**Risk**: Slow supplier response

**Strategies**:
- **Reduction/Sharing**: Get referred by existing contacts
- **Avoidance**: Don't seek further suppliers if ineffective
- **Reduction**: Leave lead time, follow up consistently

### Competition
**Risk**: Competition from industry leaders (TartanTown)

**Analysis**:
- Competition has experience and reputation
- World class pipers running/competing
- We can avoid through strong USP
- Reduce through pricing, service, USP

**Strategy**: Avoid retention, minimize sharing

**Note**: We have technical advantage to out-tech/out-Social Media, but is that best use of time?

### Loss of Property

#### Fire or Natural Disaster
**Strategy**: Insurance

#### War
**Strategy**: Insurance, legal action

#### Government Confiscation
**Strategy**: Insurance, legal action

#### Illegal Use
**Strategy**: Periodic inspections, explicit lease provisions

#### Theft
**Strategy**: Insurance, theft reduction strategies

#### Accident
**Strategy**: Insurance, periodic inspections

### Partial Loss of Property

#### Fire or Natural Disaster
**Strategy**: Insurance, periodic inspections, immediate repairs

#### Accidental Damage
**Strategy**: Insurance, periodic inspections

#### Intentional Damage
**Strategy**: Insurance, periodic inspections

### Liability due to Property

#### Negligence
**Strategy**: Insurance

#### Accidents
**Strategy**: Insurance

#### Illegal Use Lawsuits
**Strategy**: Insurance, legal protection

## Risk Assessment Framework

### Risk Matrix
| Probability \ Impact | Low | Medium | High |
|---|---|---|---|
| **High** | Medium | High | Critical |
| **Medium** | Low | Medium | High |
| **Low** | Low | Low | Medium |

### Risk Scoring
- **Probability**: 1-5 (Rare to Almost Certain)
- **Impact**: 1-5 (Negligible to Catastrophic)
- **Risk Score** = Probability × Impact

## Best Practices
- Regularly review and update risk assessment
- Implement appropriate mitigation strategies
- Maintain adequate insurance coverage
- Document risk management decisions
