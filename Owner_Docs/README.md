# Owner Docs

This directory contains documents owned and managed by the business owner(s) for external stakeholders including banks, investors, shareholders, and partners.

## Purpose

These documents are the authoritative source for:
- Business information for lenders and investors
- Ownership structure and governance
- Financial and operational summaries
- Strategic direction and goals

## Key Documents

### Business Strategy
- **Business Plan.md** - Comprehensive business strategy, structure, and financial projections
- **Marketing Plan.md** - Marketing strategy and approach
- **Sales Process.md** - Sales cycle and customer journey

### Governance & Structure
- **Mission.md** - Business mission and purpose (hardlinked)
- **Vision.md** - Long-term vision and goals (hardlinked)
- **Core Values.md** - Core values and culture (hardlinked)
- **SWOT.md** - Strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, threats analysis (hardlinked)
- **FHS Department Structure.md** - Organizational structure

### Operations & Risk
- **Risk Analysis.md** - Business risks and mitigation strategies
- **Referral Program.md** - Customer referral strategy
- **FHS Sales Objectives.md** - Sales goals and targets
- **FHS Refund Policy.md** - Return and refund policies
- **FHS Partnership Agreement.md** - Partnership terms

### Financial & Legal
- **FHS Sales Cycle and Customer Journey.md** - Customer acquisition process
- **FHS Sales Objectives.md** - Sales performance targets

## Relationship to Other Repositories

These documents are also available in the **MOS (Management Operating System)** repository as hardlinks. The MOS is a subsection of the BOS focused on day-to-day management operations.

**Authoritative Source**: This BOS repository is the single source of truth. Documents in MOS are hardlinked copies.
