# Business Operating System (BOS)

A comprehensive business operating system for Fraser Highland Shoppe, incorporating marketing, sales, operations, finance, HR, and project management knowledge.

## Structure

### Domains
- `/domains/marketing/` - Marketing strategies and tactics
- `/domains/sales/` - Sales processes and techniques  
- `/domains/business/` - Business frameworks and models
- `/domains/mindset/` - Leadership and mindset development
- `/domains/trading/` - Trading knowledge and skills
- `/domains/hr/` - Human resources and team management
- `/domains/linkedin/` - LinkedIn strategies
- `/domains/social_media/` - Social media by platform
- `/domains/email/` - Email marketing and automation
- `/domains/web/` - Web and landing page optimization
- `/domains/operations/` - Business operations and SOPs
- `/domains/project_management/` - PMBOK, BABOK, project templates

### Project Management
- `/domains/project_management/pmbok/` - Project Management Body of Knowledge
- `/domains/project_management/babok/` - Business Analysis Body of Knowledge
- `/domains/project_management/skills/` - AI helper skills
- `/domains/project_management/gannt/` - Gantt chart templates
- `/domains/project_management/quality/` - Quality management
- `/domains/project_management/procurement/` - Procurement management
- `/domains/project_management/integrated/` - Integrated project management

### Knowledge Base
- `/knowledge_base/frameworks/` - Business frameworks and models

### Templates
- `/templates/email/` - Email templates by use case
- `/templates/document/` - Document templates
- `/templates/process/` - Process templates

### AI Helpers
- `/ai_helpers/project_manager/` - AI assistance for project creation
- `/ai_helpers/business_analyst/` - AI assistance for requirements

### References

## Getting Started

1. **Project Management**: Start with `/domains/project_management/pmbok/PMBOK.md`
2. **Business Analysis**: Review `/domains/project_management/babok/BABOK.md`
3. **Marketing**: See `/domains/marketing/`
4. **Sales**: See `/domains/sales/`
5. **Operations**: See `/domains/operations/sop/`

## AI Helper Usage

Each AI helper directory contains a `skills.md` file with:
- Core competencies the AI should demonstrate
- Prompt engineering templates for common tasks
- Related templates and resources

Example prompts:
```
You are a senior project manager with PMP certification. Help me create a project charter.
You are an experienced business analyst. Help me create requirements specification.
You are a QA lead. Help me create test cases.
```
