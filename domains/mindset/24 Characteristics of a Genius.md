# 24 Characteristics of a Genius

Source: Grant Cardone (Growth Con 2024)
Reference: [geniusworld.com](https://geniusworld.com/24-characteristics-of-a-genius/)

## Drive
Strong desire to work hard and long. Willing to give all to a project. Develop by focusing on future success and keep going.

## Outgoingness
Make friends easily, be easy on friends. Be a "booster" not someone who puts others down. Wins valuable friends.

## Courage
Do things others consider impossible. Stop worrying what people think if you're different.

## Ability to Communicate
Effectively get ideas across. Take every opportunity to explain ideas to others.

## Devotion to Goals
Know what you want and go after it. Control your life and schedule. Have specific daily accomplishments.

## Patience
Be patient with others, but always impatient with yourself. Expect more of yourself than others.

## Knowledge
Continually accumulate information. Never sleep without learning at least one new thing. Read. Question experts.

## Perception
Mental radar working full time. Think more about others' needs than your own.

## Honesty
Frank, forthright, honest. Take responsibility for things that go wrong. Admit mistakes, learn from them.

## Perfectionism
Cannot tolerate mediocrity, especially in self. Never satisfied. Always strive to do better.

## Optimism
Never doubt success. Deliberately focus on something good coming up.

## Sense of Humor
Laugh at your own expense. Don't take offense when joke is on you.

## Ability to Judge
Understand facts before judging. Evaluate on open-minded, unprejudiced basis. Willing to change mind.

## Versatility
More things learned = more confidence. Don't shy from new endeavors.

## Enthusiasm
Excited about what you're doing, encourages cooperation. Believe things will turn out well. Don't hold back.

## Adaptability
Flexible to adapt to changing circumstances. Resist doing things old way. Consider new options.

## Willingness to Take Chances
Overcome fear of failure. Learn from mistakes, not afraid to try.

## Curiosity
Inquisitive mind seeks new information. Don't admit you know it all. Ask questions about things you don't understand.

## Dynamic Energy
Don't wait for good things. Be determined to make them happen.

## Individualism
Do things your way without fearing disapproval.

## Enterprise
Opportunity seekers. Take on jobs others won't. Try unknown.

## Idealism
Keep feet on ground but head in clouds. Achieve great things for betterment of mankind.

## Persuasion
Know how to motivate people to help you get ahead. Persuasive if you believe in what you're doing.

## Imagination
Think in new combinations, different perspectives. Unclutter mind. Time for daydreaming, fantasizing, inner life.
