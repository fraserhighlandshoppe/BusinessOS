# Grant Cardone 10X Boot Camp

## Key Principles

### Intention
Put your intention out into the universe.

### Awareness
Be aware of people around you - they impact your views/actions.

### Openness to Change
There will be strategies that make you uncomfortable.

### Decision Making
How you make decisions affects every decision.

### Resistance to Change
Your resistance is why you're in your position.
- Need strategies
- Need support

## 3 Lids Assessment

### 1. Belief (Confidence)
### 2. Operational Effectiveness (Ability to generate revenue)
### 3. Leadership (Ability to develop other people)

**Insight**: Larger the business = lower the score. Greater awareness.

**To change**: Be aware of where you are at.

## Your Need for Change

### If no clear answer
You'll run through life thinking you have the answer.

### To Change:
1. **Confront the beast in the room**
2. **Get honest with yourself**

### Discomfort
Not complying with masses is uncomfortable (example: charity refusing mask)

## Key Questions
- If you don't trust decisions, find someone you can trust
- What is your definition of wealth?
- What are the 3 types of wealth?
- What is biggest threat to wealth?
- What is ideal life?
- What is employee side hustle?
- What are wealth classes?
