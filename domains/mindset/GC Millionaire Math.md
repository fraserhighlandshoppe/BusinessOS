# GC Millionaire Math

## Source
[[File: Millionaire Math Workbok.pdf]]

## The Question
**Would you rather have $1M or 1M friends?**

**Answer**: To get $1M, ask each of 1M friends for $1.

## Wealth Creation Formula
**Wealth created through multiplication and division, NOT addition and subtraction.**

## Conversion Math

### Example 1: Social Media
- Have 1M followers
- Sell 5% of them a $100/month program
- Income = 60M/year

### Example 2: Time Value
When saving money, value decreases due to inflation.
**Need to multiply it.**

**50K/year × 20 years = 1M**
**Double income → 10 years only**

### Time is Money
1M / 365 days / 24h ≈ 114/hr

## Income Multiplication

### Monthly Math
5,000 people × $200/month = 1M/month

### Annual Math
5,000 people × $17/month × 12 months = 1M/year

## The Perspective
5,000 people out of 8B on earth = 0.000000625%

**This is where marketing comes in.**

## Key Insights
1. **Multiply, don't add** - exponential growth
2. **Marketing** reaches massive audience
3. **Time value** - shorten cycle
4. **Conversion rate** - sell to subset of audience
