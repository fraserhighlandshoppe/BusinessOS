# Money - Grant Cardone

## The Money Mindset

### What You Were Told About Money
- "Don't talk about money"
- "Money doesn't grow on trees"
- "Money won't make you happy"

**Everything comes down to money.**

### Misconceptions From Parents
- Stay in your lane
- Don't take too many risks
- Get into middle class and be happy
- Money is evil (root of all evil)

### Mental Block
**Money is the root of all evil** - If true, the Devil would be giving you endless streams.

## What Money Really Is
- **Choices**
- **Freedom**

## Why You've Been Stuck
- Problem with how you think about money
- Learned about money from broke people

## The Big Lie
**You can save your way to a better future.**

**FALSE**: It is impossible to save to a better life.

### Wrong Approaches
- Be thrifty
- Be smart
- Have intention with your money
- Frugal isn't the solution
- Wasteful isn't the solution either
