## Key Insight
It's the same amount of work to become a billionaire as a millionaire.

## The 10 Money Principles

### 1. Never Lose Money
Protect your capital.

### 2. Never Trade Paper for More Paper
Wall Street is built on this.
- Fees
- Commissions

### 3. 40% Rule (60% Live)
- **40% of anything earned** should be invested
  - Take off top, save until invested
- **60%** = living expenses including taxes
- Never increase standard of living until quality of invested assets increases

**Pay the price today** so you can pay any price in future.

### 4. Rent and Lease. Don't Own!
Use leverage, don't tie up capital.

### 5. 95/5 Time Split
- **95%**: Earn money
- **5%**: Budget (expenses)
- You can save (reduce expenses) to 0
- You can increase income to infinity

### 6. Only Use Debt When It Produces Income
**Earned Income**: Buy equipment to make money
- Depreciate to lower income

**Passive Income**: 
- Cost of debt < income from asset

### 7. Spend One Hour a Day Learning

### 8. 3 Investments People Should Make
1. **You** (education, health)
2. **Your Business** (income source - job or business ownership)
3. **Real Assets** (property, equipment, etc.)
