# 10X Growth

## Pillars of Growth

### Challenge
Push you to do more than thought possible.

**If you want to change, you have to change something.**

## Take Action
**Procrastination is same as quitting.**

Do it wrong instead of not doing it!

## Getting Attention

### Process
1. **Get Attention**
2. **Keep Attention**
3. **Multiply Attention** over and over

**Money follows attention. Attention follows money.**

### Speed and Confidence
If goal is big enough, you'll do things you wouldn't normally.
- Speed determines success
- Speed based on confidence
- Push and hustle get you there
- Don't interrupt what got you there

### Mindset
Be in build mode. Push to get ahead.

**Commit first, then figure out rest later.**

**You can be an idiot. But don't be an idiot who procrastinates!**

Put yourself in play. Take action.

## Actions to Take

### Get Out of Comfort Zone
- Attend events:
  - Network Marketing
  - Charity
  - Church (not your own)
  - Conference
  - Convention
  - Chamber of Commerce
  - Fundraiser

## Discipline in Successful People

### Training Others
- Train others to obey rules
- If no rules = no discipline

### Discipline Requires
- Show Up
- Commit
- Rewards (biggest = self esteem)

### Daily Routine
- Wake up
- Eat
- Sleep
- Make sales calls

## Write Offer
**76% of sales orgs don't make an offer.**

To close, don't address past (cost). Reframe to GOAL.
- $18K boot camp cost vs $10M future goal

## Promotion

### Principles
- Frequency
- Consistency
- Repetition

### What Are You Promoting?
- Assets
- Debts
- Expenses

**You promote banks, other businesses.**

### Timing
What is better than perfect time on SM?
- **Every Day**
- **All day**

## Reasons to Promote
1. **Me**
2. **Employees**
3. **Competition**
4. **Customers**
