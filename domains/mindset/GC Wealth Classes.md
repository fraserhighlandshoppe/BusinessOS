# Grant Cardone Wealth Classes

## Key Principle
You will never change your money until you look at it every day.

**Confront your money.**

## Wealth Classes (Income Ranges)

### Wealthy
- **$600K - $860K/year**
- Heavy in assets, low income
- Focus: Use debt to buy assets
- Goal: Passive income

### Upper Middle
- **$130K - $160K**
- Focus: Reduce debt (House/Car/CC)

### Lower Middle
- **$70K - $89K**

### Poverty
- **$19K - $34K**
- Note: Poverty in USA is still top 10% of world

## Strategies by Class
- **Wealthy**: Leverage debt for asset acquisition
- **Upper Middle**: Debt reduction focus
- **Lower Middle**: Income growth focus
- **Poverty**: Basic needs + growth
