# 50 Habits of Successful People

Ref: Leadership Pages, Investing Pages, Business Pages

## Habits 1-20

1. Look for and find opportunities where others see nothing
2. Find lessons while others only see problems
3. Solution focused
4. Consciously create their own success
5. Fearful but not controlled by fear
6. Ask the right questions for positive mindset
7. Rarely complain
8. Don't blame, take complete responsibility
9. Maximize potential, use what they have effectively
10. Busy, productive, proactive
11. Align with like-minded people
12. Ambitious
13. Clarity and certainty about what they want
14. Innovate instead of imitate
15. Don't procrastinate
16. Life-long learners
17. Glass half full, practical, down-to-earth
18. Consistently do what they need to regardless of feelings
19. Take calculated risks
20. Deal with problems quickly and effectively

## Habits 21-40

21. Don't believe in fate, destiny, chance, or luck
22. Take action before they have to
23. More effective at managing emotions
24. Good communicators
25. Have a life plan and work methodically
26. Become exceptional by choice
27. Work through tough stuff others avoid
28. Identified values, live reflectively
29. Have balance - money is a tool/resource
30. Understand discipline and self-control
31. Secure in self-worth
32. Generous and kind
33. Admit mistakes and apologize
34. Adaptable, embrace change
35. Keep themselves in shape physically
36. Work hard, not lazy
37. Resilient
