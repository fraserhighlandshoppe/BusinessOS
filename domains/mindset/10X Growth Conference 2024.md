# 10X Growth Conference 2024

## Key Themes

### Attention
**Best Known wins** - not best product.

### 10X Sales System
1. **Idea**
2. **Attention**
3. **Generate Leads**
4. **Nurture Leads**
5. **Convert Leads**
6. **Manage Sales**
7. **Scale**

### Entrepreneur Management
As entrepreneur, you must manage yourself:
- Manage ideas and actions

## Laws and Principles

### Law of Manifestation
- Nothing happens until action
- IDEA hits resistance

### Management Definition
- To be in charge of company/organization
- Administer or regulate
- Maintain control or influence over activity

## Key Insights

### Tucker Carlson
- **Persistent critic** may teach about yourself
- **Flatterer** lulls into invincibility
- Don't give emotional control to strangers
- When you stop lying, you have nothing to hide = powerful
- People follow strength
- People respond to threats (lack causes panic)

### Advertising Reality
- Can't depend on advertisers - they influence products
- Pharma spends on TV not to sell, but to prevent media criticism
- **Subscriptions** get you away from advertisers

### Words
- Only thing that endures
- First thing power tries to suppress

### John Maxwell
- Maximize life/success through passion
- Need system for personal growth
