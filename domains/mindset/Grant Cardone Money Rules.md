# Grant Cardone Money Rules

## Rules to Get Your Money Right

### Never Spend It Until You Get It
- Until money is in the bank, deal could fall through
- Helps cash flow - don't count money until it's in
- Don't spend tomorrow's money

### The 40% Rule
100% of income becomes:
- **40%**: Tax man
- **40%**: Pay yourself (marketing/advertising - "feed the beast")
- **20%**: Living expenses

**Insight**: If you pay taxman 40%, shouldn't you pay yourself the same?

**Warning**: Not figuring this out shows you aren't disciplined with money.
- If you don't take care of money, money won't take care of you
- If you ignore money, it will ignore you

### If You Can't Write It Off, Don't Buy It
- And converse: if it can be written off, it may be worth buying

### The 47 Rule
If working 40 hours, spend at LEAST 7 hours/week on yourself:
- 1 hour/day self-improvement
  - Read
  - Mentors and coaches

**Don't complain** - there will be a day you can't go to work.

### Always Stay Broke
- Cash is trash
- Worthless until put to work
- Bank puts it to work by lending 9x (fractional reserve)

### Never Lose Money
- Leaving money in bank is losing money (inflation, negative rates)
- Can't compound a fraction of a percent
- Heading toward negative interest rates (already in Europe/Asia)
