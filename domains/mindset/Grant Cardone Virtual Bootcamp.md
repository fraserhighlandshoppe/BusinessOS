## Key Takeaways

### Commitment
**If you can't commit to 3 days, how can you commit to rest of life?**

- Commit, and recommit daily
- Until you commit, nothing great happens
- **Commit first, then figure it out**
- Faster commitment = faster big money

### Daily Practice
- Write goals every morning and night

### Invest in Your People
Avoid scarcity mindset:
- Higher loyalty
- Higher commitment
- Less turnover

### Country Club Rich = Worst Place
Just enough to be good, not far enough for generational wealth:
- Wasting time
- Wasting money
- Wasting potential

### Knowledge
What you know is bigger obstacle than what you don't know.
Thinking you know blocks further learning.

### Time
Need to invest more time to get more time.

### Core Values
- Transparency
- Honesty
- Ownership
- Leadership
- Culture (Growth and Improvement)
- Alignment

### Universe Loves Speed
### Quit Playing Too Small
64% of small businesses break even or lose money.
Most solo-preneurs make less than working for someone.

**Go Big or Go Bigger!**
- Don't think about little problems
- To get rid of little problems, get large problems

### Quit Playing God
Don't try to go it alone.

### Entrepreneur Fallacy
Everyone doesn't think/solve like you.
Want control, so stay small to control things.
