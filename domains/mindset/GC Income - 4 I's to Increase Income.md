# The 4 I's to Increasing Income

Ref: Grant Cardone

## 1. Intelligence
### About Money
- What do you know about money?
- What do you know that is WRONG?
- Need specialist knowledge

### Learning Sources
- Learn from someone making lots of money
- Study Sales, Marketing, Psychology

## 2. Implementation
### Key Elements
- Accountability
- Boost current income (make more doing what you're doing)
- Create multiple streams (even if little drips)

## 3. Investments

### Time Investment
- Need to invest time to get more money
- Start with education - best dividends
- Nobody can take it away
- Right knowledge at right time from right person

### Money Investment
- Then Real Estate

## 4. I can do it (I Believe)
### Better Version: "I will do it"
### 10X Version: "I am doing it"

## Implementation Steps
1. Get intelligence about money and business
2. Implement with accountability
3. Invest time in education (highest ROI)
4. Invest money in real estate
5. Adopt 10X mindset: "I am doing it"
