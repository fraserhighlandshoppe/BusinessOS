## Mindset Shift

### Society's Programming
- Society has programmed us to be stuck
- People talk big game but never take action

**Decision Making**: Every time you have a decision, make it and commit to doing it.

### Your #1 Enemy: Procrastination
Make decisions quickly. Either you'll do it or you won't.

**Instead of**: "What more info do I need?"
**Pivot to**: "How can I do this with confidence?"
