# Grant Cardone Money Myths

## Money Myths Debunked

### Money is Hard
FALSE - Money is easy when you know how.

### Money is Complicated
**The banks designed it to be complicated** to keep you on sidelines.
- Fed
- Money Supply
- Designed to keep you out

**WHO is more important than HOW**

### You Don't Make Money. You Collect It.
Money is collected, not made.

### It Takes Money to Make Money
**FALSE** - It takes:
- Courage
- Activity (lots of action)
- Creativity
- Persistence
- Connection to a person

### Saving is the Way to Wealth
**Myth**: Saving money will never save you.

**Truth**: Take savings and dump into assets.

### All Debt is Bad Debt
**FALSE** - Debt that makes money is GOOD debt!

**Good Debt**:
- Produces more income than cost
- Buy house - not bad debt

### Diversify Your Investments
Not always necessary.

### Only Way to Wealth is Own Business
**FALSE** - You don't need to own business.

**Reality**: 64% of businesses only break even or lose money.
- Owners paid less than employees

## Bank Money Mechanics
When you put money in bank:
- **Asset to you**
- **Liability to bank** (they owe it)

Bank loans money out:
1. Converts to asset (loan)
2. Loan = liability to borrower
3. Used to buy asset
