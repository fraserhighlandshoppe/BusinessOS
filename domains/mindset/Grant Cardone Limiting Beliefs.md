# Grant Cardone Limiting Beliefs

## The Voice in Your Head
Listen for these negative voices:
- "I'm not good enough"
- "I don't deserve success"
- "It's not my fault"

**I've been there.** These thoughts held me back.

## Power of Thought
What you think in your mind has power and determines success or failure.

**If you think you're not worth a damn, you won't be.**

**Even if you make millions** - eventually lose it all because you don't believe in yourself.

**Pro athletes** who fall far often think they don't deserve success - end up where they started.

## Fear of Limiting Beliefs
You'll hear people repeat these beliefs in passing. Most don't realize what they're saying.

People accept beliefs from:
- Parents
- Teachers
- Friends
- Media
- Politicians
- Co-workers

## 25 Worst Limiting Beliefs

1. Patience is a virtue
2. I just want to be comfortable
3. Money won't make you happy
4. It's the journey, not the destination
5. I don't have time
6. I'm living the dream
7. I will do it when I get to it
8. That's impossible
9. Time heals
10. I can't do that
11. It takes money to make money
12. I hate paying taxes
13. Thank God it's Friday
14. I can't wait for the weekend
15. I hate Mondays
16. We can't find good people
17. Debt is bad
18. I need to save more money
19. It's not my fault
20. I will be happy when...
21. I am too old (or too young)
22. It is what it is
23. I don't like to read
24. That's not my job
25. I'm not a salesperson

## Elimination Strategy
**Eliminate these thoughts the moment they creep in.**

They can infect you and bring others down.

**The more you recognize limiting beliefs and switch thinking, the more success you achieve.**
