# GC Wealth Creation Formula

## Ways to Create Financial Wealth
- Inheritance
- Earned Income
- Investments

**Key**: Even with inheritance, you can't remain wealthy without an income.

## Step 1: Create an Income
We all start without income. First step is to create income.

**Then**: Get better at it!

### Fortify Your Income
- Don't complain about your job
- Your job creates income
- Make sure it will be there tomorrow
- Fortify it - become the Go-To person
- Cannot be replaced by technology or new employee

**Note**: People don't like earned income because it's most heavily taxed.

## Step 2: Increase Income
Only care about: "How do I increase this income from my first job?"

**NOT**: Get a second, third, fourth job.

**You must have a target!**

### Increase Your Income By Adding Value
- Don't become satisfied or complacent
- Don't compare to others' situations
- You don't become wealthy from one income flow, especially if it never increases!

**Sales is the way to control your income**:
- Increase the number of friends
- Increase the number of fans
- Increase good reviews

## Step 3: Store (Save) It All
Most people are broke because they never save.

**Save ALL of the increased income from step 2!**

### Living Standards
- Don't buy anything new
- No new debt without net positive return

**Rate ALL purchases 1-5**:
- 1 = necessary
- 3 = maybe
- 5 = not
- 4 and 5 = skipped automatically

**Pay the price now so you can pay any price later!**

**Don't sacrifice family time** just to blow paycheck drinking.

### Expense Breakdown (Live on 60%)
- Housing: 34%
- Transportation: 15%
- Groceries: 13%
- Health Care: 7%
- Insurance: 11%
- Entertainment: 5%
- Utilities: 5%

**Goal**: Save 40% of gross income without increasing outgo.

## Step 4: Invest
**Wrong Advice**: Invest in little pieces, diversify portfolio
**Right Approach**: Make HUGE moves with HUGE investments

### The Two Reasons for Money
1. Buy essentials
2. Invest to create new money

**Your parents taught to save, not invest** - "save to save" doesn't create wealth.

**29% of 55+ don't have retirement savings** - they saved to save, didn't invest to multiply.

**Commitment**: Say "I want to be wealthy!"
