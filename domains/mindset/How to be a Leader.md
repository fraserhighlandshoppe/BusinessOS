# How to be a Leader

## Source
[Cardone University - How to be a successful leader](https://cardoneuniversity.com/how-to-be-a-successful-leader/)

## Key Insight
All successful leaders know how to inspire people and motivate them to take action.

**Methods**:
1. Tell stories or paint pictures with words
2. Through visioning

Successful leaders know the goal and can articulate it clearly and compellingly so followers want to help make it happen.

## How to be a Successful Leader

### 1. Show Up
Nothing happens unless you show up.
- Every day = opportunity
- Most people won't: don't care OR fixed mindset
- Consistent = most important

### 2. Be Authentic
- People trust and respect authenticity
- Share success with everyone
- Value-driven mindset

### 3. No Negativity Allowed
Negativity destroys:
- Happiness
- Career
- Family
- Everything

Leaders don't show negative emotions or spread negativity.

### 4. Work Ethic
Leaders are not lazy.
- Act as role model
- Others watch everything you do and say
- Grind, hustle, get things done
- Without work ethic, can't lead team or get results

### 5. Provide Feedback
- Give and receive feedback
- Not scared of confrontation
- Separate emotions from truth
- Make feedback a habit
- Learn from successes and failures

### 6. Be Someone Worth Following
- Creates opportunities for others
- Plays by rules but bends when necessary
- Have qualities people want to follow
- Team looks up to and respects

### 7. Be A Mentor
- Take time to help others grow
- Part of great leadership = mentoring next generation
- Takes time and patience
- Very rewarding
