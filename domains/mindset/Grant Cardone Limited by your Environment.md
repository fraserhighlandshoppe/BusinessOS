# Are You Limited by Your Surroundings?

## Business Reality
Your business will be held down by the level of motivation, excitement, and engagement around you.

**You cannot grow if they don't want to grow.** — Grant Cardone

## The Challenge

### Employee Engagement
- What does it take to get employees really engaged?

### Leadership Engagement
- What does it take to get managers and owners engaged at the next level up?

### Motivation Questions
- How do I get motivated by success?
- How do I get motivated to leave the comfort of small success?

### Parental Influence
People exceed what their parents do.
- Parents tell them how proud they are of that success
- Person gets stuck

## You Get What You Look For

**Mindset Principle**: Look for what you want, you'll find it.

### Focus Determines Reality
- If you're looking for purple elephants, you'll find purple elephants
- Look for deals, mentors, people to network with

**Your mind seeks what you focus on.**

### The Teacher Appears When Student is Ready
- Teacher has always been there
- You just weren't ready to recognize them

## Key Insights
1. **Environment matters** - Surround yourself with growth-minded people
2. **Focus creates reality** - Look for what you want
3. **Parents create ceiling** - Exceed their level, don't stay stuck in their pride
4. **Growth requires departure** - Leave comfort zone
