# 10X Challenge - Mindset: Big Goals, Big Think

## Ref: Grant Cardone Pages

## Why People Fail at Their Goals

### Common Reasons
1. Goal not significant enough
2. Focused on mechanics of goal, not the goal itself
3. Surrounded by goal quitters
4. Not giving up something significant

### Exercises
1. **List an unachievable goal** that will make you so excited you will run through walls!
2. **List 5 people** (employees, family, partners) that can free you from the mechanics
3. **Identify quitters** in your life who have given up on their own goals
4. **What are you willing to give up** to reach your goals? Why?

## Mistakes When Setting Goals

1. **Goal Too Small**
2. **Goal is Not Exciting**
3. **Adopting others' ideas**
4. **Confusing goals with strategies**
5. **Letting others influence your goals**
6. **Confusing targets with goals**

**Key Insight**: You will quit on any goal that doesn't significantly change your life.

### Exercises
1. Which of these mistakes do you make?
2. How can you correct these mistakes?

## Discipline

**Definition**: Practice of training to obey rules and code of behavior using punishment to correct disobedience.

### Build Discipline With:
- Daily Rituals
- Culture
- Accountability Coach/Mentor

### Discipline Requires:
- Commitment
- Accountability
- Showing up when you want to
- Showing up when you don't want to
- Saying yes more than no
- No excuses
- Possibility of rewards and punishments

### Byproducts of Discipline
- Personal Confidence
- Respect of self
- Respect by others
- Leadership
- More responsibility
- Makes fortitude possible

### Discipline Challenge
- Write down goals every AM and PM
- Workout daily
- Make a hard call every day
- Training/Education daily

**Questions**:
1. Which daily rituals can you commit to?
2. What will get in your way?
3. How many days in a row can you do these before missing?
