# Grant Cardone Marketing and Money Metrics

## Source
- [[File: GC Marketing and Money Metrics Workbook.pdf]]

## Key Insight
As marketing becomes more involved, metrics become more advanced.

**You don't need ads to get to $500K revenue.**

**Numbers don't lie.** They don't have opinions.

## Most Important Marketing Asset
**Your email list** - because you own it.

## Core Metrics

### Database Metrics
1. **Number of Contacts** in database
2. **Growth** of contacts
3. **Open Rate**
4. **Click Through Rate (CTR)**

### Sales Conversion Metrics
5. **Contact Rate** - How many times to get sales call
6. **Appointment Rate** - How many to set appointment
7. **Demo Show Rate** - Appointments that show up
8. **Close Ratio** - How many presentations to make sale

### Financial Metrics
9. **Average Order Value**
10. **Lifetime Value of Customer** - Affects acquisition spend
11. **Cost per Lead**

## Growth Drivers

### Database Growth
If DB isn't growing, content value is likely low.

### Frequency of Outreach
If nobody has seen content, they won't open emails.

## Email Optimization

### Subject Line
- Impacts open rate
- Must address subject line properly
- Avoid break and trust, unsubscribe

### Content
- Affects CTR
- Include opportunities to link click multiple times
