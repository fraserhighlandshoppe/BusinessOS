# Grant Cardone Marketing Truths

## Core Truths

### 1. Marketing is Senior to Sales
Marketing leads, sales follows.

### 2. Number of Leads Determine Sales
More leads = more sales. It's math.

### 3. Top of Funnel Determines Everything Else
Fill the funnel first, then work down.

### 4. Money Follows Attention
Get attention, money comes.

### 5. Best Known Beats Best Product
Brand recognition > product quality in many cases.

### 6. All Marketing Done in Right Quantities Works
Quantity matters more than perfection.

### 7. Landing Pages Beat Websites
One website, multiple landing pages.

### 8. Marketing Doesn't Cost Money
Marketing is investment, not expense.

## Application
- Focus on top of funnel
- Quantity over perfection
- Multiple landing pages
- Attention = money
