# Grant Cardone Marketing

Marketing is all about getting attention. Sales is about turning that attention into revenue. Sales and marketing must be senior to everything else in your business.

## 5 Ways to Market When Social Media is Down

### 1. Blogging
The simplest alternative when social media isn't working.
- Blog about product/service features
- Describe what sets you apart from competitors
- Keep it fun, educational
- Remind people why they should do business with you

### 2. Emails
Simple yet effective way to market and retain customers.
- Send email about social media issue and expected resolution
- Include blog or website address
- Encourage questions via phone or reply

### 3. Offline Marketing
Traditional strategies when technology fails.
- Hand out flyers
- Have a stand on property (brick-and-mortar)
- Old-fashioned marketing strategies work

### 4. Calls
Make phone calls to clients and customers.
- Cold-calling is last resort but practical when tech fails
- Prepare and polish sales pitch
- Never miss a sale

### 5. Referrals
Individual marketing strategy using existing relationships.
- Contact current and past clients
- Ask for referrals
- Mention social media was down when they were looking

## Key Principle
**Don't panic!** Keep calm, have plan of action for each scenario, implement strategies that suit your needs.
