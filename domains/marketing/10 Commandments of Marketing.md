# 10 Commandments of Marketing

## Source
- [[File: GC 10 Commandments of Marketing workbook.pdf]]

## The 10 Commandments

### 1. Money Follows Attention
**If they don't know you, they can't flow you.**
**If they've forgotten you, they can't flow you.**
**Once you gain a bucket of money, you get more attention.**

### 2. Best Known Beats Best Product
Recognition > Quality in many cases.

### 3. Assume No One Saw Your Ad
Even people who clicked and read it.

**Repeated messaging** works better than one time.

### 4. Speed is More Important Than Quality
Move fast, iterate.

### 5. Frequency Comes Before Greatness
Volume before perfection.

### 6. Attention is the Most Valuable Currency
More valuable than cash ROI on marketing spend.

### 7. Marketing is Senior to Sales
Marketing leads, sales follows.

### 8. Repeat Successful Actions
**Double down** on what works.

### 9. No One Thing Will Blow a Deal
It's about consistency, not single moment.

### 10. Become Omni-present
**Quit thinking about introvert vs extrovert.** Doesn't matter.

**Get where your audience is.**
