# Ten Dollar A Day Traffic Plan

## The 6/3/1 Ratio
- **$6**: Cold traffic (introducing new customers)
- **$3**: Warm traffic (people who showed interest)
- **$1**: Hot traffic (existing buyers)

## Cold Traffic Goals and Offers

### Purpose
- Continuously introduce new customers
- Without them, business cannot grow
- Making people aware of you
- Build trust and authority

**Don't** take product/offer straight to cold audience - like asking someone to marry you on first meeting.

### Introduction/Indoctrination
1. **Intro Brand** - Establish credibility
2. **Pixelling** - For running ads
3. **Segmentation** - Fine-tune offers for relevance

### Once Audience is Known
- Blog posts
- Social media updates
- Podcasts
- Content videos
- Lead magnets
- Quizzes and surveys
- Whitepapers

## Warm Traffic Goals and Offers

### Audience
People who have shown some interest:
- Facebook fans, Twitter followers
- YouTube subscribers, website visitors
- Email subscribers

### Stage
Evaluation stage - interested but not ready to purchase.

### Offers
- Lead magnets (exchange for email/contact info)
- Quiz or survey (opt-in required for results)
- Free or paid webinar
- Flash sales (low dollar offer $7-10)
- Product demo
- Branding videos
- Books
- Free trial

## Hot Traffic Goals and Offers

### Audience
Your existing buyers.

### Goals
- **Activation**: Remind customers who haven't purchased recently that you still exist
- **High Dollar Sales**: Increase customer value for repeat purchases or reactivation

### Retargeting
- Retargeting ads
- Email marketing

### Offers
- Events
- Paid webinar
- High dollar offers
- Done-for-you services

## Cold Traffic Campaigns

### Campaign 1: Visitors to Content
**Goal**: Let target get to KNOW you
- Cheap
- Establishes authority
- Building assets

**Success Metric**: Cost per pixel

**Facebook Objective**: Clicks to website
- Bid 6c higher than suggested bid
- High CTR decreases cost per click

**Targeting**: Interest targeting between 500,000 and 1M

## Interest Targeting
Be very specific - put right message in front of right people.

**"But no one else would" trick**: Be your target market, or interview someone who is.

## Research Questions
1. Who are the authorities, big brands, thought leaders in your niche?
2. What books, magazines, newspapers do they read?
3. What events do they attend?
4. What websites do they frequent?
5. Where do they live?
6. What tools do they use?
7. What is uniquely about your target market?

## Ad Copy and Creative
- Image in ad must match landing page
- Person knows they're exactly where they should be
