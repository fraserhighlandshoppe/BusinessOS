# Affiliate Marketing

Source: Eben Pagan, Jeff Johnson

## Key Principles

### No List, No Product
- You need an email list and products to sell
- Will have refunds - that's a fact of online business

## Circle of Money
Sell your affiliate products AND your own products simultaneously.

## Strategy
- Build relationship through affiliate marketing first
- Then sell your own products
- Use affiliate products to build trust and authority
