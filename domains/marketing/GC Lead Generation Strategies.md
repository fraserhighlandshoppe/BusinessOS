## Create Content Daily

### Social Media Audiences
People on social media for:
- Entertainment
- Education
- Information

**Talk about problems you solve**:
- Your products/services
- Industry-related problems
- Problems your ideal customers experience
- **You must actually help people** to get response

### Social Media Platforms
**Facebook/Instagram**: Shows posts to few friends, even less new people

**Best for New Audiences**:
- TikTok - designed to show content to new people
- Instagram Reels
- YouTube Shorts

**Content must be relevant and valuable** to ideal customer.

## Build an Email List
People exchange information (email) for your information.

**Start**: Newsletter / blog / daily email

## Podcast
Start podcast or appear on others'.

**Benefits**:
- Connect with people
- They might become customers

## Outbound Social Media Reach
Find communities/people online representing ideal customer.

**Actions**:
- Join communities
- Interact
  - Leave comments
  - Send DMs

## Free Webinars
Invite people to free training.

**Steps**:
1. Get Zoom account
2. Pick a time
3. Tell people about it
4. Run it
