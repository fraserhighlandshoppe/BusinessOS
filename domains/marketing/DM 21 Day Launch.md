# DM 21 Day Launch - The 3X Method

## The Normal Business Steps
1. Generate Traffic
2. Traffic → Leads
3. Leads → Customers
4. Customers → Money
5. Reinvest Money → Generate Traffic (Grow Business)

**The Trick**: Get more from what you have - more leads become customers, more money from customers.

## The 3X Method

### Product Splintering
Most products have smaller chunks. Sell splinters separately - 10X more likely to buy main product.

**Strategy**: Each month, show a different splinter to customers.

### Conversion Hacks for List Burnout
- Bundle $47 product with bonus report, sell for $7
- People who buy bonus are 10X more likely to buy main product
- Based on commitment and consistency

### Tripwire
Low-end offer at break-even, goal: generate customer while covering marketing cost.

Use splinter products from core offer - key to perpetual traffic.

### Two Step Opt-In
Lowers shields - micro-commitment (clicking button) makes opt-in more likely.

### Lead with Specific Proof

### Lead Magnet Page
Short page with 2nd step opt-in. Download button triggers pop-up with name/email.

## Perpetual Traffic Formula

**Target Ratio**: 40/10/10 (Opt-in/Tripwire/Core)

### Example Calculation
- $500 budget → $0.50/click → 1000 clicks
- Lead Magnet with 40% opt-in rate = 400 leads
- Tripwire at 10% conversion = 40 purchases ($280)
- Core offer at 10% conversion = 4 purchases ($200)
- Total: $480 (break even on traffic, plus profit)

**DM typically sees >20% conversion on core offer.**

### The Funnel Flow
1. $0.50/click → Lead Magnet (captures email)
2. Send to Tripwire ($7 offer)
3. Send buyers to Core Offer ($50)
4. Send core buyers to Profit Maximizer (upsell)

## Profit Maximizer
Focus on desired end result of buyer: "Want some help..."

**Why they bought**: Address their need for faster results with less work.

**Common UPS**: Double average customer value by adding Profit Maximizer.

### Profit Maximizer Types
- **Physical**: Expedited delivery, assembly, advanced training
- **Info**: Done for you services, software, consulting
- **Software**: Installation, migration, higher support
- **Consultants**: More hands-on experiences

## Followup Strategy
Send "Are you still" / "Have you yet" followup campaign to non-buyers.

**Psychology**: No one likes to feel stuck/trapped. Avoid language that makes them feel trapped.

## Inverted Funnel
Begin with the end in mind - plan customer journey to Core Offer.
