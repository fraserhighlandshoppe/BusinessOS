## Marketing is the Holy Grail of Business

**Definition**: Activity of promoting and selling goods and services.

## Key Insights

### Wealthy People Focus
Wealthy people don't talk about making the sale.
**They talk about getting product onto every shelf.**

### Marketing vs Sales
Marketing and promotion can make you look bigger.

**Truth**: As long as you're great at marketing you can suck at sales and still make money.

## The Top of Funnel Strategy
**Commit to the top of the funnel.**
**Build it very wide.**
