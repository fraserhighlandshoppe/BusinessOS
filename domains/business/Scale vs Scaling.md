# Scale vs Scaling

## Definitions

### Scale
Maximizing what you have.

### Scaling
Rapid expansion and growth.

## Common Mistake
Most people skip scale and go straight to scaling, missing optimization.

## Elements of Growing and Scaling

### 1. Strategy
Plan for growth.

### 2. Market
Understand target market.

### 3. Sell
Sales process and execution.

### 4. Add People
Build team.

### 5. Understand Finances
Financial literacy.

### 6. Leverage through Operations
Systematize processes.

### 7. Leadership
Lead vision and team.

### 8. Data Tracking
Measure and optimize.

### 9. Tech
- **How**: Technology applications
- **When**: Timing of implementation

### 10. Investment
Capital for growth.

## Key Insight
Optimize (scale) before expanding (scale).
