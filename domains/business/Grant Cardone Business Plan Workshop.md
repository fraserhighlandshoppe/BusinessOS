# Grant Cardone Business Plan Workshop

## Multiple Income Flows Required

## Target: Business Revenue Goals
- **Year 1**: $50K
- **Year 3**: $100K
- **Year 5**: $200K
- **Year 3 Target**: If not $1M, you don't know capability!

**Never lower targets. Increase actions.**

## Assessment: Produce More Results

### Spectator vs Player
Take inventory of your business.

### Business is About Speed
- Money loves speed
- Speed requires confidence

## Motivation
What is your motivation?
- Take care of family
- Free time

## Obstacles
What prevents accomplishing goals?
- Business connections
- Free cash

## Resources
What are your resources?
- Phone with contacts
- Car
- Internet
- Friends
- Family
- Computer
- Skills and Knowledge
- Credit (Good debt)

## Execution
What must you do to get where you want?
- What will you do?
- How early get up?
- How late stay up?
- **If payoff big enough, you WILL execute**

## Comfort Zone Danger
When comfortable, at most risk:
- Start deluding yourself
- Make excuses

**Hungry people have motivation/drive.**
**Bigger commitment = bigger payoff.**

## Model: Keep it Super Simple

### 1. Leads
**Hard Way**:
- Cold calling
- Cold email
- Standing in street spinning sign

**Shortcut**:
- Online funnels (automated)
  - Qualified
  - Interested
  - Have some money

### 2. Close Deal (Transaction)
**Hard Way**: Chasing deal (often wrong person)

**Shortcut**: Have right person come to you
- Sales video

### 3. Deliver
**Hard Way**: Do it yourself

**Shortcut**: Over deliver for repeat business
