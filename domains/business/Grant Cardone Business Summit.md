# Grant Cardone Business Summit

Grant re-iterated his [[Grant Cardone Money Tips]]

## How to Grow Your Business
1. **Mindset** (Vision)
2. **Wealth**
3. **Marketing**
4. **Sales**
5. **Scale**

## Time vs Money
Your goal should be to maximize your time on earth.
- Sometimes that means money
- But ultimately: TIME

## Goal Setting Strategy
1. Set your goal
2. Tell everyone you meet you're going to do X
   - Even if you don't know how
   - Belief attracts belief

## Business Fundamentals
### You Must Have:
- **An Idea** - with confidence
- **Monetize ASAP** - as fast as possible
- **Multiple Products/Services** - impossible to have too many

### Founding Team Core (from Adam Cheyer - Siri)
1. **Visionary**
2. **Marketing** - Communicate the vision
3. **Product**
4. **Builder** - Hits timelines on budget

### Additional Needs
- **Financial**
- **Operations**
- **Legal**

## Scaling
Think about scale from the start.
- Not every business can scale
- If not considered early, difficult later

## Key Insights
- Right idea at right time
- Work with passionate people
