## Generating Leads

**Attention gets mindshare. Leads get customers.**

**Leads = Results of offer.**

### Offer Types
- **Straight to product offer** (Lowest conversion - pizza place with sign guy)
- **Offer of additional information**
- **Newsletter** (Value-based info)
- **Free Webinar** (Adding value)
- **Ebooks**
- **Free Consult** (Strategy call, Assessment, Estimate)

### 3 Ways People Take Up Offer
1. **DIY** (Do It Yourself)
2. **DI With You (DWY)**
3. **Done For You (DFY)**

People move from DIY → DWY → DFY

## Offer Checklist
- Higher value perceived than cost (time, info, ...)
- Low cost or free (low barrier)
- Low friction (easy)
