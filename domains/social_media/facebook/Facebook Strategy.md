# Facebook Content Strategy

Ported from wiki `FHS Social Media Strategy` (2026).

## Core Principles

1. **People deal with people they know and trust** — Be human, share stories beyond products
2. **Algorithm favors engagement** — FB shows <10% of purely promotional posts
3. **70/30 Rule** — 70% value-added content, 30% promotional
4. **Visual-first** — Photos and videos drive engagement
5. **Local focus** — Highlight Calgary community, games, teachers

## Content Pillars

### 1. Community & Events
- Highland Games coverage
- Local band/dance recaps
- Teacher spotlights
- Customer stories
- Community photos

### 2. Educational
- Piping tips
- Drum tuning guides
- Dance technique videos
- Kilt care instructions
- Gear reviews

### 3. Behind the Scenes
- Store life
- Owner perspectives (Pipe Major Kevin)
- Product preparation
- Shipping/unboxing

### 4. Promotional
- New products
- Sales and deals
- Clearance items
- Flash sales

## Post Types & Frequency

| Type | Frequency | Goal |
|---|---|---|
| Product showcase | 2x/week | Sales |
| Educational post | 1x/week | Authority |
| Community photo | 2x/week | Engagement |
| Event announcement | As needed | Attendance |
| Customer testimonial | 1x/week | Trust |
| Behind-the-scenes | 1x/week | Humanity |

## Facebook Marketplace Integration

### Listing Strategy
- Dead stock: Markdown 25-50% off retail
- Local pickup only
- Include "serious inquiries welcome" in description
- Use high-quality lifestyle photos

### Marketplace Posts
- "We're selling our extra inventory!" posts
- Link to Marketplace listings
- Customer pickup coordination

## Engagement Guidelines

### Response Time
- Comments: Within 2 hours during business hours
- Messages: Within 1 business day
- Weekend posts: Check Monday morning

### Tone
- Friendly but knowledgeable
- Use piping/drumming/dance terminology appropriately
- Avoid overly promotional language
- Include questions to encourage comments

## Metrics to Track

- Reach and impressions
- Engagement rate (likes/comments/shares)
- Click-through rate to website
- Marketplace inquiries converted to sales
- Follower growth rate
