# LinkedIn Company Page Guide

Source: [Entrepreneur Article](https://www.entrepreneur.com/article/226637)

## 10 Questions to Ask When Creating Your LinkedIn Company Page

### 1. Who should set up and maintain my company page?
- Assign to someone on your team who is an active LinkedIn member
- Preferably with digital marketing knowledge and experience
- At larger companies, marketing department handles it

### 2. How should I describe my company?
- Brief explanatory About section
- Tell potential customers and employees what you want them to know
- Describe exactly what your company does and mission
- Include headquarters location, website, industry, status, founding date, employees

### 3. What images should I use?
- Attention-grabbing banner-style horizontal image
- Reflects your brand
- Can highlight latest news and events
- Feature keynote speakers with event details

### 4. Should I include videos, too?
- Bring your brand to life
- Customer testimonials
- Product demonstrations
- CEO speaking at webinars/conferences
- Behind-the-scenes office culture
- Keep videos short and to the point

### 5. What should I list in the page's products and services section?
- Meet the needs of your target market
- Describe what each helps people accomplish
- Carefully choose which product/service to feature first

### 6. How often should I post status updates and what should they be about?
- No more than 3-5 times per day
- Company events, achievements, product releases
- Highlight "human, less hard sell side"
- Links to blog posts, memes, videos
- Encourage two-way conversation through comments

### 7. How can I encourage LinkedIn members to recommend my products and services?
- Often, all you have to do is ask
- Create customer survey for recommendations
- Remind satisfied customers on website/blog with link to LinkedIn page
- Recommendations lend credibility and trustworthiness

### 8. How can I promote my page?
- Add LinkedIn "follow" button to your website
- Put company page URL on business cards, email signatures, newsletters
- Ask employees to like and share status updates
- Use LinkedIn CPC and CPM ad campaigns

### 9. How can I track the effectiveness of my page?
- LinkedIn provides analytics similar to Google Analytics
- See who visits, what content they click on, like, share
- Statistics about follower community growth
- Access to information about attracted LinkedIn members

### 10. How can I directly recruit talent from my page?
- Millions visit LinkedIn daily for employment listings
- Create premium LinkedIn career page connected to company page
- Shows current job openings and employee testimonials
- Job seekers can apply and contact recruiters

## Common LinkedIn Profile Mistakes

### Mistake 1: Unprofessional/Awkward Profile Picture
- Avoid labradoodles, kids photos, gaming avatars, superheroes
- No profile picture at all (appears sketchy)
- **Solution**: Post high-resolution headshot (200x200 to 500x500 pixels)
