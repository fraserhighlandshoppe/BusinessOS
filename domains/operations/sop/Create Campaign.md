# Create Campaign

Standard operating procedure for creating marketing campaigns.

## Purpose

Ensure consistent, high-converting marketing campaigns following proven formulas.

## Campaign Types

| Type | Goal | Typical Duration |
|---|---|---|
| Lead Generation | Build email list | 30-60 days |
| Product Launch | Sell new product | 14-30 days |
| Promotional | Drive immediate sales | 7-14 days |
| Retargeting | Re-engage warm leads | Ongoing |

## The Mike Filsaime Formula

1. **Free Offer** - Shipping/handling included ($50 value)
2. **Continuity** - Recurring income ($29.99/month)

## Campaign Structure

### 1. Planning Phase

- Define campaign objective
- Identify target audience
- Research competitor campaigns
- Set budget and timeline

### 2. Offer Development

- Core offer creation
- Bonuses and guarantees
- Pricing strategy
- Scarcity/timing elements

### 3. Content Creation

- Headline (use templates)
- Body copy (benefits-focused)
- Visuals/creative
- Call to action

### 4. Funnel Setup

- Landing page
- Opt-in form
- Thank you page
- Email sequence

### 5. Testing & Launch

- A/B test headlines
- Test different offers
- Monitor metrics
- Optimize based on data

## Headline Templates

1. **How To [Accomplish Something] In [Time]**
2. **Secrets of [Success]**
3. **Give Me [Time] Minutes**
4. **[Number] Ways To [Achieve Goal]**

## Copywriting Process

1. Start with audience transformation
2. List features → benefits → outcomes
3. Create fact sheet
4. Write objections sheet
5. Craft offer with bonuses
6. Build order form

## Launch Sequence

### Pre-Launch (3 days)
- Teaser posts
- Email sequence to warm list
- Social media countdown

### Launch Day
- Main announcement
- Email blast
- Social media push
- Direct outreach

### Post-Launch (7 days)
- Follow-up emails
- Social proof posts
- Limited-time extensions

## Metrics to Track

- Conversion rate
- Cost per acquisition
- Return on ad spend
- Email open/click rates
- Revenue generated

## Tools

- Landing page builder (WordPress, Leadpages)
- Email marketing (Mautic, Mailchimp)
- Analytics (Google Analytics)
- Social scheduling (Hootsuite)
- Design (Canva, Photoshop)
