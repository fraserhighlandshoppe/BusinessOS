# Product Photography SOP

Standard operating procedure for product photography.

## Equipment Required

| Item | Specification |
|---|---|
| Camera | DSLR or mirrorless (Canon EOS M50, Sony A6000) |
| Lens | 50mm prime or kit lens |
| Tripod | Sturdy, adjustable height |
| Lighting | Softbox lights (2) or natural window light |
| Background | White seamless paper or lightbox |
| Reflector | 5-in-1 collapsible |
| Scale reference | Ruler or coin |

## Setup

### Studio Setup
1. Clear workspace, good natural light or 2 softbox lights at 45° angles
2. Mount white background - seamless paper or white sheet
3. Position lights to eliminate shadows
4. Set camera on tripod at 45° angle to product
5. Use remote shutter or timer to avoid camera shake

### Settings
- Aperture: f/8 (good depth of field)
- ISO: 100-200 (lowest for clean image)
- Shutter speed: 1/125 or higher
- White balance: Daylight or 5500K
- Focus: Manual focus on product center

## Photo Types & Requirements

### Main Product Shot
- White background
- Product filling 70% of frame
- Top-down or 45° angle
- Sharp focus on main feature
- No shadows or glare

### Lifestyle Shot
- Context use (product in action)
- Human element (hands, feet)
- Authentic environment
- Shows scale and purpose

### Detail Shot
- Close-up of unique features
- Text, patterns, mechanisms
- Clear and sharp
- White or neutral background

### Size Reference
- Include ruler, coin, or hand
- Clearly labeled in filename
- Shows actual dimensions

## Shooting Process

### 1. Preparation
- Clean product thoroughly
- Remove tags, labels (unless product feature)
- Arrange in best position
- Check for defects

### 2. Shooting
- Take 5-10 shots per angle
- Shoot from multiple angles (front, side, back, top)
- Vary composition slightly
- Check focus and exposure between shots

### 3. Review
- Check for:
  - Blur/focus issues
  - Shadows/artifacts
  - Color accuracy
  - Completeness

## Post-Processing

### Basic Adjustments
1. Crop to remove excess space
2. Straighten horizon/edges
3. Adjust exposure if needed (+/- 0.3 stops max)
4. Correct white balance
5. Sharpen slightly (unsharp mask: 50%, 1.0px)

### Retouching
- Remove dust spots
- Fix minor blemishes
- Do NOT alter product appearance
- Keep colors true to life

### Export Settings
- Format: JPEG
- Quality: 85-90%
- Resolution: 1500px on longest side
- Filename: `SKU_main.jpg`, `SKU_lifestyle1.jpg`


## Upload to FA

1. Log into FrontAccounting media manager
2. Upload files to product record
3. Set main image as primary
4. Add alt text: "Brand Model - Description"
5. Update inventory record with image URLs

- [ ] At least 3 angles shown
- [ ] Size reference included

## Common Issues & Solutions

| Issue | Cause | Solution |
|---|---|---|
| Shadows | Light too close | Move lights further back |
| Glare | Reflective surfaces | Use polarizing filter or diffusers |
| Blur | Camera shake | Use tripod and remote shutter |
| Dark images | Low light | Increase ISO or add lighting |
| Color cast | Mixed lighting | Use consistent light source |

## Monthly Maintenance

- Clean camera lens and sensors
- Check light bulbs/batteries
- Update editing presets
- Review and update this SOP
