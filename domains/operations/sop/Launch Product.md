# Launch Product

Standard operating procedure for product launches.

## Purpose

Ensure successful product launches using proven marketing systems.

## The 3X Method

### 3 Days Pre-Launch
1. **Day 1** - Announcement + early access
2. **Day 2** - Testimonial/social proof
3. **Day 3** - Final reminder + scarcity

### 40/10/10 Ratios
- 40% of time: Build anticipation
- 10% of time: Launch day execution  
- 10% of time: Post-launch follow-up

## Pre-Launch Phase (7-14 days)

### Week Before
- [ ] Finalize product
- [ ] Create sales page
- [ ] Set up email sequence
- [ ] Prepare bonuses

### 3 Days Before
- [ ] Send pre-launch email to list
- [ ] Post teaser on social media
- [ ] Create urgency countdown
- [ ] Line up affiliates/partners

### Day Before
- [ ] Final product check
- [ ] Test all links
- [ ] Prepare launch assets
- [ ] Brief team on roles

## Launch Day

### Morning (9am-12pm)
1. Send launch email to list
2. Post on all social channels
3. Begin direct outreach
4. Monitor metrics

### Afternoon (1pm-5pm)
1. Share testimonials
2. Post behind-the-scenes
3. Respond to questions
4. Adjust based on data

### Evening (6pm-9pm)
1. Final push post
2. Thank early buyers
3. Prepare day 2 content
4. Plan follow-up sequence

## Post-Launch (7 days)

### Day 2-3
- Send follow-up emails
- Share social proof
- Address objections

### Day 4-5
- Extend deadline (scarcity)
- Add new bonus
- Push to warm leads

### Day 6-7
- Final reminder
- Last chance messaging
- Prepare for next phase

## Email Sequence

### Email 1 - Launch Announcement
Subject: "It's Here! [Product Name] is Finally Available"

### Email 2 - Social Proof (24 hours later)
Subject: "Join [X] Happy Customers Already Using [Product]"

### Email 3 - Scarcity (48 hours later)
Subject: "Only 24 Hours Left at This Price"

### Email 4 - Final Call (72 hours later)
Subject: "Last Chance: Offer Ends Tonight"

## Pricing Strategy

- **Premium**: Higher price = higher perception of value
- **Bundle**: Package with bonuses for more value
- **Limited-time**: Create urgency with deadlines

## Bonuses

- Include 2-3 high-value bonuses
- Bonuses should solve related problems
- Set clear bonus expiration (same as main offer)

## Objections Handling

Common objections and responses:

1. "It's too expensive"
   - "Compare the value to [alternative]"
   - "Payment plans available"

2. "I don't have time"
   - "Quick implementation guide included"
   - "Done-for-you options"

3. "I'm not sure it will work"
   - "30-day money-back guarantee"
   - "Success stories from similar users"

## Success Metrics

- Conversion rate (target: 2-5%)
- Revenue in first 24 hours
- Email open/click rates
- Social media engagement
- Customer feedback
