# Schedule Social Post

Standard operating procedure for creating and scheduling social media posts.

## Purpose

Ensure consistent, high-quality social media content that follows the 4-part formula and 80/20 rule.

## The 4-Part Post Formula

1. **Hook** - Captivating first sentence
2. **Before and After Story** - Life before vs. after (don't mention product name)
3. **Take Away** - Non-salesy insight, removes resistance
4. **Call To Action** - Ask for private message (never link to website)

## 80/20 Rule

- 80% Value Content
- 20% Promotional Content

## Content Types

| Type | Description | Best For |
|---|---|---|
| Pictures | Selfies, Lifestyle, Fun | Building connection |
| Questions | Fill in the Blank prompts | Drive engagement |
| Quotes | Positive and Inspirational | Motivation |
| Resources | Blog posts, videos | Education |
| Videos | Native Facebook videos | Highest engagement |

## Posting Schedule

### Frequency
- 3-5 posts per day

### Optimal Times (Facebook)
- 0700-0900
- 1300-1600
- 1700-2100

Find your sweet spots by checking insight stats.

## Workflow

1. **Plan** - Review content calendar
2. **Create** - Write using 4-part formula
3. **Design** - Add appropriate visuals
4. **Schedule** - Use Hootsuite or native scheduler
5. **Engage** - Respond to comments within 1 hour

## Content Planning

### Questions to Drive Engagement
- "Fill in the Blank: If I had all the time and money in the world, I would _________."
- "What's the biggest challenge you face with [topic]?"
- "What would you do if you knew you couldn't fail?"

### Story Ideas
- Customer success stories (before/after)
- Team/founder stories
- Industry insights
- Behind-the-scenes content

## Checklist

- [ ] Hook is captivating
- [ ] Story doesn't mention company/product name
- [ ] Take Away removes resistance
- [ ] CTA asks for private message
- [ ] Visual is high quality
- [ ] Posted at optimal time
- [ ] Follows 80/20 rule

## Tools

- Hootsuite Publisher
- Canva for graphics
- Facebook Creator Studio
- Instagram Creator Studio

## Metrics to Track

- Engagement rate
- Reach/impressions
- Click-through rate (for link posts)
- Comments and shares
