# FHS Partnership Agreement

## Effective Date
February 2015

## Partners
- Kevin Fraser
- Kimberly Henry

## Partnership Details

### Trade Name
Fraser Highland Shoppe

### Place of Business
Airdrie, Alberta

### Nature of Business
Operations of a Scottish shop selling:
- Giftware
- Highland Dance supplies
- Parts and instruments for Pipers and Drummers

**Legal Structure**: General partnership - all partners jointly and severally liable for debts and obligations.

## Day-to-Day Operations

### Partner Responsibilities
1. Partners provide full-time services and best efforts
2. Each partner has equal rights to manage and control
3. Differences resolved by unanimous vote
4. One partner may be elected for day-to-day business
5. No partner can bind partnership for liabilities > $250 without written consent

### Meetings
- Minimum quarterly management meetings
- Quorum: 66% of shares

### Division of Labor
- **Kevin**: Product expert for Piping and Drumming
- **Kimberly**: Product expert for Highland Dance and Country Dance
- **Shared decisions**: Capital stock acquisition, advertising spend, pricing

## Capital Contribution

### Structure
- Separate capital account for each partner
- No withdrawals from capital accounts
- Accounts maintained proportional to ownership share

### Valuation
- Services and property assigned cash value
- Ownership percentages based on valuation

### After Year 2
- Additional capital required from all partners proportionally
- Ownership adjusted accordingly if partner cannot contribute

## Withdrawal of Capital
- No partner withdraws without approval of other partners

## Profit and Losses
- Shared according to ownership percentages
- Accountant closes books yearly
- Profit/loss applied to Capital Accounts

## Compensation
- Partners not paid salary
- Services valued in cash for Capital Contribution accounting

## Financial Management

### Signing Privileges
- Credit lines?
- Purchase limits without partner agreement

### Financial Statements
- Quarterly financial statements
- End of Month processes
- Annual report within practical time after year-end
- Fiscal year = Calendar year

### Partnership Funds
- Held in designated investments/banking accounts
- Funds in partnership name only
- No commingling

## Books of Account
- All partners have open access

## Banking
- Income deposited per CFO designation
- Expenses paid per CFO designation
