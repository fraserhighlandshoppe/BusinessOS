# FHS Referral Program

## Overview
Customer referral and affiliate program to increase revenue and expand client base.

## Goals

### Business Goals
- X% Increase in revenue
- X% Number of new clients
- Cross-sell and up-sell to existing clients

### Individual Referral Goals
- Number of referrals asked weekly
- Number of referrals received
- Number of referred contacted

## Process

### Referral Generation
1. **Website Referral Tab** - Generates referral coupons
2. **"Refer a Friend" Contact Form** - From/To customer name and email
3. **Invoice Blurb** - Bottom of invoices with program details
4. **Every Interaction** - Ask "Would you like fries with that?"
5. **Networking Events** - Attend CoC and other events
6. **Educational Content** - Publish every X days
7. **Customer Features** - Top client case studies

### Follow-up
- Reach out to referred contacts every X days
- Send thank-you notes when referrals sign up
- Send NRIC credits when earned
- Reward top referrers quarterly
- Hold referral contests quarterly

### Newsletter Integration
- Include referral coupon for sharing
- Feature successful referrals

## Referral Channels

1. **Friends/Family** - Direct personal networks
2. **News Publications** - Customer reviews and opinions
3. **Influencer Opinions** - Bloggers, social media influencers
4. **Testimonials** - Customer success stories
5. **Ask for Referrals** - Systematic referral requests
6. **Be Remarkable** - Stand out from competition
7. **Shareable Stories** - Simple, easy-to-share narratives
8. **Lead Nurturing** - Follow up with new referrals
9. **Social Media Strategy** - Content that sparks conversations
10. **Websites and Social Media** - Digital referral channels

## Program Rules

### Referrer Eligibility
Must:
1. Belong to Loyalty Program
2. Have account in good standing at referral time
3. Have account in good standing when reward awarded

### Referred Eligibility
To be considered new customer:
1. Not been Loyalty Program member in past
2. Sign up for Loyalty Program to be eligible

### Reward Structure

#### Referral Incentives
- **Cold Referral** (contact info only): XXX
- **Warm Referral** (contact info + mentioned before contact): YYY
- **Introduction**: ZZZ

#### Types of Incentives
- Monetary: discounts, rebates, gift cards
- Non-monetary: key chains, event admission, product access

#### Post-Purchase Rewards
When referred customer interacts:
- AAA reward

### Reward Calculation
Referrers receive Non Refundable In-Store Discount Credits (NRDCs) totaling 10% of referred customer's first purchase.

**Terms**:
- NRDCs = 10% of pre-tax purchase price
- Can apply to 10% of invoice total (pre-tax)
- Credited 30 days after referred's purchase

### Referral Credits Tracking
- Referral fees accrued to account **9792** - Advertising, Marketing Costs & Promotion

## New Customer Benefits
New customers who are referred receive **10% off** regularly priced items on first purchase.
