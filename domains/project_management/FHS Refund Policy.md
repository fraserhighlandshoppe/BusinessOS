# FHS Refund Policy

## Overview
Returns and refunds policy for Fraser Highland Shoppe products.

## General Policy
**All sales are final unless otherwise specified below.**

We offer **exchange** on some items. Items must be returned within **14 calendar days** of receipt.

**Important**: Contact us in advance for Return Authorization before mailing items.

## Shipping Charges
- **We do not refund shipping charges**
- Customer pays return shipping cost
- **Exception**: If items are defective, we pay return shipping

## Product Categories

### Costume Items
**Not eligible for return**:
- Next to skin items (kilts, shirts, hose, headdress)
- Footwear (Ghillies, etc) with signs of wear
- Sporrans and Kilt belts (may exchange if defective only)

### Custom Orders
**Not eligible for returns**

### Piping and Drumming Supplies
May be returned for **in-store credit** if not in exclusion list.

#### Restocking Fees
| Condition | Restocking Fee |
|---|---|
| Original unopened packaging | 20% |
| Opened, sellable as new | 30% |
| Used condition, sellable | 50% |

#### Refund Price
Lower of:
- Original price, OR
- Current public price (regular retail or active sale price)

## Exclusion List

### Not Eligible for Return
- Reeds
- Mouthpieces
- Mouthpiece protectors
- Pipe Bags

### Not Eligible if Opened
- Hemp
- Seasoning

## International Returns
For customers shipping from outside Canada:
- Mark parcel "RETURNING CANADIAN GOODS ZERO (0) VALUE"
- Failure to mark may result in customs charges passed to customer

## Return Process

### Step 1: Contact Us
- Get Return Authorization before shipping
- Confirm item eligibility

### Step 2: Package Items
- Original packaging preferred
- Include all accessories

### Step 3: Ship Items
- Use tracked shipping
- Customer pays unless defective

### Step 4: Receive and Process
- Inspect returned items
- Issue exchange or credit
- Process refund if applicable
