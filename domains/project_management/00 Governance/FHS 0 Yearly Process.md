# FHS 0 Yearly Process

## Purpose
Annual planning and execution of FHS marketing and business activities.

## Year-End Process

### Internal Audit

#### Customer Audit
1. Ensure all outstanding Sales Orders have been delivered or are current
2. Ensure all Customer Invoicing has been completed
3. Finalize or cancel non-current orders
4. Audit Sales Invoices and Payments (stapled: invoice, order, payment, delivery)
5. Check customer payment allocations
6. Accrue unbilled revenue (Dec 31: move to MAGIC CASH, Jan 2: move back)
7. Update allowance for doubtful accounts
8. Run Customer Balances report (goal: zero balances)

#### Supplier Audit
1. Ensure Vendor Invoicing is complete
2. Audit all Purchase Orders received into FrontAccounting
3. Check all POs have been invoiced and printed
4. Ensure all invoices have been paid and printed
5. Check Outstanding Vendor Balances
6. Verify vendor allocations

#### Inventory Audit
1. Run full store inventory count
2. Investigate shrinkage and overage
3. Invoice shrinkage to Cash Sales (100% discount)
4. Generate Purchase Orders for overstock
5. Compare Sales transactions to COGS

#### Banking Audit
1. Audit all bank account activity reconciliations
2. Reconcile Kevin's Visa/Savings, Square Up, PayPal
3. Balance the cashbox (journal entry using 1001 Magic Cash)

#### Payroll Audit
1. Ensure Payroll is complete
2. Accrue wages, vacation, benefits
3. Calculate commissions and bonuses

#### Expenses Audit
1. Ensure all expenses entered
2. Match vendor till slips to journal entries
3. Print expense account journals

### Tax Reports

#### Generate Tax Reports
1. Run Inventory Purchase Report (remove charges/shipping, line 8320)
2. Run Sales Summary Sheet (check against P/L and GST calcs)
3. Run Balance Sheet (validate assets/liabilities)
4. Run Profit/Loss (validate expenses vs budget/last year)
5. Transfer GST totals to CRA GST Payable

#### Calculate T5013 Totals
- Gross Income + Net GST
- Net GST Collected
- Adjusted Gross Sales
- Closing Income (previous year's opening)
- Purchases, Subcontracts, Other Costs
- Closing Inventory
- COGS
- All expense categories
- Net Income
- Income Taxes Owed

#### Calculate Asset Depreciation
- Class 8 (20%): 1820 → 5952
- Class 12 (100%): 1521, 1770 → 5660
- Class 10 (30%): 1850 → 5951

### Close Temporary Accounts
1. Transfer Owner Drawing to Equity
2. Transfer CCA values to accumulated amortization
3. Transfer Revenue/Expense to Retained Earnings
4. Update Company Setup for new fiscal year
5. Close previous fiscal year

## Inventory

### Year-End Inventory Count
1. Generate invoice for shrinkage (use 0% cash customer)
2. For overstock, generate credit note (use 0% cash customer)
3. Trace goods source for shrinkage investigation

## Strategy and Planning

### Marketing
- Plan advertising locations (Publications, social media, competition books)
- Select competitions to attend
- Choose Highland Games for sponsorship
- Review marketing calendar (cultural dates, sales timing, competitions)

### Products
- Review sales and turnover rates
- Identify new product lines to carry
- Determine products to discontinue

### Systems and Software
- Confirm workflows and document process changes
- Discuss enhancements for workflow/software improvements
- Prioritize changes by impact and timing
