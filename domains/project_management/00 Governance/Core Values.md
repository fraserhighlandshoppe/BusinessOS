# FHS Core Values

## Purpose
Core values drive all decisions at Fraser Highland Shoppe and guide our culture and operations.

## Core Values

### 1. Availability
- Open during waking hours to serve customers when they want
- Responsive to customer needs and inquiries

### 2. Education
- Educate customers about products and their proper use
- Share knowledge about Highland arts and culture
- Provide learning opportunities for staff and community

### 3. Collaboration and Partnership
- Work with pipe bands, dance schools, and community groups
- Partner with suppliers who share our values
- Collaborate with customers to find solutions

### 4. Friendship
- Build genuine relationships with customers
- Treat everyone like family
- Create welcoming community space

### 5. Trust and Personal Responsibility in ALL Relationships
- Be a trusted advisor to every customer
- Take responsibility for our commitments
- Honor all relationships with integrity

## Business Cornerstones
- Honesty
- Ethical Behavior
- Integrity
- Quality
- Service and Support

## Core Values Exercise Questions

1. **What is your most meaningful memory of the shop?**
2. **What positive stories have become "larger than life" at this shop?**
3. **What other symbols (facilities, property, events, rituals) have meaning?**
4. **What draws new people to the shop?**
5. **What difficult decisions were particularly good?**

## Avoiding Common Value Statement Pitfalls

❌ Don't use:
- Ethical (too generic)
- Teamwork (obvious)
- Authentic (vague)
- Fun (not core)
- Customer orientated (expected)

✅ Do use values that:
- Are genuinely different for FHS
- Would be bad if we had the opposite
- Reflect what makes us unique
