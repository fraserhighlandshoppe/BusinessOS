## Monthly Process

### Cash Management
1. **Balance Cash Box (Wallet)**
   - Journal Entry using 1001 Magic Cash as opposite account

### Purchase Orders
- Check outstanding Purchase Orders in FrontAccounting

### Sales Orders
- Check outstanding Sales Orders

### Financial Adjustments
1. **Record all transactions**
   - Accruals
   - Amortization
   - Depreciation

### Bank Reconciliation
1. **Calculate account balances**
   - Balance/reconcile cash box, Square, etc.
   - Reconcile bank statement against journal entries
   - May need to add bank charges, interest, etc

2. **Calculate credits and debits**
   - For income and expense accounts

### Financial Reporting
1. **Review Trial Balance** (Report 708)
2. **Review Budget vs. Actuals**
   - Determine why discrepancies between budget (assumptions) and actuals

### Cash Flow Review
- Do we have enough cash to cover next month?

### Management Reporting
1. **Sales Reports**
2. **Balance Sheets** (Report 706)
3. **Profit & Loss** (Report 707) - Income and Expenses
4. **Sales Summary** (Report 114)
5. **Operating Ratio** (Expenses vs Income)
6. **Revenue/COGS** (COGS vs Income)

## Financial Reports and Activities

### Accounts Receivable
1. Print Customer Statement of Accounts (Report 108)
2. Calculate over-due admin fees
   - Email statements requesting settlement
3. Collections (aging on statements)
4. Write-offs (bad debt - need paper trail showing collection attempts)

### Accounts Payable
1. Submit GST tax report
2. Setup transfer for Mastercard
3. Ensure all received shipments entered and invoiced
4. Annotate invoices (print entries, staple)
5. File invoices
6. Process affiliate commissions (WooCommerce WPAM plugin)
   - Check returns, refunds, order cancellations against affiliates

### Inventory Management
1. Monthly Inventory Forecast Report (Report 312)
2. Monthly Inventory Count
3. Quarterly Inventory Forecast

## Payroll Processing
1. **Ensure all employee data entered**
2. **Employee salary payments**
   - Calculate Gross payments
     - Contract specifics: salary/wage, tips, commission, overtime
     - Exempt pay (reduces taxable)
     - Deductions: agreements, dues, taxes, retirement, health plans
     - Net = Gross - deductions
     - Generate payroll cheque or EFT
3. **Employee expense payments**
4. **Payroll tax submissions**
