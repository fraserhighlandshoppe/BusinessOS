# FHS 0 Weekly Process

## Overview
Weekly operational processes for Fraser Highland Shoppe.

## Cash Management
1. **Balance Cash Box (Wallet)**
   - Journal Entry using 1001 Magic Cash as opposite account

### Bank Reconciliation
- Check bank accounts (ATB Online)
- Reconcile into FrontAccounting
- Check cash flow - adequacy for next week and next 4 weeks

## Marketing

### Newsletter Preparation
- Prep article for next month's newsletter

### Weekly Sale
1. Prep next week's weekly sale item
2. Create coupon or Discount
3. Publish to weekly sales letter

## Accounts Payable
1. **Tax Report** - Submit GST as required
2. **Setup transfer** for Mastercard
3. **Verify shipments entered and invoiced**
   - Annotate invoices (print entries, staple)
   - File invoices
   - Pay invoices (currently pay at time of purchase via CC)

## Inventory Management
1. **Monthly Inventory Forecast Report** (Report 312)
2. **Weekly Inventory Count**
