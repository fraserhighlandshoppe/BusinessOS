# FHS FA Receive a Shipment

## Overview
Process for receiving supplier shipments in FrontAccounting.

## Prerequisites
- Purchase Order must exist in FrontAccounting
- See [FHS FA Creating a Purchase Order](FHS_FA_Creating_a_Purchase_Order.md)

## Step-by-Step Process

### Step 1: Access FrontAccounting
- Log in to FrontAccounting
- Click **Purchases** tab at top of screen
- Click **Outstanding Purchase Orders** link

### Step 2: Select Purchase Order
- Select appropriate Purchase Order
- May need to change date range to find PO

### Step 3: Open PO for Receiving
- Click **Receive Icon** at right end of row
- **Alternative**: Click **Edit** (pencil) to alter PO

### Step 4: Receive Items
**Select Appropriate Data**:
- Change Deliver Into Location
- Confirm Receive Date
- Confirm Delivery numbers and update as necessary

**Finalize**:
- Click **Process Receive Items**

## Best Practices
- Always verify delivery quantity matches PO
- Update inventory immediately after receiving
- Check for damaged items before processing
- Keep delivery documentation for records
