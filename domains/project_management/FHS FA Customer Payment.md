# FHS FA Customer Payment

## Overview
Process for recording customer payments in FrontAccounting.

## When to Use
- Customer purchases on "due in 10 days" terms
- Split payments
- Partial payments against invoices

## Step-by-Step Process

### Step 1: Access Customer Payment
1. Log into FrontAccounting
2. Go to **Sales** tab
3. Select **Customer Payments**

### Step 2: Enter Payment Details
**Customer Payment Entry**:
1. Select customer
   - Select correct branch
2. Select correct payment bank account
3. Check date of deposit
4. Add any bank fees
5. Fill in "This Allocation" against appropriate invoices
   - Partial payments or full payments against 1+ invoices
6. Add discount if appropriate
7. Add memo if needed (e.g., interac/customer cheque #123/...)
8. Click **Add Payment**

### Step 3: Print/Email Receipt
If customer wants receipt:
1. Use receipt print menu
2. Receipt shows:
   - Customer info
   - Payment details
   - Invoice allocations
   - Receipt number

- Process payments promptly
- Match payment dates to bank deposits
- Keep receipt copies for records
- Use memos for payment method tracking
