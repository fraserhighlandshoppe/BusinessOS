# FHS Sales Cycle and Customer Journey

## Overview
Blends Grant Cardone's Sales Cycle, Digital Marketer's Customer Journey, and Mautic's Customer Journey with FSBO and Harvard Business Review processes.

## External Resources

### Grant Cardone Sales Cycle
1. **Attention** - Make prospect aware of product/service
2. **Greet** - Attract or greet the customer
3. **Determine Needs and Wants** - Discovery
4. **Presentation** - Present solution
5. **Proposal** - Formal offer
6. **Close** - Make the sale

### Customer Journey (Blended)
1. **Awareness** - Customer becomes aware of need
2. **Interest** - Customer researches options
3. **Engage** - Attract or greet
4. **Subscribe** - Join mailing list/follow page
5. **Consider** - Determine requirements
6. **Intent** - Presentation/Proposal
7. **Evaluate** - Customer decides
8. **Convert/Purchase** - Make sale
9. **Experience** - Deliver product/service
10. **Excite** - Upsell/cross-sell
11. **Ascend** - Grow relationship
12. **Advocate** - Get referrals
13. **Promote** - Customer becomes promoter

## FHS Sales Cycle Through Customer Journey

### Awareness (Attention)
**Goal**: Make prospect aware of product/service that addresses their need

**Tools**:
- Social Media Profile
- Social Media Posts
- Social Media Interaction
- Website
- Mailing List
- TV/Radio
- Print Ads
- Bulk Mail
- Door Hangers
- Word of Mouth

### Interest
**Goal**: Customer researches options for comparisons

**Tools**:
- Website with Product Info
- Short and Long Copy with product benefits
- Social Media Posts
- Email newsletter (headline caught interest)

### Engage (Greet)
**Goal**: Attract customer (digital) or greet in person

**Tools**:
- Email List
- Direct Email Correspondence
- Social Media Posts
- Social Media Interactions
- Facebook Messenger

**Action**: Book initial meeting

### Exception Handling
- Client may not be a fit
- May not build rapport/trust
- Client may not progress through journey

### Initial Meeting
**Goal**: Establish relationship, build trust, set expectations

### Subscribe
**Goal**: Get customer through to Intent and Evaluate

**Tools**:
- Website Landing Page
- Social Media Posts
- Hardcopy flyers/door hangers

### Exception Handling (Subscription)
- Subscriber might unsubscribe

### Consider (Needs and Wants)
**Goal**: Determine client's needs and wants

**Tools**:
- Questions/Questionnaire
- Know Your Client (KYC)
- Financial Needs Assessment (FNA)

### Intent (Presentation/Proposal)
**Goal**: Present solution and proposal

### Evaluate
**Goal**: Customer decides

### Convert / Purchase
**Goal**: Make the sale

### Experience
**Goal**: Deliver product/service successfully

### Excite
**Goal**: Upsell/cross-sell

### Ascend
**Goal**: Grow relationship

### Advocate
**Goal**: Get referrals

### Promote
**Goal**: Customer becomes promoter

## Touchpoint Optimization

### Principle
Touchpoints lead to friction. Friction can lead to derailed journeys or negative emotions. Craft approach so touchpoints lead to positive experience.

### Exception Handling
- Ensure exceptions still provide positive experience
- Have recovery plans for derailed journeys
