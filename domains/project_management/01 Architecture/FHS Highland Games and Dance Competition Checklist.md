# FHS Highland Games and Dance Competition Checklist

## Overview
Comprehensive checklist for participating in Highland Games and Dance Competitions.

## Steps of a Games or Competition
1. **Prep** - Planning and preparation
2. **Travel** - Getting to venue
3. **Setup** - Booth and equipment setup
4. **Sell** - Running the business
5. **Marketing** - Promotions and engagement
6. **Meals** - Food and hydration
7. **Tear Down** - Pack up and clean
8. **Travel** - Return journey
9. **Unpack** - Home setup

## Prep Phase

### Registration
- Register with event authority
- Book hotel if needed

### Home
- Arrange house sitter
- Prepare home for absence (shovel snow, etc.)

### Goods
- Purchase needed items
- Cashbox with sufficient coins and bills

### Marketing
- Social Media Posts
- Branded shirts

### Tech Checklist
- Charge all devices:
  - Tablet
  - Laptop
  - Square Up reader
  - Battery pack
  - Cell phone
  - Bluetooth Speaker
  - UPS
  - Label Printer
  - Printer (power, paper, labels, spare ink)
  - Power Bar
  - Barcode Scanner
  - Cable locks

### Booth Equipment
- Tents
- Tables
- Chairs
- Tablecloths
- Racks
- Fridge Cart
- Price Gun
- Label Printer

### Vehicle
- Air conditioning check
- Oil change
- Tire inspection
- Full gas tank

### Meals
- Pop and juice packs/crystals
- Lunch meat
- Bread
- Snacks

### Tools
- Garbage Bags
- S-Hooks
- Toolbox (pliers, screwdriver, hammer, side cutters, socket set)

### Test Setup
- Check tent and parts
- Check tables, chairs

## Night Before

### Final Pack
#### Clothes
- Branded shirts
- Kilt and gear (if applicable)

#### Electronics
- Tablet (Bluetooth, batteries)
- Laptop (cable lock, barcode scanner, keyboard, mouse, USB power cables)
- Power Bar
- Extension Cord
- Printer (spare ink)

## Travel Day

### Loading
- Secure all equipment in vehicle
- Double-check everything is packed

### Arrival
- Arrive early for setup
- Check in with event organizers

## Setup Phase

### Booth Setup
1. Erect tent
2. Set up tables and chairs
3. Apply tablecloths
4. Organize racks
5. Set up fridge cart
6. Position price gun and label printer

### Tech Setup
1. Power all devices
2. Connect to WiFi
3. Test Square Up reader
4. Verify barcode scanner works

## Sell Phase

### Customer Service
- Engage with visitors
- Provide excellent service
- Answer questions about products
- Process sales efficiently

### Inventory Management
- Track sales
- Monitor stock levels
- Replenish as needed

## Marketing Phase

### Social Media
- Post from event location
- Share photos of products
- Engage with followers

### Community Engagement
- Network with other vendors
- Connect with event attendees
- Build relationships

## Meals Phase

### Hydration
- Drink water regularly
- Bring reusable water bottles

### Nutrition
- Pack healthy snacks
- Take breaks for meals
- Maintain energy levels

## Tear Down Phase

### Packing Order
1. Sell remaining items
2. Pack unsold goods
3. Disassemble booth
4. Pack equipment

### Cleanup
- Leave venue clean
- Take all trash with you
- Return borrowed items

## Travel Home

### Vehicle Prep
- Secure all packed items
- Remove equipment from vehicle

### Post-Event
- Unpack and organize
- Update inventory records
- Clean and maintain equipment
