# FHS Employee Policies

## Overview
Employee policies for Fraser Highland Shoppe covering hiring, vacation, sick days, training, goals, and compensation.

## Hiring Policy

### Criteria
Hire employees who:
- Possess the values of our business
- Are passionate about Piping, Drumming, or Dancing

## Vacation Policy

### Alberta Vacation Entitlement
- **Years 2-5**: 2 weeks paid vacation
- **Year 5+**: 3 weeks paid vacation

### Vacation Pay Calculation
**Monthly Salary Employees**:
- Weekly pay = Monthly pay ÷ 4⅓

**Non-monthly Employees**:
- 2 weeks vacation = 4% of earned wages
- 3 weeks vacation = 6% of earned wages

### Vacation Rules
- Vacation pay = percentage of wages for year before entitlement
- "Wages" includes previously paid vacation pay (excludes overtime, holiday pay, etc.)
- Vacation must be taken in 12 months after entitlement
- Unbroken period preferred, but shorter periods (min 1 day) allowed if requested
- Employer decides if mutual agreement not reached
- Employee must receive 2 weeks written notice

### Vacation Scheduling
- Vacation pay computed first, then time off
- Employees must take actual time off
- Cannot take vacation pay as cash without taking days off (PILL exception applies)

## Sick Days Policy

### Alberta Labour Law
- **No paid sick days required by law**
- Employees not entitled to paid sick leave under Employment Standards Code

## Training Policy

### Purpose
- Develop employee skills
- Support career growth
- Align with business objectives

## Employee Goals Policy

### Goal Setting
- Employees file goals every **January and July**
- Set using SMART template
- Goals set collaboratively with manager
- Goals incorporate:
  - Corporate annual goals
  - Job Scorecard (job description)
  - Training plan

### Goal Evaluation
- Progress evaluated every **June and November**
- Rating: Met, Exceeded, or Not Met goals
- Customer satisfaction surveys considered

## Performance Reviews

### Rating System
- **Met** - Goals achieved
- **Exceeded** - Goals surpassed
- **Not Met** - Goals not achieved

### Evaluation Factors
- Customer satisfaction survey results
- Goal achievement
- Skill development
- Team contribution

## Compensation and Benefits

### Sales Positions
- Paid by **commission**
- Bonus potential for EXCEED rating performance reviews

### Non-Customer-Facing Positions
- Paid **minimum wage**

### Bonuses
- Awarded for EXCEED performance ratings
- Set by Board of Directors
- Based on individual and company performance
