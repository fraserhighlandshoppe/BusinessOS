# FHS Product Review

## Overview
Product review strategy to leverage word-of-mouth marketing and social proof.

## Why Product Reviews Matter

### Human Psychology
- Word of mouth is most powerful marketing tool
- We're programmed to act on others' actions
- Herd mentality drives purchasing decisions
- Safety in numbers with social proof

### Online Age Impact
- Even online, we listen to fellow humans
- "Herd mentality" leads to purchases
- Forum activity drives buying (Slickdeals example)
- "SD affect" - unnecessary purchases from social proof

## Social Proof Power

### Review Volume Impact
An item with:
- **4.5 stars, 10 reviews** ≠
- **4 stars, 300 reviews**

More reviews = Better sales, more convincing, higher visibility

## How to Get Reviews

### 1. Provide Great Consumer Experience
- Avoid misleading product descriptions
- Be honest and precise about products
- Prevent confusion and false expectations

### 2. Send Follow-up Emails After Purchase
- Amazon's generic emails aren't enough
- Send personalized review requests
- Time close to delivery while experience is fresh
- Use tools like FeedbackFive for automation
- Include easy review links

### 3. Request Reviews via Newsletter and Social Media
- Newsletter subscribers already like you
- Facebook fans trust your recommendations
- Good chance they've purchased before
- Request legitimate, positive reviews

### 4. Reach Out to Similar Product Reviewers
- Visit "Customers Who Bought This Item Also Bought"
- Visit "Customers Who Viewed This Item Also Viewed"
- Check competitor reviews
- Find contact info via profile or other social networks
- Send email or connect on Twitter/Facebook

### 5. Solicit Top Amazon Reviewers
- Amazon maintains list of top reviewers
- Look for "Top Ten Reviewer" or "Hall of Fame Reviewer"
- Their reviews are ultra-valuable
- Request reviews from esteemed reviewers

## Example Review Request Email

```
Hello Kevin,

Thank you again for your order of "[Product Name]".

We are happy to offer you 10% discount exclusively on your next order. 
Simply leave us feedback and product review within 7 days. 
After next order email us and we will refund 10% of product price.

It was delivered a few days ago, so I hope you've had a chance to 
open the package and ensure everything is in order. 

Please let us know if anything is wrong so we can correct it. 
You can also simply reply to this email.

We only strive for 5 star feedback. If anything lower than that, 
please let us know the reason so we can assure a happy customer.

If everything went smoothly, I'd really appreciate it if you could 
take a few seconds to leave feedback for Fraser Highland Shoppe. 
It really affects our ability to sell and be successful. 

One of the things that makes Amazon such a great place to shop is 
the feedback system and active participation by buyers like you.
```

## Best Practices
- Always provide honest, accurate product info
- Follow up promptly after delivery
- Make review process as easy as possible
- Use multiple channels (email, social, newsletter)
- Track review request effectiveness
