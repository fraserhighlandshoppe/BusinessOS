# FHS Loyalty Program

## Overview
Customer loyalty program offering benefits and exclusive offers.

## Program Benefits

### Discounts
- **10% off** virtually all products
- Access to Registered Users price book

### Additional Savings
- Receive additional discounts through [[FHS Referral Program]]
- Non-refundable in-store credits

### Exclusive Offers
- Special offers not available to general public
- Newsletter exclusive deals

### Advance Notice
- Advanced notice of sales and promotions
- Early access to events

## Accounting

### Discount Recording
Loyalty program payments recorded as:
- **Account 4510** - Other Revenue/Discounts Given

## How to Join

### Registration
1. Register on website
2. Provide contact information
3. Receive welcome discount

### Benefits Activation
- Automatic 10% discount applied
- Access to member-only offers
- Newsletter subscription

## Referral Program Integration

### Earn Benefits
- Refer friends to FHS
- Earn additional in-store credits
- Both referrer and referee receive benefits

### Tracking
- Track referrals through unique codes
- Apply credits automatically

## Newsletter Integration

### Content
- Member-exclusive offers
- Early sale announcements
- Event invitations
- Product previews

### Frequency
- Regular updates
- Special occasion communications
