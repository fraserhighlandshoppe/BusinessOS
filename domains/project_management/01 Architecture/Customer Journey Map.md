# Customer Journey Map

## Purpose
Visually illustrate customer needs, interactions, and emotional states throughout their experience with FHS.

## Definition
Customer journey maps are documents that:
- Illustrate an individual customer's needs and goals
- Show the series of interactions necessary to fulfill those needs
- Capture resulting emotional states throughout the process

## Key Elements

### Customer Needs
- What the customer has set out to achieve
- Core tasks they perform
- Goals they want to accomplish

### Interactions
- The flow of the customer experience
- Touchpoints from awareness to purchase to post-purchase
- Both physical and digital interactions

### Emotional States
- Satisfaction levels before, during, and after interactions
- Pain points and moments of delight
- How customers feel at each stage

## Re-assessment Guidelines

### 1. Review Touchpoints
- Put customer journey in front of customers
- Verify they agree with each touchpoint
- Identify missing touchpoints
- Examine unhappy customer journeys

### 2. Find Gaps
- Speak with customer-facing team
- Interview customers at each stage
- Ask: "Why didn't they place an order?"
- Identify unmet needs
- Bridge physical and digital touchpoints

### 3. Add Value at Every Stage
- See business through customer's eyes
- Treat every potential customer as advocate
- Interact directly with people you serve

## Based on Behavior
- Base personas on actual customer behavior
- Communicate core tasks customers perform
- Recreate personas from behavior patterns, not just job roles

## Effective Journey Mapping

### Research Methods
- Ethnographic research
- Contextual inquiry
- Experience a day in the customer's life
- Perceive customer emotions

### Data Sources
- Internal research
- External research
- Click-through rates
- Open rates
- Customer feedback

## Benefits
- Highlights flow of customer experience
- Reveals pain points and frustrations
- Makes needs and emotions digestible
- Invites stakeholders to share customer experience
- Helps convey story to management and teams
- Improves service and product experience
