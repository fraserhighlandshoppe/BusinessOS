# FHS Applicant Process

## Overview
Complete hiring process from job posting to offer acceptance.

## Application Process

### Hurdle 1: Typing Test
**Purpose**: Assess typing speed and accuracy

**Process**:
1. Applicant must paste URL for typing speed results
2. Many people won't follow instructions and will insert score

**Evaluation**:
- Low score with many errors = didn't try multiple times
- Slow typing = likely slow in other tasks

### Hurdle 2: Multi-Step Assignment
**Task**:
1. Download video from link
2. Upload video to YouTube
3. Create new WordPress post
4. Title: "Ryan Video" (exact spacing)
5. Write 100-word blog post summarizing video
6. Insert YouTube video into post
7. Copy URL and paste in application space

**Evaluation**:
- Can they follow directions exactly? (Ryan Video vs Ryan's Video)
- Can they interpret, summarize, and write?
- Ability to research and think on feet
- Writing ability assessment

### Hurdle 3: Deadline Fax Test
**Question**: "I'm looking for someone who's hopefully going to be on my team for the next 5-10 years. What is it going to take for you to want to be in that kind of position?"

**Process**:
1. Hand-write response
2. Fax within 24 hours

**Why Use Fax**:
1. Tests ability to work under pressure with tight deadline
2. Tests logistical problem-solving (not everyone has fax access)
3. Handwriting analysis reveals personality traits
4. Reveals salary/job expectations upfront

## Short List
- Compile candidates who passed all hurdles
- Review qualifications and fit

## Interviews
- Conduct initial interviews
- Assess skills and cultural fit
- Verify qualifications

## Job Offer
- Extend formal job offer
- Include terms and conditions
- Get acceptance
