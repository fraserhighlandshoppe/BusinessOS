# FHS Sales Objectives

## Overview
Strategic sales objectives for Fraser Highland Shoppe.

## Primary Objectives

### Market Position
1. **Become the #1 stop** for Piping, Drumming, and Dancing supplies in Alberta

### Community Presence
2. **Become active and vocal member** in Scottish community around Airdrie

### Digital Presence
3. **Make website an active community education and communication tool**
   - New content weekly (blog)
   - Standing pages for product lines/packages
   - Expand keyword coverage

### Customer Growth
4. **Increase Customer Database by 25% every year**
5. **Increase repeat customer sales** from 1.5 to 2.5 occurrences per customer per year
6. **Increase repeat customer sales** from $260 to $433 per year per customer
7. **Increase customers served** from 25 to 30 customers per year
   - 25 doesn't include Kevin/Kim
   - Cash sales at Highland Games counted as 1

### Market Expansion
8. **Increase bands as customers** from 2 to 5
9. **Increase sales at Highland Games** so each game pays for itself

### Revenue Streams
10. **Institute PRODUCT OF THE MONTH (subscription) club**
    - Sold at cost for club members
11. **Increase Gift Certificate sales** from 0 to 1/month
12. **Provide new revenue streams** through partnerships and affiliates
    - What local businesses can we partner with?
    - What chain business could we partner with?
    - Where does Brewhouse get its tartan skirts?

- Track customer database growth monthly
- Monitor repeat purchase frequency
- Measure average customer spend
- Count customers served annually
- Track band customer count
- Evaluate game profitability
