# How to Generate Referrals

## Overview
Referrals are a powerful way to leverage your marketing investment. You've already invested time, effort, energy, and money to get a customer - they're your best source for new potential customers.

## Mindset Shift

### Traditional View
- Referrals = "nice tip" for good job
- Accidental result

### Strategic View
- Referrals = Designed process
- Systematic approach
- **Result**: 5-10x more referrals

## Key Principles

### 1. Make Referrals Part of the Relationship from Day 1
Instead of waiting until after transaction:
- Begin making referrals "part of the deal"
- Explain you rely on them
- Say you're going to earn them
- Plant seed to start referring NOW
- See [[How to Ask for a Referral]]

### 2. Connect During Emotional "Windows of Opportunity"
During complex transactions or relationships:
- There are emotional windows to make big impression
- Contact, call, visit - offer something memorable

### 3. Give Customers Stories to Tell
- Humans use stories to bond and connect
- Give great stories customers will tell automatically
- Stories about your products and services

### 4. Thank Customers When They Refer
- Thank them for sending referral
- Reward the behavior you want more of

## Referral Generation Tactics

### Early Integration
- Make referral part of customer experience from first interaction
- Set expectation that their input is valuable

### Emotional Connection
- Identify emotional windows in customer journey
- Create memorable experiences during these moments

### Story Creation
- Develop unique, interesting, fun stories
- Stories customers want to share
- Make referral natural extension of experience

### Recognition
- Thank customers who refer
- Publicly acknowledge good referrals
- Reward referral behavior

## Exercise Questions
1. How could you incorporate Referrals into relationships from the beginning?
2. What are the emotional "Windows Of Opportunity" for your customers?
3. How could you give customers unique stories to tell others?
