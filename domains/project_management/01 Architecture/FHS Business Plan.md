## Executive Summary

### Purpose
To operate a retail shop catering to Scottish, Highland Dancing, and Pipes and Drums communities.

### Business Scope
Originally focused on:
- Bagpipes and Piping
- Drums
- Highland Dance
- Scottish Country Dance
- Scottish apparel
- Celtic jewelry and giftware

Expanded to include:
- Musical instruments and accessories
- Scottish general interest items
- Special order capabilities

### One-Minute Story
We provide quality and affordable products within the realm of Scottish Culture. We help pipers, drummers and dancers acquire the items they need to practice their arts. We also have items that Scots/Celts would use to celebrate their culture and heritage.

### Business Structure
- Operated as a Partnership (Service Alberta definition)
- Partnership registered with Province of Alberta
- 1 class of shares, 100 shares total
- Maximum 50 shareholders
- Transfer controlled by exit plan
- Shares not offered to public

## Strategic Framework

### 6 Success Questions
1. **Why** - Why does this matter?
2. **Why Not** - What prevents doing it?
3. **What If** - What if we did it?
4. **What If Not** - What if we didn't?
5. **What Else** - What else could we do?
6. **How/When/By When** - Timeline and execution

### 7 Additional Questions
1. What business are you in?
2. What is your purpose?
3. What data do you have and where is it?
4. What technology infrastructure is needed?
5. Who is your customer?
6. What is your value proposition?
7. Who are your competitors?
8. What's the risk of standing still?
