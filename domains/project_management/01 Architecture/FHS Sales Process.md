# FHS Sales Process

## Overview
End-to-end sales process supporting FHS Sales Objectives and Marketing Plan.

## Growth Strategies
There are only 4 ways to grow sales:
1. **Attract new customers**
2. **Keep existing customers**
3. **Help customers buy more**
4. **Help customers buy more often**

## End-to-End Process

### 1. Locate Lead
**Goal**: Find prospects in marketplace

**Methods**:
- Advertising
- Social Media
- Word of mouth
- Referrals

### 2. Convert Lead
**Goal**: Turn lead into customer

**Steps**:
- Proposal Generation
- Quote in FrontAccounting
- Followup
- Contract Process
- Customer Purchase (Quote to Order)

### 3. Deliver Goods
**Goal**: Fulfill customer order

**Steps**:
- Invoice Order (printed invoice required for audit)
- Direct Invoice (POS/till purchases)
- Trade In Process (if applicable)

### 4. Customer Satisfaction Followup
**Goal**: Ensure customer satisfaction and gather feedback

**Process**:
- Send followup email after every transaction
- Include link to satisfaction survey
- Include product review link
- Request testimonial

**WooCommerce Integration**:
- 5% coupon after first order (min $20 spend)

### 5. Relationship Maintenance
**Goal**: Cycle back to step 2

**Methods**:
- More marketing
- Customer education
- Referral requests

## Specific Processes

### Locate Lead → Advertising
- Professional photography for LinkedIn profile
- Avoid common LinkedIn picture mistakes

### Locate Lead → Social Media
#### LinkedIn Strategy
- Don't rely solely on LinkedIn for business
- Use other channels for primary lead generation
- Optimize profile picture and content

### Convert Lead → Proposal Generation
#### Quote in FA
- See [[FHS Discount Policy]]
- Follow up using Elegant Follow-up Technique

#### Followup
- Use Simple Email Trick (research industry news, send relevant article)

### Receive Payment
**Process**:
1. Receive Payment in FA
2. Print Receipt (1 for bookkeeping, 1 for customer)
3. Submit printed Receipt to Bookkeeper
4. Bookkeeper attaches receipt to Invoice

**Post-Payment**:
- Send followup email with satisfaction survey
- Request product review
- Request testimonial
