# FHS Department Structure

## Overview
FHS organizational structure with roles, responsibilities, and departmental functions.

## Department Roles

### COO - Chief Operations Officer
- **Main Operations Person**
- Responsibilities:
  - Shipping, Receiving
  - Pricing
  - Inventory Control
- Actions:
  - Supervise General Partners
  - Obtain insurance for business
  - Ensure all business documents are secured

### CFO - Chief Financial Officer
- **Bookkeeping and Finance**
- Responsibilities:
  - Paying Vendors
  - Ensuring accounts are up to date
  - Quarterly reports/audits
- Actions:
  - Ensure receivables collected on time
  - Pay payables before due dates (as late as possible)
  - Prepare budget
  - Establish and manage bank accounts

### PAO - Public Affairs Officer
- **Marketing and Customer Relations**
- Responsibilities:
  - Marketing
  - Customer Relationships
  - Customer Loyalty Program
  - Customer Referrals Program
  - Customer Returns and Refunds Policy
- Actions:
  - Produce meeting minutes
  - Address marketing of property

### CIO - Chief Information Officer
- **Technology**
- Responsibilities:
  - Domain name management
  - Hosting setup
  - Technology maintenance

## Team Structure

### Leadership
- General Partner - CEO
- General Partner - CFO
- General Partner - COO
- General Partner - PAO

### Operations Team
- Book Keeper
- Accountant
- Lawyer (Legal Counsel)

## Management Agreement
- Must be drawn up and reviewed by Legal Counsel
- COO supervises General Partners

## Meetings

### Quarterly General Partners Meeting
- Review consolidated reports
- Review book keeper reports
- Review legal summaries
- Review accountant advisories
- PAO produces meeting minutes

### Annual General Meeting (AGM)
- COO discusses issues, risks, new risks
- COO discusses maintenance, capital improvements, capital schedule
- CFO presents expense report and projected budget
- CFO confirms insurance levels
- PAO addresses property marketing
- COO advises on legal issues
