# FHS Newsletter

## Overview
Segmented newsletter publication following a template with business news, team updates, offers, and referral coupons.

## Publication Schedule
- **Segmented**: Daily, Weekly, Monthly
- **Weekly**: Summary of dailies
- **Monthly**: Summary of weeklies

## Newsletter Template

### Content Sections
1. **News about the business** - Latest updates
2. **Info/news about our team** - Personal growth stories
3. **Offers** - Promotions and deals
4. **Referral Coupon** - Loyalty Program member referral tool

### Content Strategy
- Latest things in business/events
- Something personal - growth story
- Something family - relatable content
- Opportunity - new products/services

### Pre-writing
- Pre-write 3 months at a time
- If there is meaning, they will read

## Referral Integration
**Every newsletter will have a Referral Coupon** for Loyalty Program members to easily refer friends.

**Quarterly Rewards**:
- Reward top referrers
- Publish winners in newsletter

**Quarterly Contest**:
- Hold referrals contest
- Publish results in newsletter

## Subscriber Preferences
- Allow customers to choose frequency
- Weekly = summary of dailies
- Monthly = summary of weeklies

## Auto-responders
Upon signup, send 3 auto-emails:
1. Information about FHS
2. Links to articles
3. More articles as content grows

**Note**: Need articles for each target segment.

## Email Strategy

### List Segmentation
- **Segment your list** - Don't broadcast to everyone
- If customer chooses one offer, stop sending other offers
- Layer smart automation on top of offers
- Use multi-media
- Implement auto thank you letters
- Integrate Email + CRM + eCommerce + Affiliate modules

## Compliance
- **Reputation Scores** (SPAM filter monitoring)
- **CAN-SPAM legislation** compliance
