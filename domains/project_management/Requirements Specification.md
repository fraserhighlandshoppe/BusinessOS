# Requirements Specification

## Authorship
Usually written by Business Analyst. Different analysts capture requirements differently.

## Common Formats

### 1. Written Narrative
Series of paragraphs describing different behaviors solution addresses.

**Process**: Once narrative written, decompose into individual requirements.

### 2. Use Cases
Useful for systems performing actions.

**Process**: Analyst sits with users to document step-by-step process requirement addresses.

## Requirements Categories

### 1. Functional Requirements
What the system must do.

### 2. Non Functional Requirements
Quality attributes (performance, security, etc.).

### 3. Interface Requirements
How system interacts with other systems/users.

### 4. Useability Requirements
User experience requirements.

### 5. Other Requirements
Additional constraints, business rules.
