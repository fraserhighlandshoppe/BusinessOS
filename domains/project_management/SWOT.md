# FHS SWOT Analysis

## Overview
SWOT analysis captures strengths/weaknesses within the company and opportunities/threats that face Fraser Highland Shoppe.

## Strengths
- **Low overhead** - Provides excellent margins and pricing flexibility
- **Availability** - Open during waking hours for customer convenience
- **Experience** - Active participation in Piping and Drumming world
- **Experience** - Experience in the Dance world
- **Personal service** - One-on-one customer attention

## Weaknesses
- **Home-based startup** - Difficult to fulfill large order spikes
- **Limited marketing budget** - Low awareness generation capability
- **Outside commitments** - Limited hours for sales vs order fulfillment
- **Low visibility** - Not well-known in target market
- **Industry awareness** - Need to generate awareness for Highland arts
- **Limited cash flow**
- **No local politics experience**
- **Inventory forecasting challenges**
- **No company reputation**
- **Product brand reputation unknown**
- **No market share**
- **Marketing lethargy** - Analysis paralysis

## Opportunities and Trends
- **Underserved industry** - Piping and Drumming market not well served locally
- **No focused competition** - No well-marketed competitors
- **Reduced marketing costs** - Internet and direct marketing cheaper
- **Cyclical industry** - Pipe band community has natural cycles
- **Technology changes** - New tools and methods available
- **Distribution evolution** - New channels emerging
- **Market shifts** - Changing consumer behaviors
- **Product innovation** - New products and solutions

## Threats
- **Industry leaders** - Competition from TartanTown with:
  - Experience and reputation
  - World class pipers as owners
- **Economic downturn** - Reduced discretionary spending
- **Shipping cost increases** - Higher fulfillment costs
- **Internet skepticism** - General concern about online business

## SWOT Analysis Questions
If having difficulties, look at:
1. Company's philosophy or mission?
2. Product features, benefits, quality?
3. Product's competitive advantage?
4. Distribution methods or distributor satisfaction?
5. Pricing structures?
6. Target market's awareness of your product?
7. Target market's attitudes toward the product?
8. Target market's brand loyalty?
9. Competition's activities?
10. Overall market?
