# FHS Mission

## Mission Statement
Fraser Highland Shoppe's mission is to become the go-to highland shoppe for Pipers, Drummers and Dancers in Southern Alberta.

## Detailed Mission
Our mission is to provide complete customer satisfaction to every customer by understanding their wants and needs before selling them a product.

Our products and services will exceed the expectations of our customers.

Offer sales across Canada and around the world!

## Core Values Alignment
Our mission is driven by our core values:
- **Availability** - We're here when you need us
- **Education** - We help you understand your craft
- **Collaboration** - We build relationships
- **Friendship** - We treat you like family
- **Trust** - We take responsibility for our commitments

## Target Audience
- Pipers
- Drummers
- Dancers
- Pipe bands
- Dance schools
- Ex-pats interested in Scottish culture

## Product Categories
- Practice equipment (chanter reeds, practice drums)
- Reeds and accessories
- Giftware
- Highland dress basics (kilts, sporrans, jewelry)
- Books and CDs
- Competition gear
