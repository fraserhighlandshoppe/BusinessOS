# Unified Operating Script

## Overview

This document combines Grant Cardone's 10X methodology with FHS operational processes into a unified business operating system covering the complete customer lifecycle from acquisition to after-sales support.

---

## Phase 1: Client Acquisition (Marketing)

### Grant Cardone Principles
- **Attention First**: Marketing creates awareness that drives action
- **10X Frequency**: Consistent, high-volume outreach
- **Multi-Channel**: Never rely on one form of communication

### FHS Processes (Daily/Weekly)
- Daily Sale posts (social media, email)
- CRM appointment management
- Social media engagement
- Newsletter preparation
- Blog content creation

### Unified Process
1. **Generate Attention** (Daily)
   - Social media posts (FB, IG, TikTok, YouTube)
   - Email campaigns
   - Website/blog content
   - Direct social outreach

2. **Capture Leads** (Daily)
   - CRM entry for all contacts
   - Referral requests
   - Newsletter signups
   - Lead magnet distribution

3. **Nurture Prospects** (Weekly)
   - Follow-up sequences
   - Value-added content
   - Community engagement

---

## Phase 2: Sales Process

### Grant Cardone Sales Cycle (6 Stages)
1. **Idea** - Product solving a problem
2. **Attention** - Marketing generates leads
3. **Intention** - Client interested
4. **Present** - Show solution value
5. **Propose** - Make the offer
6. **Close** - Handle objections, get commitment

### Pre-Qualification (Grant Cardone)
- **Budget**: Do they have authority and resources?
- **Authority**: Are they the decision maker?
- **Need**: Is there a real problem to solve?
- **Timeline**: When do they need it?

### FHS Sales Process Alignment
- **FHS Sales Cycle and Customer Journey** maps to GC stages
- **FHS Sales Objectives** provides targets

### Unified Sales Process
1. **Lead Qualification** (Daily)
   - Review CRM opportunities
   - Pre-qualify using BANT framework
   - Schedule discovery calls

2. **Discovery & Needs Assessment** (Daily)
   - Understand customer goals
   - Identify pain points
   - Determine budget and authority

3. **Solution Presentation** (Daily)
   - Demonstrate product value
   - Show how it solves their problem
   - Address specific use cases

4. **Objection Handling** (Daily)
   - Common objections: price, timing, alternatives
   - Reframe to value and ROI
   - Use 10X mindset: "Would you rather have $1M or 1M friends?"

5. **Closing** (Daily)
   - Make clear offer
   - Handle final objections
   - Secure commitment

## Phase 3: Order Management

### Grant Cardone Principles
- **Speed**: Close deals quickly
- **Action**: Execute immediately
- **Confidence**: Trust the process

### FHS Processes (Daily/Weekly)
- **FHS FA Create Purchase Order**
- **FHS FA Receive Shipment**
- **FHS FA Invoicing**
- **FHS FA Customer Payment**

### Unified Order Process
1. **Order Entry** (Daily)
   - Create quote/invoice in WooCommerce
   - Confirm product availability
   - Set delivery expectations

2. **Payment Processing** (Daily)
   - Collect payment via Square/Credit Card
   - Record transaction in FrontAccounting
   - Update CRM status

3. **Purchase Order Creation** (Weekly)
   - Generate PO for inventory needs
   - Track supplier lead times
   - Monitor order status

---

## Phase 4: Fulfillment

### Grant Cardone Principles
- **Service**: Exceed expectations
- **Support**: Be available when needed
- **Quality**: Never compromise on standards

### FHS Processes (Daily/Weekly/Monthly)
- **Inventory Receiving** (Daily)
- **Product Labeling** (Daily)
- **Inventory Count** (Weekly)
- **Inventory Forecast** (Monthly)

### Unified Fulfillment Process
1. **Inventory Management** (Daily)
   - Receive and label products
   - Update inventory systems
   - Check stock levels

2. **Order Fulfillment** (Daily)
   - Pick and pack orders
   - Generate shipping labels
   - Track deliveries

3. **Quality Assurance** (Daily)
   - Verify product condition
   - Check for completeness
   - Document any issues

---

## Phase 5: After-Sales Support

### Grant Cardone Principles
- **Experience**: Deliver exceptional service
- **Excite**: Keep customers engaged
- **Ascend**: Grow the relationship

### FHS Processes (Daily/Weekly/Monthly)
- **Customer Service** (Daily)
- **Support** (Daily)
- **Documentation** (Ongoing)
- **Warranty/Returns** (Daily)

### Unified Support Process
1. **Customer Communication** (Daily)
   - Follow up on order satisfaction
   - Answer product questions
   - Provide technical support

2. **Documentation** (Weekly)
   - Record support interactions
   - Update knowledge base
   - Create how-to guides

3. **Returns/Exchanges** (As Needed)
   - Validate return requests
   - Process returns per policy
   - Update customer records
