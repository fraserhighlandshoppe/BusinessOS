# FHS 0 Daily Process

## Overview

## Customer Facing

### CRM Management
1. **Log into CRM**
   - Check for appointments
   - CRM meetings:
     - Ask if they know anyone else who might benefit
     - Update NOTE on call result
   - CRM calls:
     - Double check CASL compliance
     - Ask for referrals
     - Update call results

### Follow-up Reminders
- Check CRM home page
- Check CRM calendar
- Check Thunderbird Calendar

### Marketing Campaign Activities
- Daily Sale post (Thunderbird calendar)
- CRM tasks (consider as widget on home screen)

### Follow-up Outstanding Opportunities
- Check CRM Opportunities
- Ask for referrals

### Customer Service
1. **Update customers** on outstanding Sales orders (CRM triggered)
2. **Check outstanding Sales Orders** for ability to fulfill
   - Fulfill where possible
   - Confirm payment
   - Generate invoice/payment receipt/packing slip
3. **Check Delivered Orders** needing invoicing
4. **New Sales Orders**
   - Check emails
   - Check WooCommerce orders
   - Produce quotes or invoices
   - Ask for referrals
   - Place purchase orders

### Returns/Exchanges
- Check emails
- Validate returns policy

## Vendor Facing

### Purchase Orders
- Check outstanding Purchase Orders
- Check for shipping/tracking

### Inventory Receiving
- Generate product labels
- Affix labels and prices

### Vendor Forms
- Fill out Vendor Application forms
- Create vendor directory on G-Drive
- Store vendor documents (trade price lists, etc.)

## Marketing Activities

### Social Media
- Answer social media communications
- Reach out to people who liked page
- Reach out to people who liked posts
- Invite post likers to like page

### Campaign Management
1. Write copy as needed (Newsletter)
2. Review upcoming campaigns
   - Marketing Calendar
   - CRM
   - Mautic
3. Action ongoing campaigns
   - Social Media Posts
   - Paper letters
   - Phone calls

### Daily Sale
1. Check for today's item
2. Create discount or scheduled sale price or coupon
3. Write Blog Post on shop.fraserhighlandshoppe.ca
   - Ensure weekly_sale is category tag
4. Share URL to Social Media
   - Executive summary post linking to announcement
5. Share to Kijiji
