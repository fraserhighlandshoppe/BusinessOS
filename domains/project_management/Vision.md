# FHS Vision

## Vision Statement (30 Seconds)
Drawing from our Core Values, demographics, and mission, our vision is to provide a long-lasting business where customers know they can always come to us for products, service, and support in their pursuit of the Highland arts.

## Internal Vision
This step is "Where we are going" - goals, growth, employees, sales figures.

### Financial Goals
- Extract enough money for basic lifestyle: $25k for Kevin, XX for Kim
- Requires aggressive growth and hiring employees
- Position to pass business to heirs (or sell to outside party)

### Growth Strategy
- Create very solid business model
- Systematic growth through employee development
- Sustainable operations for long-term viability
