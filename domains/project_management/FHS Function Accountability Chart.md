# FHS Function Accountability Chart

## Overview
Organizational accountability chart showing who is responsible for each function, key performance indicators (KPIs), and financial items.

## Main Functions

### Head of Company
- **Accountable**: Kevin S Fraser, Kimberly D Henry
- **KPIs**: Overall business performance, profit margin, growth targets
- **Financial Items**: P&L, Balance Sheet, Cash Flow

### Marketing
- **Accountable**: Kimberly D Henry (PAO)
- **KPIs**: 
  - Website traffic and engagement
  - Customer acquisition rate
  - Newsletter open/click rates
  - Social media engagement
  - Referral program participation
- **Financial Items**: Marketing budget, ROI on marketing spend

### R&D/Innovation
- **Accountable**: Management team
- **KPIs**:
  - New product development cycle
  - Innovation implementation rate
  - Customer feedback on new offerings
- **Financial Items**: R&D budget, innovation ROI

### Sales
- **Accountable**: Kevin S Fraser
- **KPIs**:
  - Revenue growth
  - Customer acquisition
  - Average transaction value
  - Sales conversion rate
  - Repeat customer rate
- **Financial Items**: Sales revenue, commissions, sales expenses

### Operations
- **Accountable**: Kimberly D Henry (COO)
- **KPIs**:
  - Inventory turnover
  - Order fulfillment time
  - Customer satisfaction scores
  - Error rates
- **Financial Items**: COGS, operational expenses, inventory levels

### Treasury
- **Accountable**: Kevin S Fraser (CFO)
- **KPIs**:
  - Cash flow management
  - Accounts receivable turnover
  - Accounts payable timing
  - Bank balance accuracy
- **Financial Items**: Cash, AR, AP, bank accounts, credit lines

### Controller
- **Accountable**: Bookkeeper/Accountant
- **KPIs**:
  - Financial reporting accuracy
  - Month-end close time
  - Compliance with regulations
  - Budget variance tracking
- **Financial Items**: GL accounts, journal entries, financial statements

### IT
- **Accountable**: Technical staff
- **KPIs**:
  - System uptime
  - Response time
  - Security incidents
  - User satisfaction
- **Financial Items**: IT budget, software licenses, hardware

### HR
- **Accountable**: Management team
- **KPIs**:
  - Employee satisfaction
  - Turnover rate
  - Training completion
  - Goal achievement
- **Financial Items**: Payroll, benefits, HR budget

#### Talent Development
- **Accountable**: Managers
- **KPIs**:
  - Training hours per employee
  - Skill development
  - Career progression
  - Employee retention

### Customer Advocacy
- **Accountable**: Customer service team
- **KPIs**:
  - Customer satisfaction scores
  - Complaint resolution time
  - Net Promoter Score (NPS)
  - Referral generation
- **Financial Items**: Customer service costs, retention rates

## Business Units

### Unit 1: Retail Store Operations
- **Accountable**: COO
- **KPIs**: Sales per square foot, inventory turnover, customer traffic
- **Financial Items**: Store revenue, rent, utilities, staffing costs

### Unit 2: Online/Digital Operations
- **Accountable**: PAO
- **KPIs**: Website conversion, digital sales, email engagement
- **Financial Items**: E-commerce revenue, digital marketing costs

## Accountability Principles
1. Clear ownership of each function
2. Measurable KPIs for performance tracking
3. Direct link to financial items
4. Regular review and reporting
5. Alignment with business objectives
