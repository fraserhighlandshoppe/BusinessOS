# Email & Newsletter

Ported from the FHS wiki `FHS Newsletter` and `FHS Marketing Plan` (email
objectives). Platform: **MailPoet on WordPress** (lists not yet set up — see
setup checklist below).

## Strategy

- Segment the list (daily / weekly / monthly). Weekly = summary of dailies;
  monthly = summary of weeklies.
- Let customers choose frequency.
- Every newsletter carries a referral coupon (ties to Loyalty/Referral
  program).
- Reward top referrers quarterly (publish winner in newsletter); run a
  referrals contest quarterly (publish in newsletter).

## Newsletter template

- News about the business
- Info/news about our team
- Offers
- Referral coupon

Tone: something personal (growth, family) so readers relate; something about
opportunities/events; "if there is meaning they will read."

## Auto-responders

On signup, send 3 emails introducing FHS + links to articles. Need articles for
each target segment (pipers, drummers, dancers, studios, gift buyers). Adjust
as more articles are written. Pre-write ~3 months at a time.

## Cadence & content rules

- New blog content weekly.
- Add a video monthly.
- Case studies with customers (blogs/videos).
- Interviews with experts, pipe majors, dance teachers.
- Standing product-line/package pages (≥1 new/month).
- Expand keyword coverage (SEO).

## Setup checklist (MailPoet)

- [ ] Create/list segments (daily, weekly, monthly).
- [ ] Build signup forms (site + social + Games booth).
- [ ] Configure CAN-SPAM / CASL compliance (unsubscribe, sender identity).
- [ ] Import/seed subscriber list from FrontAccounting customers.
- [ ] Build the 3-email welcome sequence.
- [ ] Add referral-coupon block to every template.
- [ ] Monitor reputation/SPAM scores (authentication, clean list).
