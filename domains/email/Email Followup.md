# Email Followup Execution

Source: FHS Wiki
Related: [[The Simple Email Trick That Makes Following Up Effective]]

## Email Followup Framework

### 1. Define Your Dream Client
Identify your ideal customer profile and what they truly need.

### 2. Know Your Follow-Up Goal
Set clear desired outcomes for both you AND the client.

### 3. Opt-In Incentives
Give your dream client what she wants and needs.

### 4. Map Your Funnel
Plan the customer journey from first contact to conversion.

### 5. Balance Information and Offers
Don't overwhelm - provide value before asking for action.

### 6. Write Clickworthy Subject Lines
- Not Spammy
- Not Generic
- Answer "So What?"
- Be Specific (e.g., "123 XYZ did abc")

### 7. Personalized Greeting
- Never apologize - shows low confidence
- Do your homework - don't write "I am under the impression that..."
- Be specific

### 8. Write Conversationally
- Make it as if they are one side of a conversation
- Speak as if in person

### 9. Crafting Compelling Calls to Action
- Crystal Clear: "click here to XYZ" or list steps
- Single, focused action per email

### 10. Put Campaigns on Autopilot
- Use mailing lists or autoresponders
- Systematize follow-up sequences

### 11. Test, Track, Tweak & Improve
- Measure open rates, click-through rates
- Optimize based on data

## Additional Follow-Up Tactics

### Mini Product Creation
Create low-cost products by:
- Recording video or webinar training
- Include with workbook and checklist
- Sell to clients

### Group Coaching Value Boost
- Create training library for members
- Include in monthly group coaching program

### Lead Page Strategy
- Create lead page giving away content
- Promote via Facebook ads
- Grow mailing list
