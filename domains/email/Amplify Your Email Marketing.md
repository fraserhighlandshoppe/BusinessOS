# 5 Free Tools You Can Use to Amplify Your Email Marketing

## A/B Significance Tester
Split testing helps determine what works for your audience. Use Kissmetrics free tool to check if results are statistically significant.

## Bulletproof Email Buttons
- Responsive CTAs increase earnings per click by 38%
- Use Campaign Monitor's free tool (no coding required)
- Pro tip: Increase font-size from 13px to desired value

## GifRocket
- Mac-only tool for creating GIFs from video
- No graphic artist needed
- Takes under 2 minutes to film and convert
- Combine with smartphone camera for magical results

## Headline Analyzer
- CoSchedule's free tool to evaluate subject lines
- Scores emotional response and power
- Helps decide if headline will get clicks
- Take advice with a grain of salt (can be overly critical of short lines)

## Sender Score
- Technical tool to check email sender reputation
- Free from ReturnPath
- Shows how mailbox providers view your emails
- Critical for inbox delivery

## Subject Line Strategies

### Highlighting Pain in Subject Line
- "This is why your prospects aren't buying" beats curiosity headlines
- Pain-based headlines drive more clicks (18% lift in test)
- Curiosity headlines drive opens but may not convert
- Use direct, pain-based subject lines for complex/expensive offers

### Use CSS Buttons in Email
- Mobile optimization is critical
- Buttons beat text hyperlinks
- 22% higher click-through rate
- 38% higher earnings per click
