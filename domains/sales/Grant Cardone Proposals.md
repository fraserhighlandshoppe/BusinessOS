# Grant Cardone Proposals

## Present in Writing. Communicate Clearly.

**Reference**: [Cardone University - 5 Rules](https://cardoneuniversity.com/5-rules-to-hack-your-sales-game/)

## Key Principles

### People Believe What They See
Actions speak louder than words. Written proposals are more credible and valued.

### Talk is Cheap
Always have contract and legal pad.
- Anything of value should be written down
- Validated in writing
- Build case of logic for why to do business with you

## Proposal Preparation

### Rehearse
- Clearly communicate offer
- Include benefits
- Include presentation items

### Delivery
- Don't cover mouth
- Speak clearly and confidently
- Suggest optimism, pride, credibility

## Proposal Elements
1. **Offer** - Clear value proposition
2. **Benefits** - Why they should buy
3. **Inclusions** - What's included
