# Grant Cardone Sales System Mistakes

## Key Mistakes to Avoid

### #1 Wrong Target
Not targeting correctly.

### #2 Over Complicate the Solution
Keep it simple.

### #3 No Strategy
Need clear strategy.

### #4 One Man Show
**Statistics**: 64% of 1-man shows don't break even.

**Solution**: Build team, delegate, scale.

### #5 Wrong Information
Bad data leads to bad decisions.

### #6 No Sales
If you're not selling, you're not in business.

### #7 No Marketing
Sales without marketing is unsustainable.

### #8 Wrong Priorities
Focus on revenue, not activity.

## Prevention Strategy
1. Define clear target
2. Keep solution simple
3. Have strategy
4. Build team early
5. Get accurate information
6. Always be selling
7. Always be marketing
8. Prioritize revenue
