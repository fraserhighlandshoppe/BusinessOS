# Grant Cardone Sales Tips

## Get Your Mindset Right

**CREATIVITY FOLLOWS COMMITMENT**

Once you're all in, magic happens:
- Get interesting ideas to fine-tune sales skills
- Insert personality into sales process
- Inspiration strikes

**Requirement**: 100% sold on product, company, selling as career.

## Value Your Product More Than Money

Money motivates initially, not long term.
**Purpose-driven** = greatness at anything.

**Conviction** differentiates beginner's luck from long-term success.

**Drop in production** = uncertainty in what you're selling.

**Resell yourself** on what you offer when sales dip.
**Be unreasonable** about conviction.

## Don't Use "Tricks" in 2022

Tricks are for kids, not salespeople.

**Rule of thumb**: If you wouldn't advertise it, don't do it.

If tempted to use methods, you're not 100% sold - revisit conviction.

## Make a List

Selling = helping others with what you offer.

### List Items:
1. **Why people should choose your product**
   - How product helps customer

2. **Advantages of buying from your company**
   - What company offers competitors don't

3. **Benefits of controlling revenue as salesperson**
   - What you bring no other salesperson does

**Revisit list** when facing roadblock or losing deal.

## Understand the Sales Cycle Inside-Out

**7-Step Sales Cycle**:
1. Ensure your idea is GREAT
2. Greet the customer
3. State your intention for interaction
4. Qualify buyer's wants and needs
5. Present product or service
6. Put forth sales presentation
7. Close the deal

**Knowledge backwards and forwards** = flexible, comfortable, confident.

## Nail Down Your Sales Pitch

**Less than 2%** of deals made on first call/meeting.

**PITCHING IS NOT SELLING**

Pitch = get attention, determine opportunity.
Not = close deal.

**Goal**: Fill pipeline.

Great pitch = remembered when industry need arises.

**No universal pitch** - too many variables.
