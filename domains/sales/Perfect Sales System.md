# The Perfect Sales Process

Reference: [LinkedIn Article](https://www.linkedin.com/pulse/perfect-sales-process-grant-cardone)

## Why Change the Sales Process?
- People have less time
- More access to information
- Both husband and wife more likely employed
- Generations buy without human engagement

## The Perfect Sales Process Must Be:
- Fast and easy for buyer
- Easy and effective for salesperson
- Provide quick, credible info to buyer
- Treat buyer as informed
- Satisfy customer and company by consummating sale

## Why Shorten and Simplify?
Buyers are sensitive to time:
- "How long will I be there?"
- "Am I getting stuck with slow salesperson?"

## The 5 Essential Steps

### 1. Greet
**Goal**: Introduce, make good impression, put buyer at ease.

Set stage for remaining steps. Don't waste time if no relationship. Make great impression if unknown. Avoid small talk if known.

**Example**: "Welcome. Thank you for coming here. What can I get you information on?"

### 2. Determine Wants and Needs
**Method**: Fact finding on previous purchases OR consultation

**Purpose**:
1. Know what product to show
2. Build value in presentation

**Key Insight**: All purchases solve a problem. Don't buy drill - buy hole.

### 3. Select Product and Present/Build Value
Select for client rather than letting them wander inventory.

Present based on what client told you is important.

**Examples**:
- Insurance: Select based on needs found
- Surgery, car, furniture, club membership: Present solution

**Water example**: Choking man doesn't need crystal quality - just sell drink!

### 4. Make a Proposal
**Always make a proposal** - even if not ready.

Don't present without figures. Aggressively get to figures with every buyer.

### 5. Close or Exit
**Goal**: Find out how much game you have.

**Must be prepared to CLOSE** - different art from selling.

**Key Points**:
- Everyone born to sell, but must learn to close
- Professional salespeople demand help in closing
- Need hundreds of closes, not just a few
- Need fresh, innovative ways for every situation
