# Grant Cardone Prospecting Tips

## Lead Types
### Cold Leads
- Don't know you, you don't know them
- Least likely to generate prospects
- Avoid - feeds telemarketers, bad reputation

### Cold Targeted Leads
- Don't know you, but fit ideal customer profile
- Area where all-star salespeople shine
- Warm them up to become buyers

### Warm Leads
- Know you and familiar with business
- Past customers, referrals, inbound leads
- Customers who come for service (not purchase)

### Hot Leads
- Well acquainted or very well acquainted
- Know product and want to buy
- Need someone to close

## Strategy
Have **unique script for each lead type**. Pay special attention to cold targeted leads - become lifeblood of outbound lead generation.

## Prospecting vs Sales

**Prospecting is NOT sales**:
- Not about getting "yes" or closing deals
- About getting out of obscurity
- Rekindling relationships
- Nurturing relationships

**Win in prospecting**: Gather info, schedule next contact - that's a victory.

**Problem**: Expecting immediate sale leads to disappointment and quitting.

## World-Class Prospecting Attitude

### Community, Giving, Service
- 80% of content should be free
- 20% is where money is
- Free content gets you close to leads

### Service-Oriented Calls
- Free advice
- Free trial
- Free webinar
- "Checking in to see if I can help"
- Offering free seminar on competitor's product

### Resilience and Indifference
- Expect rejection, negativity, hate
- Let it roll off your back
- Take it with a smile

## Do the Math
Every call is a victory if you know the numbers.

### The Process
1. How many calls to set appointment?
2. How many appointments for demo?
3. How many signups show up?
4. How many contracts needed to close deal?
5. How many contracts before closing?

**Result**: Reverse engineer science of generating single deal. Turn prospecting into a game.

## Prospect Daily
Prospecting is life/salvation of business future.

**Inflow proportional to outflow**: Give enough, get enough.

### Daily Practice
- Set aside sacred time
- Preferably first hours of day
- "Call avoidance" is deadly poison
- Antidote: action
- Crank out calls relentlessly to fill pipeline
