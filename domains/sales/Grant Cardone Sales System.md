# Grant Cardone Sales System

## Requirements for Success
1. Intention
2. Heart
3. Mind
4. Actions
5. Target

## Sales Strategy

### 1. Idea / Target
Identify your target market and solution.

### 2. Attention (Marketing)
Promoting and selling products and services.

### 3. Leads
Generate prospects interested in your offering.

### 4. Manage Activity
Systematic approach to sales activities.

### 5. Sales (Monetization)
Closing deals and generating revenue.

### 6. Manage Revenue
Track and optimize revenue streams.

### 7. Scale
Grow and expand your business operations.

## Key Principle
All successful activities require precise formulas - otherwise it's just luck.
