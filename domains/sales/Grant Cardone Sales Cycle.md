# Grant Cardone Sales Cycle

## Goal
Shorten the cycle as much as possible.

## Sales Cycle Stages

### 1. Idea
- Come up with product
- Must be solution to someone's problem

### 2. Attention
- Advertise. Marketing.
- Generate LEADS

### 3. Intention
- Know in your mind what your intention is
- Intention: Get client to purchase your product

### 4. Present
- Many salespeople reach presentation but never propose
- Some want deep details, most don't
- Only talk long enough to sell
- Don't talk yourself out of sale

### 5. Propose
- MUST propose solution
- Client won't hand you cash

**See**: [[Grant Cardone Proposals]]

### 6. Close
- Objection handling
- Practice during training time
- **DON'T practice during close**
- Shouldn't be surprised by objection
- Objections you walk in prepared to handle

### The 2 Real Objections
1. They aren't the decision maker
2. They don't think it will work
