### Implementation Sequence:
1. **Identify Tripwire Product** - Low-cost ($7-$47), high-value offer
2. **Create Lead Magnet** - Free content that leads naturally to tripwire
3. **Build Landing Page** - Optimized for tripwire conversion
4. **Set Up Email Sequence** - Immediate delivery + nurture sequence
5. **Configure Payment Processing** - Simple checkout flow
6. **Implement Upsell Path** - Core offer presentation post-purchase

## Key Principles

### 1. Tripwire Product Design
- **Price Point**: $7-$47 (impulse buy threshold)
- **High Perceived Value**: 10x+ value vs price
- **Immediate Delivery**: Digital product for instant gratification
- **Natural Progression**: Leads logically to core offer

### 2. Lead Magnet to Tripwire Bridge
- **Content Alignment**: Lead magnet solves problem A, tripwire solves problem B
- **Consumption Mechanics**: Lead magnet creates desire for tripwire solution
- **Trust Building**: Demonstrate expertise before asking for payment

### 3. Conversion Optimization
- **Page Elements**: Headline, benefit bullets, social proof, guarantee, CTA
- **Order Form**: Minimal fields, trust badges, clear pricing
- **Thank You Page**: Immediate access + core offer introduction

### 4. Email Follow-Up Sequence
- **Email 1** (Immediate): Delivery + consumption instructions
- **Email 2** (Day 1): Case study / success story
- **Email 3** (Day 3): FAQ / objection handling
- **Email 4** (Day 5): Core offer soft pitch
- **Email 5** (Day 7): Direct core offer presentation

## Metrics & KPIs
- **Tripwire Conversion Rate**: Target 5-15% of leads
- **Average Order Value**: Tripwire price + upsell take rate
- **Lead-to-Customer**: Percentage becoming paying customers
- **Core Offer Conversion**: Tripwire buyers → core offer buyers
- **ROAS**: Return on ad spend for cold traffic to tripwire
