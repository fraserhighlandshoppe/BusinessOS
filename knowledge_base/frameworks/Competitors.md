## Detail

### The Highland Shoppe (Calgary) — HIGH threat
Online-only. Full Highland Dress, Celtic rings/jewelry, Bagpipes & Accessories
(Peter Henderson, MacRae, McCallum, Pipers Choice, electronic/small pipes),
"Bagpipes In Stock" (configured/tested), bagpipe repairs, giftware, gift
certificates. Blog active into 2025 (piping schools, kilt fitting). Social:
Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, YouTube. This is now FHS's closest direct
competitor in piping/drumming — the 2017 wiki understated them.

### The Scottish Shoppe (Calgary) — MEDIUM
Established 1971. Scottish/Irish/English/Welsh/Celtic: kilts, jewelry, food,
clan items, books, giftware, rentals. Some piping but clothing/grocery-led.
Free Calgary delivery $30+. Facebook only. Word-of-street: higher priced.
Opportunity to compete on price, product, availability.

### Tartantown (Vancouver) — MEDIUM (distance)
Canada's marquee pipe/dance/drum shop. Lee & Sons bags, Naill, McCallum,
MacRae, Henderson; deep dance outfits; strong customer reviews; blog; FB/IG.
Physically distant but a national mail-order powerhouse.

### Celtic Pipe Line (Calgary) — LOW / UNCERTAIN
celticpipeline.com returns 404 as of 2026. Formerly a part-time/home piping
business with good community ties but light stock. Treat as inactive unless
proven otherwise.

### Kinnaird Bagpipes (Saskatoon, SK) — MEDIUM (distance)
923 Emmeline Terrace, Saskatoon, SK S7J 5G7
Phone: 1-866-249-2921 | sales@kinnairdbagpipes.com
Family-owned since 2000. Specializes in bagpipe reeds and accessories (Piper's Pal, reed storage, blowing accessories). International shipping, deep technical expertise for pipers. Active on Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, Pinterest.

## Highland Dance Competition

Highland dance is primarily served through:
- **Facebook Groups**: "Highland Dance Canada", "Scottish Highland Games" groups
- **Instagram**: @highlanddancecanada, @scottishhighlanddance, individual teachers
- **Competition listings**: HighlandGames.com, game-specific programs
- **Teachers**: Often run their own small retail operations or recommend suppliers

Key dance-specific suppliers (need research):
- Dance outfit makers (kilts, ghillies, garters)
- Competition shoes/attire
- Music instruction materials

FHS opportunity: Dance inventory is deep, but marketing to dance community requires different channels (FB groups, teacher relationships) than piping/drumming.

**Research notes (2026-07-17):**
- Tartantown (Vancouver) has deepest dance catalog alongside piping/drums
- The Highland Shoppe carries dance kilts, sporrans, accessories
- No dedicated dance-only retailers identified in Canadian market
- FHS should target dance teachers via FB groups and IG hashtags (#highlanddance, #scottishhighlanddance)

## FHS competitive response (from wiki + research)

- Avoid competition via a strong USP (practitioner ownership + availability).
- Reduce competition via pricing, customer service, USP.
- Out-tech / out-content / out-social better-funded competitors — viable since
  they're content-active but FHS has owner authority (Pipe Major Kevin) they
  can't copy.
- Keep items in stock that competitors don't (availability is a documented FHS
  strength).
