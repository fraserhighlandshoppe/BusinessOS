# Content-to-Payoff Funnel Framework

## Overview

The Content-to-Payoff Funnel is a systematic 72-hour system for creating, optimizing, and distributing high-converting content efficiently.

## Strategic Implementation

### Phase 1: Content Planning (Day 1)
"Create a 60-second editorial plan using the Digital Marketer's Content Planner template."

### Phase 2: Content Creation (Days 1-2)
"Execute the editorial calendar using blog post templates for 7 content types:
- Crowdsourced Post
- Content Aggregator
- Interview
- Quote Post
- Embed Reactor
- Stat Round Up
- YouTube Cut Up
"

### Phase 3: Content Distribution (Days 2-3)
"Implement the Content Distribution Matrix:

#### Email List Marketing
- Send to subscribers to keep list happy and healthy
- Use for Lead Magnet opt-ins and low-dollar offers
- Segment list and retarget with ads based on known interests

#### Social Media
- Share on relevant platforms with Platform-Specific Content Mix
- Use WCA targeting for optimal performance

#### Website Traffic
- Ascend traffic to conversion points using SEO for WordPress
- Optimize landing pages with 16-Point Checklist
"

### Phase 4: Content Repurposing (Days 3-7)
"7-Day Content Repurposing System:
- [ ] Convert blog posts into downloadable Lead Magnets
- [ ] Cross-link relevant content
- [ ] Adapt for email sequence
- [ ] Transform into video content
- [ ] Create infographics and charts
- [ ] Develop social media snippets
- [ ] Build email campaign sequences
"
