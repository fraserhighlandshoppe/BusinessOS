## Page Layout (Tests 1-8)

### 1. Clear Visual Hierarchy
- Ensure page answers: who you are, what you do, next action
- Flow: images → large text → action area

### 2. Above-the-Fold Priority
- Place critical info/CTA above fold (598% click lift documented)

### 3. CTA Contrast & Visual Cues
- Use arrows/contrasting colors (12% conversion lift)

### 4. Visual Cues (Arrows)
- Guide attention to CTAs (12% bump potential)

### 5. Avoid Sliders
- Remove auto-slide; compress images; increase time if required (4.32% lift)

### 6. Well Constructed Headlines
- Formats: "How to ___", "Who Else Wants ___?", "[Number] Simple Steps"
- Negative slant headlines: +20% bump ("rubberneck effect")

### 7. Involvement Devices (Quizzes/Polls)
- Include "see results below" text
- Placement encourages reading (24% higher conversion)

### 8. Triggered Popups
- Exit-intent pop ups (2,689 leads in 14 days)
- Scroll-based pops (300% increase in opt-ins)

## Images (Tests 9-13)

### 9. Use REAL People
- Use author/instructor/webinar host image
- Big lift in webinar opt-in rates

### 10. Avoid Unedited Stock Photos
- Edit with Canva/Pixlr for custom feel

### 11. Image Captions
- Add text CTA in caption (most read copy on page)

### 12. Images Point to CTAs
- Align gaze/attention direction toward CTA

### 13. Minimal Copy on Images
- Use HTML buttons instead
- Reduces load times, avoids SEO issues

## Videos (Tests 14-21)

### 14. No Video Fakeouts
- Don't use non-functional video placeholders

### 15. VSL Basics
- Replace body copy with video sales letter

### 16. Video Length
- Sweet spot: 12-24 minutes (offer size dependent)

### 17. Remove Video Controls
- Autoplay, no fast-forward/skip options

### 18. Pause When Page Inactive
- Hold attention through page-blur detection

### 19. Time-Triggered CTA
- Reveal buy button on cue (doubles conversions documented)

### 20. Autoplay Muted
- Reduce procrastination; follow Facebook/YouTube standard

### 21. Trigger Long-Form Sales Letter with CTA
- Load full sales letter below VSL for skeptical visitors

## Sales Pages (Tests 22-26)

### 22. Fonts Matter
- San-serif fonts increase conversions (+30%)
- Arial, 12pt font or larger for readability

### 23. Reiterate CTAs
- Insert CTAs in hot scroll zones (not just top/bottom)

### 24. Testimonials & Social Proof
- Place success stories in order forms (+33% lift)
- Real customer testimonials essential

### 25. Visual Deliverables
- Professional product shots (+145% impact)
- Must match offer type exactly

### 26. Strong Ad Scent
- Landing page imagery/copy must match ad clicked
- Avoid disconnect between expectation and delivery
