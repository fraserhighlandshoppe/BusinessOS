## Implementation Sequence:
1. **Account Setup** - Legal entity and financial requirements
2. **Inventory Preparation** - Product codes and asset creation
3. **Listing Configuration** - Category association and optimization
4. **Shipping Integration** - Carrier setup and tax configuration
5. **Policy Documentation** - Customer service and returns policies

## Key Principles

### Required Documentation
- **VAT Registration**: Mandatory for EU sales compliance
- **Bank Account**: UK/EU-based account for disbursements
- **Legal Entity**: Registered company details verification
- **Payment Credentials**: Credit card authorization for fees

### Product Asset Requirements
- **Barcode Standards**: EAN/UPC codes for product identification
- **Image Specifications**: Pure white background, no watermarks
- **Title Optimization**: Clear, keyword-rich product descriptions
- **Feature Bullet Points**: Key selling points clearly articulated

### Marketplace Checklist
- [ ] Seller Central account created and verified
- [ ] Legal company details entered correctly
- [ ] VAT number submitted and validated
- [ ] Bank account information configured
- [ ] Credit card on file for transaction fees
- [ ] Shipping rates configured per carrier
- [ ] Return and refund policies documented
- [ ] Product inventory staged with codes/images
