# Cold Prospecting Framework

## Core Principle
NEVER RELY on 1 Form of Communication - adopt an omnippresent approach across multiple channels

## Key Benefits
- Cost Effective
- Preemptive customer acquisition
- Targeted prospect engagement
- Establishes New Relationships
- Empowers Sales Team with pipeline control

## Prospecting Cadence
1. **Frequency > Perfection** - Consistent outreach beats perfect preparation
2. **Persistence is Key** - Multiple touches required for response
3. **Follow a Process** - Consistent methodology every time

## Communication Channels
- Cold Call
- Cold Email
- LinkedIn Outreach
- Text Message
- Personal Visit
- Handwritten Letter

## Golden Rules
• Always be Honest, Transparent, and Straight Forward
• Be Persistent
• Identify and Solve Problems
• Be Respectful
• Be Memorable
• Be Credible - Use 3rd Party Information to Validate Claims

## Prospecting Process
1. **Intro** - Make initial contact
2. **Reason/Big Claim** - State clear value proposition
3. **Qualify/Fact Find** - Discover needs and qualify prospects
4. **Close** - Move toward conversion

## Communication Tips
- Honest, Transparent, Straight Forward messaging
- Consistent Persistence across all channels
- Problem-Solving approach in outreach
- Respectful tone - never give up on follow-up
- Build Credibility with third-party validation

## Process Integration
This framework fits into the 6/3/1 traffic system as the foundation for:
- Cold traffic acquisition ( $6 budget allocation)
- Lead generation for warm traffic nurturing
- Foundation for sales pipeline development
