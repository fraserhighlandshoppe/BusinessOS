## Implementation Sequence:
1. **Pre-Client Qualification** - Assess fit and readiness
2. **Intake Questionnaire** - Gather business goals and challenges
3. **Onboarding Process** - Structured client setup
4. **Relationship Management** - Long-term value delivery

## Key Principles

### Onboarding Value
- **Early Relationship Building**: Capture client goals while motivation is high
- **Strategic Discovery**: Learn if initial direction is correct
- **Expectation Setting**: Define success metrics upfront
- **Long-Term Retention**: Foundation for 6-12 month relationship

### Questionnaire Design
- **Business Context**: Company history, mission, current state
- **Goals & Objectives**: Measurable outcomes expected
- **Challenges & Pain Points**: Current obstacles to address
- **Budget & Resources**: Available investment capacity
- **Timeline**: Implementation urgency and milestones
- **Stakeholder Map**: Key decision makers and influencers
- **Prior Experience**: Previous agency/marketing attempts

### Framework Usage
- **Agencies**: Standardize new client onboarding
- **Consultants**: Qualify prospects before proposals
- **In-House Teams**: Structured audit process

- [ ] Intake form deployed to all prospects
- [ ] Questionnaire questions finalized
- [ ] Scoring criteria established
- [ ] Onboarding workflow documented
- [ ] Account manager assigned per new client
- [ ] Success metrics defined and communicated
- [ ] Quarterly review cadence established

## Metrics & KPIs
- **Client Retention Rate**: % remaining after 12 months
- **Time to Onboard**: Days from signature to first deliverable
- **Client Satisfaction**: Post-onboarding survey scores
- **Revenue Per Client**: Average lifetime value
- **Referral Rate**: % clients referring new business
