## Implementation Sequence:
1. **Social Listening** - Monitor brand mentions and customer feedback
2. **Social Networking** - Connect with authority figures and influencers ([[Social_Media_Mindmaps.md]])
3. **Social Influencing** - Establish authority through valuable content sharing ([[Content_Strategy_Framework]])
4. **Social Selling** - Generate leads/sales from existing audience

## Key Principles

### Sequential Social Engagement
- **Phase 1**: Social Listening (Monitor/Measure)
- **Phase 2**: Social Networking (Connect/Build)
- **Phase 3**: Social Influencing (Establish Authority)
- **Phase 4**: Social Selling (Convert/Sell)

### Integration Points
- **Content Engine**: [[Social_Media_Mindmaps.md]] provides content calendar
- **Audit System**: [[Digital_Marketer_10_Minute_Social_Audit.md]] validates execution
- **Engagement Tracking**: Instagram planner tool tracks implementation ([[Digital_Marketer_Instagram_Planner_and_Audit_Tool.md]])

## Framework Questions

### Social Listening
- What brand mentions exist online?
- How are customer service issues identified?
- What market sentiment can be measured?

### Social Networking
- Who are authoritative influencers in your niche?
- Which brands align with your values?
- How do you establish professional connections? ([[Content_Strategy_Framework]])

### Social Influencing
- What content demonstrates expertise?
- When to share vs create original content?
- How to build trust through value distribution?

### Social Selling
- Where to guide customers in buying process?
- How to convert social engagement into sales?
- What objections need handling? ([[Digital_Marketer_Plug_Leaky_Sales_Funnel]])
