### Fill-in-the-Blank Formulas

1. **How To [Accomplish Something] In [Short Amount of Time]**
   - "How To Craft High-Converting Headlines In Less Than 60 Seconds"

2. **How To [Receive Benefit] Without [Undesired Expenditure]**
   - "How To Get A-List Copy Without The Massive Price Tag"

3. **How To [Accomplish/Benefit] In [Short Time] Without [Expenditure]**
   - "How To Land Higher Dollar Clients In The Next Hour Without Spending A Penny"

4. **The Unbelievably Easy Way To [Accomplish Something]**
   - "The Unbelievably Easy Way To Land 10 New Clients Today"

5. **The Guaranteed Method For Avoiding [Common Mistake]**
   - "The Guaranteed Method For Avoiding Student Debt"

6. **10 Things [Something Interesting] Says About You**
   - "10 Things Owning A Business Says About You"

7. **10 Little-Known Ways To [Accomplish Something]**
   - "10 Little-Known Ways To Land New Clients"

8. **How To [Accomplish Something] Like [Famous Example]**
   - "How To Pick Up Clients Like Neil Patel"

9. **The Scientifically Proven Formula For [Accomplishing Something]**
   - "The Scientifically Proven Formula For Landing High-Dollar Clients"

10. **Is [Something Most People Do] Causing [Something Bad]?**

## Social Media Scripts

### 3 Message Formula (Cold Outreach)

**Message #1 - Relationship Building**
```
"Hey John, what's up? I noticed we're friends here on Facebook and we haven't chatted for a while. I noticed that your profile says you're living up in New York. Is that correct? You up in NY?"
```

**Message #2 - Value Proposition**
```
"John's that's awesome, I visited New York for the first time last September and I loved it. I was actually hoping you said you were still there, because I have a business project that I'm expanding and I'm doing some networking in your neck of the woods. Would you at all be open to connecting more regarding a side income project, if it didn't interfere with what you're already doing?"
```

**Message #3 - Meeting Setup**
```
"That's great. Shoot me a few times that you're free this week for about 15 mins and can have access to the internet. Tuesday and Thursday are the best for me if you can make something work either of those days."
```

## Copywriting Steps

1. **Choose Subject** - Audience and their transformation
2. **Choose Customer Avatar** - Define target
3. **Choose Pain Point** - What problem to address
4. **Choose Lifecycle Aim** - Reach, Act, Convert, Engage
5. **Choose Method** - Entertain, Educate, Inspire, Convince
6. **Write Copy** - Hook, Headline, Story, Testimonial, CTA
7. **Validate** - Check against checklists

## Social Media Post Formula (4-Part)

1. **Hook** - Captivating first sentence
2. **Before and After Story** - Life before vs. after (don't mention product name)
3. **Take Away** - Non-salesy insight, removes resistance
4. **Call To Action** - Ask for private message (never link to website)
