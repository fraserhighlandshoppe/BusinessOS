## Implementation Sequence:
1. **Review Top 101 Lines** – Categorize by intent (sales, engagement, urgency).
2. **Test in Campaign** – Run A/B tests across segments.
3. **Measure Results** – Track open rates, click‑through rates, conversions.
4icions.**Iterate** – Refine based on data.

## Key Principles
- **Relevance** – Align subject with email content.
- **Personalization** – Use dynamic tags where possible.
- **Urgency** – Trigger action.

## Metrics & KPIs
- **Openmassage** – Win rate compared to baseline.
- **Click‑through** – Correlate with conversion.
- **Revenue Lift** – Quantify impactäter.
