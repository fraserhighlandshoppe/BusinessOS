## 16-Point Landing Page Checklist

### Section 2: Checklist Items

| # | Element | Optimization Rule | Reference |
|---|---------|-------------------|-----------|
| 1 | Market Callout | Direct/indirect audience targeting | [[Content_Strategy_Framework]] |
| 2 | Clear & Concise | Single message + single offer | [[Funnel_Building_Framework]] |
| 3 | Easily Understood | 5-second comprehension test | [[5_Step_Sales_Funnel_Framework]] |
| 4 | Compelling Headline | Benefit-rich, grabs attention | [[Content_Strategy_Framework]] |
| 5 | CTA Above Fold | Visible without scrolling | [[Profit_Maximizer_Framework]] |
| 6 | Contrasting Button Color | Stand out from design elements | [[Sales_Page_Optimization_Checklist.md]] |
| 7 | Custom Button Text | Specific command/result (e.g., "Free Instant Access") | [[A_B_Testing_Methodology]] |
| 8 | Social Proof | Share icons, "As Seen On", testimonials | [[Profit_Maximizer_Framework]] |
| 9 | Limited Navigation | No distraction links (except maybe affiliate) | [[Funnel_Building_Framework]] |
| 10 | Visual Cues | Arrows, boxes directing to CTA | [[49_Split_Tests_Conversion_Framework.md]] |
| 11 | Hero Shot | Image/graphical representation of offer | [[Content_Strategy_Framework]] |
| 12 | Limited Form Fields | Only essential info (test dropping name) | [[Tripwire_Offer_Framework]] |
| 13 | Source Congruency | Match ad text/imagery exactly | [[A_B_Testing_Methodology]] |
| 14 | Brand Consistency | Maintain overall brand look/feel | [[Funnel_Building_Framework]] |
| 15 | Enable Sharing | Obvious social share buttons | [[Content_Strategy_Framework]] |
| 16 | Visible Privacy/TOS | Required for Google ads + trust | [[Profit_Maximizer_Framework]] |
