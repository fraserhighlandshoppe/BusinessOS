## Implementation Sequence:
1. **Identify Survey Goal** – Define objective (customer satisfaction, product feedback).
2. **Select Best Questions** – Use top 10 curated questions from DigitalMarketer.
3. **Deploy Survey** – Insert into email, website, or chatbot.
4. **Analyze Results** – Capture insights and map to action items.

## Key Principles
- **Clarity** – Keep questions short and unambiguous.
- **Relevance** – Align with the specific product or service.
- **Actionability** – Ensure each answer drives a concrete change.

## Checklist
- [ ] Survey goal defined.
- [ ] 10 questions compiled.
- [ ] Survey tool configured.
- [ ] Response analysis plan established.

## Metrics & KPIs
- **Response Rate** – % of recipients who complete.
- **Net Promoter Score** – Derived from recommended scoring.
- **Insight Action Rate** – % of insights leading to changes.
