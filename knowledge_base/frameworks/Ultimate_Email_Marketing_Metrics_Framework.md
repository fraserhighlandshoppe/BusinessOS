## Implementation Sequence:
1. **Deliverability Check** - Ensure inbox placement
2. **Open Rate Optimization** - Subject line and sender name testing
3. **Click-Through Rate Enhancement** - Content and CTA improvement
4. **Conversion Rate Tracking** - Revenue attribution and ROI calculation
5. **Deliverability Maintenance** - Ongoing list hygiene and sender reputation

## Key Principles

### The Four Universal Email Marketing Metrics
1. **Deliverability Rate** - Percentage reaching inbox (vs spam)
   - Monitor: Spam complaints, bounce rates, sender reputation
   - Optimization: List hygiene, authentication (SPF/DKIM/DMARC), content quality
   - Benchmark: > 95% deliverability

2. **Open Rate** - Percentage opening delivered emails
   - Formula: (Opened ÷ Delivered) × 100
   - Optimization: Subject lines, sender name, send time, preheader text
   - Benchmark: Industry average 20-30%, target 35%+

3. **Click-Through Rate (CTR)** - Percentage clicking links in opened emails
   - Formula: (Clicks ÷ Opened) × 100
   - Optimization: Button CTAs, link placement, value proposition clarity
   - Benchmark: Industry average 2-5%, target 7%+

4. **Conversion Rate** - Percentage completing desired action
   - Formula: (Conversions ÷ Clicks) × 100  
   - Optimization: Landing page alignment, offer relevance, friction reduction
   - Benchmark: Varies by offer, target 10%+ for email-sourced traffic
