# Grant Cardone Wealth Creation Formula

## Core Philosophy
Income is the foundation of wealth. You cannot become wealthy without first increasing income.

## The Formula

```
Wealth = Income × Number of Income Streams × Investment × Savings
```

## Step 1: Create Income

### Key Principle
> You cannot become wealthy without first increasing income.

### How to Create Income Today
1. **Have a target income goal** - Define what wealth means to you
2. **Don't wait for the "right time"** - Start now, even from zero
3. **Income is the entry point** - No way around it

### Income Sources
- Job income
- Investment income (dividends, interest)
- Business income
- Sales income
- Commission income

### Key Takeaway
> College is not the way to start your path to wealth. Income generation is.

## Step 2: Increase Income Streams

### Strategy
Create multiple streams of income to multiply wealth potential.

### Types of Income Streams
1. **Active Income** - Money earned from direct work
2. **Passive Income** - Money earned without direct work
3. **Portfolio Income** - Income from investments

### Multiplication Effect
- One income stream: Linear growth
- Multiple streams: Exponential growth

## Step 3: Invest

### Investment Principles
1. **Invest early** - Time is your greatest ally
2. **Invest consistently** - Regular contributions compound
3. **Invest wisely** - Focus on assets that generate income

### Investment Vehicles
- Real estate
- Stocks and bonds
- Business ventures
- Intellectual property

### Key Principle
> Invest in assets that produce income, not just appreciation.

## Step 4: Save

### Savings Strategy
1. **Save a percentage of income** - Minimum 10%
2. **Pay yourself first** - Treat savings as a non-negotiable expense
3. **Increase savings rate** - As income grows, save more

### The Savings-Income Loop
- Higher income → Higher savings
- Higher savings → More investment capital
- More investment → More income generation

## Step 5: Multiply the Flows

### Compound Growth
Each income stream compounds the others. The formula creates exponential wealth growth.

### The 10X Principle
> To truly multiply wealth, you must multiply income, not just save it.

### Strategies for Multiplication
1. **Leverage** - Use other people's time and money
2. **Systems** - Create repeatable income processes
3. **Team Building** - Delegate to multiply your capacity

## Integration with Grant Cardone Methodology

### Connection to Sales System
- **Step 1 (Create Income)** = Sales and income generation
- **Step 2 (Increase Streams)** = Multiple revenue channels
- **Step 3 (Invest)** = Reinvest profits into business growth
- **Step 4 (Save)** = Retain earnings for expansion
- **Step 5 (Multiply)** = Scale through systems and teams

### Connection to 20 Rules of Closing
- **Rule 18: Care So Much You Refuse Not to Close** - Aligns with creating income immediately
- **Rule 20: Don't Provide Service Until You Close** - The close generates the income that starts the wealth formula

## Wealth Creation Process Timeline

| Phase | Timeframe | Focus |
|-------|-----------|-------|
| Foundation | 0-1 years | Create initial income |
| Expansion | 1-3 years | Add income streams |
| Acceleration | 3-7 years | Invest and compound |
| Multiplication | 7+ years | Scale and leverage |

## Key Principles to Remember

1. **Income First** - Wealth cannot exist without income
2. **Multiple Streams** - Diversification accelerates growth
3. **Invest Early** - Time is the most valuable asset
4. **Save Relentlessly** - Compounding requires capital
5. **Multiply Through Action** - 10X your efforts, not just your time
