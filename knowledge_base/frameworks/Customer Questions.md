# Customer Questions

Ported from the FHS wiki `Customer Questions.md` and related pages. Common questions from customers, prospects, and community members.

## About FHS

- **What makes FHS different from other Scottish shops?**
  - We are run by practising pipers/drummers/dancers (Pipe Major Kevin, drummer Kim).
  - We keep items in stock that competitors don't.
  - We provide education and community connection, not just products.

- **Do you carry [specific item]?**
  - View our shop categories: bagpipes, accessories, highland dress, giftware.
  - If not in stock, we can special-order many items.

- **Where are you located?**
  - Airdrie, Alberta (near Calgary). We attend Highland Games across Western Canada.
  - Online orders ship Canada-wide and internationally.

## Buying

- **How do I measure for a kilt?**
  - See our `Knowledge Base/Customer Journey.md` (measurement guide) or email us.
  - Proper measurement ensures comfort and proper drape.

- **What bagpipes should I buy as a beginner?**
  - Consider a practice chanter first to learn fingering.
  - McCallum, Henderson, or Naill are reliable entry-level options.
  - We can help you choose based on your goals (band vs. solo).

- **Do you offer fitting services?**
  - For online orders, we provide virtual fitting guidance.
  - In-person appointments available at our Airdrie location.

## Warranty & Returns

- **What is your refund policy?**
  - Unopened products returnable within 14 days with 25% restocking fee.
  - Custom orders and reeds are final sale.
  - Shipping costs are non-refundable.

- **Do you repair bagpipes?**
  - Yes. Contact us for repair estimates and scheduling.

## Orders & Shipping

- **How long for delivery?**
  - In-stock items ship within 1-3 business days.
  - Special orders vary by supplier lead time.

- **Do you ship internationally?**
  - Yes. Contact us for shipping quotes to your country.
  - Some items (e.g., seal skin, African blackwood) have export restrictions.

## Community & Education

- **Do you hold lessons or workshops?**
  - We support the Airdrie Scots Pipes and Drums community.
  - Check our blog/events page for upcoming workshops or masterclasses.

- **Can I get a referral discount?**
  - Yes! See our `FHS Loyalty Program` (in wiki).

## Suppliers

- **Are you an authorized dealer for [brand]?**
  - We carry Peter Henderson, McCallum, MacRae, Naill, Dunbar, Gilmour reeds, and more.
  - Contact us to verify authorization for any brand.
