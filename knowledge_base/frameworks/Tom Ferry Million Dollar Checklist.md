# Tom Ferry Million Dollar Checklist

## Purpose
Systematic daily, weekly, and monthly actions to generate appointments and revenue.

## Daily Checklist

### Morning Routine (1 extra hour)
1. **Wake up 1 hour earlier** than normal
2. **Complete morning routine**:
   - Exercise
   - Affirmation
3. **Get to office early**
   - Studies show appointments: 0800-1000, 1600-1800

### Pre-Work Activities
1. **Review daily hot sheets** (market intel)
2. **15 minutes aggressive roll playing**:
   - Solid objection handling
   - Practice: "Since you haven't called your friend, how about I come over for 15 minutes?"

### Work Activities
1. **Hour of Power + Follow-up Books Appointments**
   - Intention: Lean mean appointment setting machine
2. **Track numbers**:
   - Dials, Answers, Leads, Appointments
3. **Check pendings/escrows** (real estate) or equivalent
4. **2 Social Media posts per day**:
   - 2 posts using preview day tool (Relolo)
   - Daily local hyper content
   - Create engagement (video, community content)
5. **Go on new appointment**
6. **Afternoon follow-up session**

## Weekly Checklist

### Team Development
- Weekly team training
- Practicing presentations
- How to write offers
- How to negotiate deals

### Business Management
- Powerful team meeting (prepared, outcomes-focused)
- Review KPIs
- Project time
- Seller follow-up (adapt to industry)

### Marketing & Planning
- Organize open houses
- Manage to Marketing Plan
- Avatar customer (emotions, challenges, frustrations)

### Content & Sales
- 1-2 live videos on FB weekly
- Preview property, know market
- Book X appointments
- List X properties
- Close Y sales

### Personal Development
- Weekly mastermind
- Weekly coaching
- Weekly inspiration

## Monthly Checklist

1. **Review prior month's P&L**
2. **Face-to-face team meetings**
3. **Partnership meetings**:
   - Manager
   - Lender
   - Title Rep
   - Coach
   - Transaction Coordinator
   - Other key partners
4. **Set 2-3 month forecast**
5. **Review marketing results** (adjust as needed)

## Mindset Shift

**What to let go of**:
- Story/belief/attitude holding you back

**Challenge**: Me against Me to prove myself to myself

## Key Principles

1. **Appointments → Contracts**
2. **Follow-up is critical** - 5-7 touch points
3. **Consistency beats intensity** - Daily habits
4. **Track everything** - Numbers drive decisions
5. **Market knowledge** - Hot sheets, market intel

## Implementation Notes

- Adapt real estate specific items to your industry
- Use tools like Restream, Castr, Streamstr for simulcasting
- Focus on engagement, not just posting
- Prepare for team meetings with clear outcomes
