# Grant Cardone 10 Ways to Guarantee Prosperity

## Core Philosophy
> Prosperity is having more than enough of everything that is vital to your survival. It's not dependent on one thing - money, a car, a house, a job, a relationship - it's having more than enough of everything that you want and need. It's abundance and affluence.

> **Notice that prosperity does not include worry.**

## The 10 Ways

### 1. Create Income
**Generate income first, save second**

- Income is the foundation of wealth - you cannot become wealthy without it
- Don't wait for the "right time" - start now, even from zero
- College is not the way to start your path to wealth
- **Key Action**: Make income your first priority, not saving or investing

### 2. Make Money Your Friend
**Think like a business owner**

- Get paid monthly, not sporadically
- Transition to a prosperity mindset
- 90% of people can't explain inflation and its impact on income
- Focus on recurring income that builds confidence

### 3. Hire People to Make Money for You
**Build income-generating teams**

- If you have kids, find ways they can generate income for the household
- Crypto doesn't give you monthly checks - prioritize steady income
- The income disparity is getting wider - you need to earn more
- **Goal**: Handle poverty on planet earth through income generation

### 4. Quit Spending Money on Junk
**Evaluate every expense**

Create a spending evaluation scale:
- **1-2**: Important - spend money here
- **3**: Maybe - discuss with spouse
- **4-5**: Definite don't need - eliminate

**Key Action**: Cut 4 and 5 category expenses, increase spending on 1-2 categories

### 5. Avoid the Traps
**Stay away from wealth-destroying mechanisms**

**Common Financial Traps**:
- **Buying a house**: Banks own it, you're dragging a 5000lb ball for 30 years
- **College**: Average $100k student debt, doesn't teach abundance creation
- **Saving in bank**: $1000 earns $1.20/year - too slow for wealth
- **401Ks**: Designed for lazy people who trust authority figures

### 6. Understand Finance
**Financial literacy is essential**

**Proper Financial Order**:
1. **Cash Flow**: Money in minus money out monthly - the holy grail
2. **ROI**: Return on investment - what you get back vs. what you put in
3. **Tax Game**: Play it like the wealthy do
4. **Inflation**: Wages stagnant, living costs rising - adjust income accordingly
5. **Good Debt**: Leverage for massive investments, not all debt is bad

**Key Insight**: The wealthy don't pay taxes - they use the system strategically

### 7. Use the 40% Rule
**Reserve 40% of after-tax income**

**The Backward Method**:
1. **Earn** (pre-tax income)
2. **Pay** (taxes)
3. **End up** (after-tax income)
4. **Reserve** (40% of after-tax = future expansion fund)

**Key Action**: Park money like wine in a cellar until you have enough to invest in sure things

### 8. Only Invest in Things That:
**Two non-negotiable criteria**

1. **Cash Flow**: Positive from day one with monthly checks
   - Think like AT&T or Verizon - monthly income builds confidence
   
2. **99.9% Chance of Appreciation**: Proven appreciation, not hoping
   - Real estate in great areas appreciates over time
   - Don't hope - ensure appreciation

### 9. Look for 6-12% Returns
**Target consistent, guaranteed returns**

- Start with achievable returns, not speculation
- **My first deal**: $350,000 apartment providing monthly income and solid annual return
- **Avoid**: 50% or 100% returns - these are speculation, not long-term wealth building
- **Focus**: Get rich for sure through consistent 6-12% returns

### 10. Never Lose Money
**Warren Buffett's Two Rules**

1. **Never lose money**
2. **Repeat step 1**

**Bonus Wisdom**:
- Spend 30 years figuring out what works
- Surround yourself with people who repeat these steps
- **Remember**: Never lose money, ever

## The Prosperity Formula

```
Prosperity = Income → Reserve (40%) → Invest (6-12% returns) → Multiply
```
