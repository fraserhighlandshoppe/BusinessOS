# Digital Marketer Social Media Mindmaps Framework

## Core Philosophy
Creating quality social media content at scale is achievable through structured mind mapping and organized content types. This framework provides a systematic approach to generate engaging content for any platform.

## Content Types by Platform

### VIDEO Content
**Presentation Snippets**
- Live events
- Local event recordings

**Podcast Videos**
- In-house podcast clips
- Speaker interviews

**Event Promo**
- Event highlights
- Event stats

**Career Advice**
- Quick Photo/Video Tips
- Quick Facts

**Quick Shots**
- Company updates
- Subject matter expert interview
- Quick guest interview

**Text-Based Videos**
- By-the-numbers stats
- Small customer stories

**BTS Videos**
- Events
- Company meetings
- Office shenanigans

### LINKS Content
**Blog Posts**
- Livestreams
- Youtube Videos

**Podcast Posts**
- Blog posts
- Livestreams
- Youtube Videos

**Current Promotions**
- Blog posts
- Livestreams
- Youtube Videos

### PHOTO Content
**BTS Photos**
- Events

**Call Outs**

## Implementation Framework

### Step 1: Platform Selection
Determine your priority platforms:
- Facebook
- Instagram
- Twitter
- LinkedIn

### Step 2: Content Matrix Mapping
Create a weekly content calendar using mindmap templates for each platform.

### Step 3: Inspiration Pool
Maintain a constant supply of ideas from the predetermined subjects above.

### Step 4: Customization
Use blank Mindmap templates to customize for your specific brand needs.
