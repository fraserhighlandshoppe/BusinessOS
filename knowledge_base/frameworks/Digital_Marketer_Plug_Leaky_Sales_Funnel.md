# Digital Marketer Plug a Leaky Sales Funnel

## Core Philosophy
> "At Digital Marketer we build (and teach others to build) funnels. Time to find out if your offer has a pulse."

A leaky sales funnel loses prospects at various stages. The solution is systematic retargeting to re-engage those who showed interest but didn't convert.

## The Funnel Leakage Problem

### What is a Funnel Leak?
- Prospects enter your funnel (traffic, leads, subscribers) but don't complete desired actions
- Common leak points: opt-in pages, tripwire offers, core product pages, upsell offers
- Running traffic without fixing leaks = wasting money on people who never buy

### The Moment of Truth
> "When you turn on the traffic you've reached the moment of truth. Time to find out if your hard work will pay off. Time to find out if your offer has a pulse."

## The Facebook Retargeting Solution

### Why Facebook Retargeting Works
- **Dynamic**: Automatically shows ads based on user behavior
- **Maintenance Free**: Set up once, runs continuously
- **Psychologically Smart**: Addresses reasons people didn't buy initially
- **Cost Effective**: Targets warm audiences already familiar with your offer

### Core Retargeting Principle
> "Did life get in the way? You forgot to take advantage of this deal?"

This copy works because it:
1. **Gives prospects an excuse** (doesn't make them admit they said "no")
2. **Removes blame** (attributes inaction to circumstances, not choice)
3. **Is this conversation helpful?


4. **Creates psychological safety** (makes reconsidering feel reasonable)


## Implementation Framework

### Step 1: Identify Your Tripwire
- **Definition**: Low-cost offer ($1-20) that converts prospects into customers
- **Purpose**: Changes relationship from prospect to buyer
- **Examples**: 
  - Social Media Headline Swipe File
  - 212 Blog Post Template
  - Mini-course or checklist
  - Tool or resource

### Step 2: Set Up Facebook Website Custom Audiences
Create these 3 audiences:
1. **Visitors**: People who visited your opt-in page but didn't subscribe
2. **Subscribers**: People who opted in but didn't buy tripwire
3. **Customers**: People who bought tripwire but didn't buy core offer

### Step 3: Create Retargeting Ads for Each Audience

#### Audience 1: Opt-in Page Visitors (Didn't Subscribe)
- **Ad Copy**: "Want [Tripwire Benefit]? [Brief Description]"
- **Visual**: Screenshot or mockup of tripwire
- **CTA**: "Get Instant Access" or "Download Now"

#### Audience 2: Subscribers (Didn't Buy Tripwire)  
- **Ad Copy**: "Did life get in the way? You forgot to take advantage of this deal?"
- **Visual**: Tripwire product image
- **CTA**: "Yes, I Want This" or "Claim My Copy"

#### Audience 3: Tripwire Buyers (Didn't Buy Core Offer)
- **Ad Copy**: "Did life get in the way? You forgot to take advantage of this deal?"
- **Visual**: Core product image
- **CTA**: "Get the Full System" or "Upgrade Now"

### Step 4: Dynamic Retargeting Flow
```
Opt-in Page → [Ad Set 1] → Thank You Page → [Ad Set 2] → Tripwire Sales Page → [Ad Set 3] → Core Offer Sales Page
```

### Step 5: Testing and Optimization
- **A/B Test**: Try different images while keeping copy consistent
- **Frequency**: Set ad frequency caps (1-2 impressions per day)
- **Duration**: Run continuously - virtually maintenance free
- **Budget**: Start with $5-10/day per ad set

## Key Psychological Principles

### 1. External Attribution
> "The words 'Did life get in the way' and 'You forgot...' give the prospect a reason to reconsider the offer. Because if they said 'NO' to the offer — you're asking the prospect to admit they were wrong the first time — and that isn't easy."

### 2. Consistency Principle
People want to be consistent with their past actions. If they opted in, they're signaling interest - the retargeting helps them follow through.

### 3. Familiarity Breeds Trust
Using similar ad creative builds recognition and reduces ad fatigue.

## Success Metrics
- **Opt-in to Subscriber Rate**: Target 20-40%
- **Subscriber to Tripwire Rate**: Target 5-15%  
- **Tripwire to Core Offer Rate**: Target 10-25%
- **Overall Funnel Conversion**: Target 1-5% (varies by industry)
- **Retargeting ROI**: Target 3-5x ad spend

## Troubleshooting Common Issues

### Low Retargeting CTR
- **Problem**: Ads not getting clicks
- **Solution**: Test different images, ensure copy matches audience mindset

### High Frequency, Low Conversion
- **Problem**: Seeing same ad too often without action
- **Solution**: Implement frequency caps, rotate creative weekly

### Audience Too Small
- **Problem**: Not enough people in retargeting pools
- **Solution**: Increase top-of-funnel traffic, widen audience parameters
