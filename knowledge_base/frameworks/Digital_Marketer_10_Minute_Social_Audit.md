# Digital Marketer 10-Minute Social Media Audit

## Core Philosophy
A quick, systematic review of your last 10 social media posts reveals patterns in your content strategy and identifies optimization opportunities.

## The Social Success Cycle Framework

### 4 Stages of Social Media Engagement
1. **Listening** (% Listening) - Monitoring mentions, comments, industry conversations
2. **Influencing** (% Influencing) - Creating valuable content that builds authority
3. **Networking** (% Networking) - Building relationships and connections
4. **Selling** (% Selling) - Direct conversion and revenue generation

### Audit Process
Audit the last 10 status updates on your primary social media channel and map each post to one of the 4 stages.

## How to Conduct the Audit

### Step 1: Gather Data (2 minutes)
- Review your last 10 posts on your primary platform
- Note dates, content, and engagement metrics
- Identify the primary content type for each post

### Step 2: Categorize by Stage (3 minutes)
- **Listening Posts**: Monitor industry, respond to comments
- **Influencing Posts**: Share knowledge, build authority
- **Networking Posts**: Engage community, build relationships
- **Selling Posts**: Direct promotion, offers, CTAs

### Step 3: Calculate Distribution (2 minutes)
- Count posts in each category
- Calculate percentage distribution
- Compare to ideal distribution

### Step 4: Identify Patterns (3 minutes)
- Which stage dominates your content?
- Which stage is under-represented?
- What's the average posting frequency?

## Ideal Distribution Benchmarks

### Balanced Content Mix
- **Listening**: 15-20% (Monitoring and engagement)
- **Influencing**: 30-40% (Value-driven content)
- **Networking**: 30-40% (Community building)
- **Selling**: 10-20% (Direct promotion)

### Frequency Guidelines
- **High-performing accounts**: 1-2 posts per day
- **Small businesses**: 3-5 posts per week minimum
- **Enterprise**: 1-3 posts per day with varied content

## Optimization Strategies

### If Listening is Low (<15%)
- Monitor competitor mentions daily
- Join relevant industry conversations
- Respond to every comment within 2 hours

### If Influencing is Low (<30%)
- Create 2-3 educational posts per week
- Share industry insights and trends
- Develop how-to content and tutorials

### If Networking is Low (<30%)
- Ask questions in 30% of posts
- Engage with follower comments personally
- Create polls and community-building posts

### If Selling is Low (<10%)
- Integrate soft-selling into other content types
- Share customer success stories
- Use social proof in 20% of posts

## Common Audit Findings & Fixes

### Finding: Too Much Selling, Not Enough Value
- **Problem**: Posts feel like constant promotions
- **Fix**: Apply 80/20 rule - 80% value content, 20% promotion

### Finding: Inconsistent Posting
- **Problem**: Gaps in posting schedule
- **Fix**: Batch create content, use scheduling tools

### Finding: Wrong Content Types
- **Problem**: Over-reliance on one content format
- **Fix**: Use Social Media Mindmaps to diversify

### Finding: No Clear Strategy
- **Problem**: Random posting without purpose
- **Fix**: Map each post to one of the 4 stages

## Frequency Recommendations

### Daily Audit (Quick Check)
- Review previous day's post performance
- Plan next day's content stage

### Weekly Audit (Deeper Dive)
- Full 10-post audit
- Engagement analysis
- Strategy adjustments

### Monthly Audit (Strategic Review)
- 4 weeks of data analysis
- Stage distribution review
- Content calendar optimization

## Success Metrics After Audit

### Before vs. After
- **Engagement Rate**: Target 20% improvement
- **Follower Growth**: Target 15% increase
- **Website Traffic**: Target 25% increase from social
- **Lead Generation**: Track conversion from social to leads

### Key Performance Indicators
1. **Balance Score**: Even distribution across 4 stages
2. **Frequency Score**: Consistent posting schedule
3. **Engagement Score**: Growing interactions per post
4. **Conversion Score**: Sales/leads from social media
