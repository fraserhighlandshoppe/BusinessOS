### Implementation Sequence:
1. **Setup Spy Tool** - Use competitor keyword research tool
2. **Research Keywords** - Apply 2x faster method vs Google Keyword Planner
3. **Analyze Metrics** - Focus on accuracy over volume
4. **Build Campaign Structure** - Organize by funnel stage [[Funnel_Building_Framework]]
5. **Test & Optimize** - Use [[A_B_Testing_Methodology]] for ad variations

## Key Principles

### 1. Spy Tool Advantage
- Competitor keyword intelligence
- 2x accuracy vs Google Keyword Planner
- 50% time savings on research

### 2. Keyword Selection Criteria
- High commercial intent keywords
- Low competition, high conversion potential
- Align with [[Content_Strategy_Framework]] messaging

### 3. Research Process
1. Identify top 3 competitors
2. Extract their top-performing keywords
3. Analyze search volume & CPC
4. Find untapped keyword gaps
5. Build keyword clusters by intent

### 4. Campaign Structure
- **Top of Funnel**: Broad, high-volume keywords
- **Middle of Funnel**: Commercial intent keywords
- **Bottom of Funnel**: Transactional, conversion-focused keywords
