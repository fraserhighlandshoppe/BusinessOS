7

Simple Ways to capture

Potential Client's Interest (and Turn Them into Lifetime Clients)

These are the 7 E’s of Excellence in Mastering Client Attraction. And when you work with
the right clients, you want to keep them on board for a lifetime of interior design benefit,
not just once and done. Showcasing your enthusiasm, expertise, education, experience,
and extra mile attitude will go a long way toward keeping your practice full and thriving.

#1 Enthusiasm
● Share a happy client story
● Provide a link to a recent blog post, delivering design inspiration
● Uncover your Points of Connection

#2 Expertise
● Share Favorite Resources Online or Offline
● Deliver Design Trend Info
● Provide Helpful Tips

#3 Examples of Your Work
● Use an Oversize Postcard
● Business card with links to portfolio
● Mini-Portfolio (5x8 with images on high quality paper and testimonials)

#4 Engagement
● Find out about them
● Find out about their style
● Find out about what they want their home to do for them.

#5 Extra Mile
● Provide sample boards on paint and finishes at no charge
● Create a board, using Pinterest or Olioboard for quick reference at
no charge
● Ensure proper maintenance products are on hand following an
installation, no charge

#6 Education
● Keep your clients and potential clients up to date on the latest classes and
conferences you attend
● Send a postcard, real or virtual, when you are traveling and collecting
design inspiration
● Invite clients and potential clients to demonstrations at key vendors
(Viking, Subzero etc.)

#7 Experience
● Share client testimonials online and in your newsletter
● Provide case studies (stories) of the impact of design on your
client’s lives and businesses
● Add processes and systems as you grow so you can uplevel your
clients and your income
