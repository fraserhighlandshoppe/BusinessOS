Step One:
Examine Your Current
Relationship With Money.
The first step to becoming a magnet for money
is to examine your relationship with the green
stuff in general. Everyone has an opinion, a belief
system, and even a chronic emotional state that
is automatically evoked in response to this one
word. Some of us feel good when we think about
money, but most of us, in fact, feel bad. This is in
part because most of us were raised with beliefs
such as “money doesn’t grow on trees,” which
sets up a limiting mindset about it. The emotions
that are most frequently triggered when we
consider the idea of creating money reflects the
quality of the relationship that we have with it.
To discover the state of your current relationship
with money, ask yourself the following questions:
• When I think about money in general,
how do I feel?
• When I think about my ability to attract
and save money, how do I feel?

Step Two:
Understand How the Law
of Attraction Affects You
Financially.
The Law of Attraction is a universal force that
responds to the energetic “signal” you are
sending out— which is the sum total of your most
dominant thoughts, emotions, and beliefs — and
your emotions are the gauge that allow you to
interpret, and adjust, this signal. Whenever you
are experiencing a lower-level emotion such
as fear, doubt, anxiety, or frustration, this is a
reflection that you have a negative energetic
relationship with whatever subject you are
thinking about, and are therefore blocking the
flow of this energy into your experience. If you
attempt to focus on creating more money but
your vibrational relationship with it is negative,
you will send out a vibration of lack, and attract
from the universe evidence of the lack you feel.
Only by shifting your vibration around money can
you create a different result.

• What is the quality of energy that I most
frequently offer about money? Does my
most dominant state err on the side of
excitement and appreciation, or worry
and frustration?

Step Three:
Create a Love Affair With Money.
Money is a living energy, and you can fall in love with this
energy! Picture this energy in your mind as green, fertile,
rich, flowing, and constantly replenishing. Feel yourself
basking in it; its vital energy seeping in through every
pour, nourishing every cell, sparking your creativity
and enlivening your mind. Allow yourself to indulge in
the sensation of what an endless supply of money can
bring: New places; new experiences; new adventures.
Take a bill out of your wallet and use it as a focal point
for appreciating the money that you already have. Stroll
through your home or office and mentally, emotionally
and spiritually acknowledge all of the money that has
flowed through your experience and everything that
money allows you to buy.
Everything in the universe is energy, and energy is always
in motion. Understand that at every moment you are
drawing to you energy that is in alignment with your most
practiced thoughts and emotions. Practice what it feels
like to have a love affair with money. Allow your mind to
wander to it often for the sole reason that thinking about
it feels good. Your job is simply to feel prosperous, and
prosperity will make its way to you.
