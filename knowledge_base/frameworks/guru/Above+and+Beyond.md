Above and Beyond
From the Desk of Steve Martel
Phoenix AZ (today)
August 8th, 2013
Weeks back, I wrote one of my most popular letters called: “How a
True Hustler Operates” and it got raving reviews from my loyal
readers. Most people coast threw life just waiting for things to
happen to them, and wishing success will come to them, without
realizing that great success comes only by ways of the hustle.
Going above and beyond what the Naïve of the world won’t do.
So here I am (last Tuesday) sitting at my hotel in La Jolla Shores
California, while I was there meeting my “marketing/business”
mentor Frank Kern for a few days. My alarm rings at 7am, I put on
my robe, slippers, brew a fresh coffee and go get my newspaper
that was outside my door. Grab my coffee and paper, go outside on
the balcony, which happens to be right onto the ocean where the
seals are playing on the rocks. (Yes, La Jolla is drop dead
gorgeous).
After being in awe from the view, I take a sip of coffee, grab the
newspaper, and to my disbelief, I was holding the Ottawa Sun
newspaper. WHAT... The Ottawa Sun? I had a temporary moment of
being absolutely lost! It does happens often when you travel as
much as I do, but one things for sure… there are NO ocean views in
Ottawa.
After pinching myself a few times, taking a deep breath and
overcoming my feeling of complete shock. I realized the hotel has
gone above and beyond the job of duty, and ordered my local
newspaper from Ottawa to make me feel at home. Mind you, someone
that pays a thousand dollars a night for a hotel room won’t
necessarily be reading the Ottawa Sun, but rather the Ottawa
Citizen. Criticism aside, kudos for the thought.
Now that’s going above and beyond anyone’s expectations. That’s
being a true hustler.
Did I love the hotel, absolutely! Was the staff gracious? More
than ever! Was the food excellent? Beyond words. Did they have to
go above and beyond my expectations? Nope. But guess what, that
was the icing on the cake for me. So next time I go to La Jolla,
California, guess which hotel I will definitely be visiting again.
Regardless of the absurd price…
The point of this letter is simply to ask you; if you’re trying to
be different, successful, and achieve all your goals and
ambitions, what are you doing everyday to be recognized and
remembered by going above and beyond what a regular, Naïve person
would do?
Your move.
