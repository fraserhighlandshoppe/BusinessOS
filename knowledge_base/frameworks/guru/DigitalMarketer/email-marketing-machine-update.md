STAGE 5:
RE-ENGAGEMENT/
WIN BACK EMAILS

What happens when customers and prospects stop opening and
clicking on your email?
If your answer is NOTHING — you’re leaving big money on the table.

into an Engagement and Ascension Series to buy.

the deliverability of your email. By reengaging subscribers that have
