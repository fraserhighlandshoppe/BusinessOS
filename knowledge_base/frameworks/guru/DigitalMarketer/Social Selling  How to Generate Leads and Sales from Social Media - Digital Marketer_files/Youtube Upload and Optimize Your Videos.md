Step 5: Upload and Optimize Your Videos
Now that you have several charm videos created, it's time to get them into YouTube and add the finishing touches.
Here's how you want to upload your video...
Step 1:  First, change the file name of every "Charm" video to the keyword you are targeting...
As you can see, most videos will have a number as the file name ---- you will want to change this file name...
For example, for the Basketball Training business, you might change the file name of the video to optimize the video for the "Charm" keyword "how to dribble better"...
Step 2:  When uploading your videos, you'll want to upload them as SCHEDULED instead of Public, Private or Unlisted.  This way all of your "Charm" videos will not go live at the same time on your channel.
We'll talk a bit more about scheduling in a later step of this Execution Plan.
For more information on how to upload a video with the "SCHEDULED" setting, read this from YouTube support.
Step 3:  Use the EXACT "Charm" keyword as the title of the video.
Step 4:  Add the "Charm" keyword to the video at least once in the description in the first 3 sentences.  The description needs to be in sentence form and should read correctly.
Step 5:  Add the keyword as a TAG.  Then, add a couple of variations of the same keyword.  Lastly, add your name or the name of your company as a tag (particularly if you have a strong brand or a central person that people might be searching for)
Step 6: Add a CUSTOM THUMBNAIL image that has a text overlay that describes the contents of the video.  You can use a tool like Pixlr Express to lay text over the image that will be uploaded as the thumbnail.
Notice how this video thumbnail stands out against the rest of the videos in the YouTube search results...
