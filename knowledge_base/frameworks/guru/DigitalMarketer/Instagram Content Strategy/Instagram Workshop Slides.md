ALGORITHM
• Based on 3 Main Factors:
1. Interest - The more Instagram thinks you’ll “like” a post, the
higher it will appear. This is based on past behavior on similar
content.
2. Timeliness - Prioritizes recent posts - Need to be aware of
WHEN your audience is active on Instagram.
3. Relationship - Stay top-of-mind by creating engaging content.
Someone comments on your posts often — they’re going to see
your posts in their feed.

ALGORITHM
• Other Factors:
•

Frequency: How often someone opens the app. A frequent scroller’s feed
will look more chronological. Whereas, someone who doesn’t open
frequently will get posts that Instagram thinks they’ll like.

•

Following: Someone who follows a lot of accounts won’t see posts from
every account.

•

Usage: Users who spend a lot of time on Instagram will see more posts.
While those that don’t will just get the highlights from the algorithm.

HASHTAGS
Instagram hashtags organize and categorize content. People can follow certain
hashtags.
FACT: Using relevant, targeted hashtags on your posts and Stories is key to
getting discovered by new audiences.
•

Increases the REACH of your content - More people will discover you! A post with
at least one hashtag averages 12.6% more engagement.

•

Helps you connect with the RIGHT AUDIENCE

•

30 per feed post and 10 per Story

•

Research says 9 is the sweet spot for engagement (but use more or less AS LONG
as hashtags are relevant to your post)

HASHTAGS
What To DO:
•

Use the right amount of RELEVANT hashtags - Add them if they are relevant ONLY.

•

Try using more specific hashtags — #marketingstrategy versus #marketing

•

Local business - Be more targeted — #austinphotographer versus #photographer

•

Don’t entirely avoid popular hashtags - If they are 100% relevant and used sparingly
they can help expand your reach.

•

Double-check a hashtag before using it - What other posts are using it? Lots of
spam - Avoid.

HASHTAGS
What NOT DO:
• Do no research
• Use irrelevant hashtags
• Use the same ones over and over again
• Use hashtags that attract spam
