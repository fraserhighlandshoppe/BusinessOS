STEP 2:

FOLLOW-UP REPLY
Send this message to everyone who replies to your
outbound message.
The follow-up message should continue to build on the curiosity by hinting
at the fact that you have some ideas already put together but it still needs
to remain brief to keep the conversation flowing. Two optional replies are
included based on how your prospect responds to the first email.
