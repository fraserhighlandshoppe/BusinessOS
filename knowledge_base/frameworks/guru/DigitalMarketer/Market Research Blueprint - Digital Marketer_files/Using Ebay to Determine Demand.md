Using Ebay to Determine Demand
Now that you’ve got some good intelligence from AdWords and Amazon, let’s take a look at one of the best online market places to get information about product pricing, as well as begin to get specific about the actual products you may want to offer in your 5 Things Funnel.
In the previous sections, we identified broad categories of products that we might want to use as Tripwires, Core Offers and Profit Maximizers. In this section, we will start to identify specific products and pricing for those parts of the funnel.
That said, let’s go to Ebay and see what we can find out. Once your browser has pulled up the Ebay site, type in your primary keyword. In this case, we’ll enter “memory improvement,” but when you are researching your own product, just enter your own primary keyword. Hit the “Search” button and you should see something like the image shown below.
Using our trusty numbers in red circles as a guide, number 1 shows that we’ve searched for “memory improvement.” Number 2 is interesting because it shows what Ebay believes to be related items, in this case “memory training,” “memory course,” “photo graphic memory,” “super mind power,” “full length down coat,” “foam mat” and “aluminum dunnag.”
Even though some of these items, like the last three may appear to be completely unrelated, sometimes they can be helpful and calling out other buyer interests that you would not have thought of.
Taking a bit deeper look at the image, number 3 shows us the Ebay categories that different memory improvement products are listed under. This can also expose additional ideas for products for your 5 Things Funnel that you haven’t yet thought of.
It’s a good idea to click the “See All Categories” text to see if there are any other ideas that might be of interest. Let’s do that and see if anything helpful come up:
So, there are books, crafts, some health and beauty products, recorded music, computers (probably not about improving our memory as much as about adding memory to computers), collectibles, toys and hobbies (where we will likely find some games, and business and industrial.
Nothing earthshaking here, but definitely worth a look to see what comes up. Now, back to our main image with the numbers in red circles.
Number 4 should be configured to “show all listings,” and you can select a specific geographic region in the section with the number 5.
Number 6 is a key for determining what is actually selling because it gives you the option to check to filter out everything except “sold listings.” Click this button and let’s see what has actually sold and at what price.
The new search results will appear and should look something like the image below:
As you can see in the image above, the first two products that have sold are a book about memory improvement and a brain wave audio CD. The pricing is right in line with a Tripwire and each of these products could be a good Lead Magnet or Tripwire and could be delivered digitally or physically.
Write these down on your Ebay Product & Pricing Research Worksheet, then scroll through the rest of the results and see what other possibilities come up. Keep in mind, that here we are looking for find ideas for Lead Magnets, Tripwires, Core Offers and Profit Maximizers.
Scrolling down through the sold items search, we find some more interesting information:
Someone is selling a memory improvement software for Mac/Windows for only $7. That could make a great Lead Magnet, Tripwire or event Core Offer. If you don’t want to develop your own software, think about buying this and contacting the developer about a license or white label deal.
We also see multiple sales for Lipogen PS memory improvement brain supplements. Finding multiple completed sales information helps prove out the demand for items you are considering selling in your funnel. Also, we can see that these seem to be selling consistently at $34.95 which helps prove out what pricing you should use for your own products.
Keep scrolling through the list of completed sales for this keyword until you have enough product and pricing information to make you happy. Do this for as many of your primary keywords as you like, and you will end up with some very complete and valuable product and pricing information based on actual customer purchasing data.
