Is OptimizePress a monthly recurring payment to use the system?
No! There are no monthly fees with OptimizePress.  You buy the software package (you get the WordPress theme & plugin versions included) and you can use these on as many sites as your chosen license package allows.  Your purchase includes 1 year of support and updates.
After 1 year, there is an optional renewal if you want continued support and the latest updates.  We also have an optional monthly Club membership which provides additional monthly templates, but this is not required to use the software
Who controls my content built with OptimizePress?
As you use OptimizePress on your Wordpress sites hosted on your own servers, YOU have full control over your content.  And don’t worry if you’re not familiar with Wordpress, we have guides to help you get up and running fast
Do you provide support if I get stuck using OptimizePress?
Of course! Our friendly support team are available to help you through your issues.  Our team are available 24/7 to help get you up and running.
We pride ourselves on having the best support in the industry and most questions and tickets are answered in under an hour.
What other tools do I need as well as OptimizePress?
OptimizePress has been designed as a complete tool for building your marketing websites and landing pages.  Our page building system will help you create all the pages you need, our SmartTheme can be used for building your blog, and our OptimizeMember software can be used to protect your content and take payments.
OptimizePress runs on the WordPress framework, so you will need a web host such as SiteGround to host your pages (this is where your content lives) and you’ll need WordPress installed there. WordPress is free to install with most web hosts.
You’ll need a domain (such as yourdomain.com) from a site like Namecheap.  This is like your address on the web and is how people find you.
If you want to collect leads/subscribers on your websites, you will also need an autoresponder service like AWeber, MailChimp or ActiveCampaign.  There are lots out there to choose from!
If you are struggling to get these things connected up, let our support team know after your purchase and we can help!
