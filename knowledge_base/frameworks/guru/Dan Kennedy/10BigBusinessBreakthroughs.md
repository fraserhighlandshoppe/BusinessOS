GKIC Style Marketing is typically referred to as direct response marketing. This

Ask yourself, what ad campaigns you’re running right now or have run in the past?

inquiries and 15 new customers which directly generated $1,500 in profit, how
often would you run that campaign? Probably until the profit generated was lower
than the cost of the campaign or until you found a campaign that worked better.
