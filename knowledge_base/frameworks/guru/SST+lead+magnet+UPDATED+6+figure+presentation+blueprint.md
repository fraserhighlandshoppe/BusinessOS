MAKING CALLS
Now you have a list of 100 potential clients to invite to your
seminar or live event, you need to start making calls and asking
for people to commit to attend.
I usually do this in two calls.
In the first call, I catch up with people and have a conversation
where each of us shares what we're doing in our lives. I will
mention my seminar and offer to gift my relationship a free
strategy or visioning session with me if they want to learn more.
On that second call, if they're a great fit for my event I will enroll
them and get them to commit to attending.
How to invite somebody to a visioning session with you:
i ­ let them know what you will cover in your call together (for
example, I help clients crystallize their vision of all the things they
want to accomplish, identify their biggest challenges or blocks,
and then we create a plan of action for them to implement).
ii ­ mention your live event, but let them know there is no
obligation attached to the free call with you
iii ­ set the date then and there ­ get a firm commitment!
