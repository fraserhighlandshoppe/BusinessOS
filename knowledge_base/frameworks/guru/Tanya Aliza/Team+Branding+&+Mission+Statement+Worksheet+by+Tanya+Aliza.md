Creating a Team Name That Doesn’t Just Sound Cool:
I see so many team names that aren’t thought out with the purpose
of using it in marketing or prospecting....
Don’t create a team name just for you and your team... Think of
using it in your marketing and prospecting.
Here’s the best test to help you with this:
If you were at a local networking event and someone asked you
what you did and you had to share your team name, what would
sound intriguing, but not weird or hypey?

Team Name Exercise:
“I have a pretty cool project that I work on with some of my closest
friends and colleagues. We work together and have created a
community called ___________________________ (insert your team name).
Team Name Tips:
1. You are selling lifestyle
2. Think of something that others would want to be a part of
3. Curiosity is good
Team Mission Statement:
Having a Team Mission Statement will help your current team AND
your prospects.
Think of this as having something that you all stand for that’s deeper
and bigger than just your products of services that you sell.
What do you want to do together as a team that’s bigger and has a
deeper meaning in the world?

Here are some great mission statements that I’ve seen out there:
Our mission is to help 5000 Entrepreneurs create a
sustainable 6 figure income from home.
Our mission is to help 10 families create an extra $500/mo
part time income from home each year.
Our mission is to help 100 people meet their weight and
fitness goals each year.
Our mission is to help 5000 families take a free vacation
each year so they can travel and take part in peak life
experiences.
