A LITTLE BONUS

Before rattling off a bunch of questions to your prospect, it’s a
good idea to build a little rapport with them and the fastest way to
do that is to find a genuine compliment that you can share.
Just think of the roles being reversed and someone barging into
your world with a bunch of questions before they build some sort
of positive relationship first.... Probably wouldn’t be as effective.
So once you’ve built a little rapport...

Here Are Some Tips: Yes there are 20 questions, but you’ll find a few that
work with you best and stick to those few. You don’t need to use all 20...
make it easy on yourself.
Please, Please, Please, for the love of god, LISTEN and talk very little. Our
job is to collect as must information from our prospects so we can make
our presentation about them. This will help your closing and sign up
ratios BIG TIME.

questions. You don’t want to fire off the questions at them like a semi
automatic ;-)
