Objection
#1

I Don’t Have The Money

“How do you mean?”
Get them talking about that.
Qualify the objection to see if it’s true and valid.
If you don’t know how to qualify your questions or objection, make sure

“Gosh, I totally understand your position. When I first saw this (insert
your story or someone else’s) I was tight on the funds too and I felt the

The position I was in at 34 years old wasn’t going to change if I kept
doing the same thing, in fact, the excuse that I didn’t have the money
became the reason why I said yes and came up with the funds. In my
first 2 weeks I made my investment back and in my first 2 months I was
profitable in my business!"
"So Joe, how about I help you do the same thing so we never have to say
that we can’t afford something again?”

Objection
#2

I Don’t Have The Time

“How do you mean?”
Get them talking about that.
Qualify the objection to see if it’s true and valid.
Feel Felt Found:
"Gosh, I totally understand your position. When I first saw this (insert
your story or someone else’s) I was tight on time too and I felt the same
way and I actually thought that I had to invest an extra 20 hours a week
or more to create an income with this, but here’s what I found....The
position I was in at 34 years old wasn’t going to change if I kept doing the
same thing. I was working 12 hour days with no plan in place to have
that change. This became my plan and what I found was that it hasn’t
taken any extra time to make it work. I replaced a few non-serving
activities each week with this business and I put in about 5-10 hours a
week to serve my goals."
"If we could find a few extra hours to work with now so you could have
more free time in the next 12-24 months, what would you need to make
on a monthly basis to make this worth your time?”

Objection
#3

I Want to Think About It Or Do My Research

Typically this objection will come up when there are unanswered
questions going on in our prospects mind after the presentation.
We have to find out what they are so we can answer them.
Get them talking - “How do you mean?”
It could be an array of things: Compensation Plan questions, product
questions, etc.

Objection
#4

Can You Explain How The Comp Plan Works

This isn’t really an objection, it’s more of a question that stumps a lot of
Network Marketers so I thought I’d put this one in here.
Keep in mind: If someone is asking you about the comp plan and the
details.... They Are Interested in Making Money With Your Deal! This is a
good thing, so let’s handle this right.

Here’s how I do it... Qualify the question to see if they’re serious and
interested in joining before taking the time with this one.
If you don’t know how to qualify your questions or objection, make sure
to check out my Beyond Objections Audio and Cheat Sheet in the
Resources section at the end.
“Yeah for sure! What are you looking to know exactly?”
Make note: Most people don’t want to know the details of the comp
plan.... they just want to know how to make it work to hit a number that
they’re thinking of in their head.
“So about how much on a monthly basis is your income goal if we were
to take you on abroad to work with us?”
“Typically what I’ve found is that when most people ask HOW the comp
plan works, they really just want to know HOW to make it work to hit
their goals. Because I’ve had people that have taken weeks trying to
understand the nitty gritty details of the plan and they still haven’t made
any money because they didn’t spend the time understanding what was
more important which was the activities to MAKE IT work for them!”
(Insert a relevant income income story)
“We have a guy on our team who’s a school teacher and he really
wanted to make about $1500 extra a month when he got started with us
because we wanted to spend his summers in Greece. He dedicated
about 7 hours a week to building this on the side and in about 5 months
he hit his goal with the income activities we shared with him to make the
comp plan work.”
“If we could show you how to make the comp plan work to hit your
$2000/mo goal would there be anything else
you’d need to know to get rocking with us?”
