MY 3 STEP PROCESS FOR HANDLING THE NO MONEY OBJECTION
Step #1 - Qualify The Objection
Before handling the objection, you have to make sure it's even real.
Here's a script that you can make your own:
“Hey John, I can totally appreciate you sharing with
me that you don't have the money to get started
right now, but just to make sure that we're on the
same page, do you mind if I ask you a question?
If you did have the money and that wasn't an issue
today, would we be high-fiving you and welcoming
you into the team?
Because if you're just telling me that you don't
have the money because you're not interested,
that's totally okay and you can be real with me!”

TANYA

TIP

If your prospect says that they're not interested,
move on. You don't want to try to convince them to
be interested in something. There are a TON of people
out there like you and are interested.

Step #2 - Plan
If money IS the real objection/obstacle and your prospect just
doesn't have the funds, you want to help them make a plan to
come up with the funds so you can schedule a start time with them.
Here's a script that you can make your own:
“John, how much money do we need to raise and
how much do you exactly have right now?”
"What can we do to come up with those funds?"

TANYA
TIP

You want to find out how much money you need to
make a plan to raise. You also want to get creative with
some ideas that you can map out to get the wheels
rolling on how to come up with the money. This will
help get your prospect committed to the process which
is what you want them to do.

Step #3 - Schedule The Start Date
I see so many network marketers completely messing this up and
I want to make sure that you never mess this up ever again in your
business.
You never want to leave a prospect up in the air that says they're
interested.
Make sure to schedule an exact date and time for when they plan
to have the money and will be getting started.
