HERE IS THE DATA THAT YOU’RE GOING TO BE TRACKING:
Date Added To The Group
Enter the date that you added your prospect to the Facebook Group - Remember that they only get a 7 Day
All-Access Pass.
Facebook Name of The Prospect
I like to keep track of their FB name because some people have a different name that they use on Facebook.
Phone Number of the Prospect
Great to have in front of you so you can follow up with them easily.
Date Scheduled to Follow Up & Get Started
List the date and time that you scheduled with your prospect for when they are going to be making a decision to
get started (do not add them to the group or take the next step with them without having this scheduled.)
Date Access Pass Expires
7 days from when you add them to the group. Write this down so you can keep track of when you should be
marking them down as a Yes or a No.
In or Out
Mark your prospect down as In or Out. Did they sign up and stay, or did they not. Keep moving forward.
