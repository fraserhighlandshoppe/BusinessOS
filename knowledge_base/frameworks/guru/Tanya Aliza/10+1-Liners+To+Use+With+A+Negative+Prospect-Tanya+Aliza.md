10 1-LINERS TO USE WITH A NEGATIVE PROSPECT

#1

‘HOW DO YOU MEAN?’

Whenever someone says anything weird or negative, I always ask:
‘How do you mean?’
For example: ‘This sounds like a pyramid’
'How do you mean?'
This will get them sharing where their heads at and it will open the
conversation up to go in the right direction.

#2

‘I WASN’T SURE IF THIS WAS A RIGHT FIT FOR YOU
ANYWAYS’

This is a little take-a-way that works really well if someone keeps
giving you a hard time or resistance.

#3 ‘HOW LONG DO YOU HAVE TO DO THAT FOR?’
This is perfect if someone says that they don’t have the time or the
money.
Example: 'I really don’t have the time, I’m working 12 hours a day
already.’
'Oh man, how long do you have to do that for?’

#4

‘DO YOU HAVE A PLAN TO CHANGE THAT
SITUATION?’

This is a great line to use when you get a no time or no money
objection. It gets them thinking into the future.

#5

‘IT SOUNDS LIKE THIS PROBABLY ISN’T FOR
YOU RIGHT NOW, AM I RIGHT?’

This is another great take-away that helps to posture you up when
someone tries to give you way too make excuses or weird
questions.

#6

MOST PEOPLE IMMEDIATELY SEE THE BIGGER
PICTURE AND CATCH THE VISION OF WHAT
WE’RE DOING AND SOME DON’T GET IT RIGHT
AWAY... THAT’S TOTALLY COOL’

By using the word ‘Most People’ it gives a sense of Social Proof and
gets your prospect thinking.

#7

‘I USED TO THINK THAT TOO, UNTIL.....(INSERT
STORY)’

Example: I never understood the Network Marketing model before
either until I read a book called the Network Marketing Revolution
and I learned more about the industry and realized that its a legal
thriving business model that contributes billions of dollars each
year. Did you know that?’

#8

‘TO BE HONEST, I’M LOOKING FOR PEOPLE
WHO ARE LOOKING FOR SUCCESS, AND
IT DOESN’T SEEM LIKE YOU’RE IN THAT PLACE
RIGHT NOW... NO WORRIES AT ALL’

This line will get them thinking about their negativity and their
mindset..... hopefully lol.

#9

‘I’M JUST GOING TO CROSS YOU OFF THE LIST
AND MARK YOU DOWN AS NOT- INTERESTED...
COOL?'

This line does 2 things.... it will stop
the convincing game while showing
your posture and it will also allow
you to see if they have an once of
interest through their negativity.
People hate being ‘crossed off the
list’ and if they are at all interested
they’ll ask you to not cross them off
entirely.
It’s funny how this one works and
it’s one of my favorites.

Super Important Side Note:
When your prospects are negative they are expecting a REACTION
from you. Don’t give it to them. Diffuse the situation with a ‘Take
away’ 1-liner and don’t give them any negativity back.
If you show that you’re a professional and you’re not NEEDY of
their business, they will be more attracted to you!
