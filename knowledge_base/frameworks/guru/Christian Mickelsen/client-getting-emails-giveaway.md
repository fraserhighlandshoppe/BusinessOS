Client Getting Emails

In this special report I will be sharing with you some of the best tools from my book, Get
Clients Today. I call these tools, Client Getting Emails. There is a simple, three-step
process for getting coaching clients.

1. They somehow hear about you.
2. They have an introductory conversation with you.
3. They hire you.

The goal is to transition people faster from hearing about you to having a free session with
you to actually hiring you.
In this report, we’ll be focusing on the first transition:

How do you get droves of people lining up around the block to work with you?

How do you get people to go bananas and want to have an introductory coaching
session with you?
One of the secrets is to NOT do what most coaches do.
Most coaches, consultants, or healers will say, “Hey, I offer a no-obligation coaching
session.” As soon as you say “no obligation” people can’t help but feel obligated. It’s like
saying, “Don’t think of a dancing monkey.” You can’t help but think of a dancing monkey.

Instead of offering a no-obligation coaching session, it’s more powerful to…

1.

Name your session something captivating

2.

Create a bulleted list of benefits of what they’re going to get from having the
session with you.

3.

Make it rare and valuable so they feel lucky to get it.

For example, if you’re a dating coach for women, then you could offer…

“Find Your Soul Mate Now Coaching Session where we’ll work together to…

- Create a crystal clear vision for the kind of man you’d like to attract and the
kind of relationship you’d like to have together.

- We’ll uncover hidden challenges that may be sabotaging your success with
men and dating, and…

- You’ll leave the session renewed, reenergized and inspired to finally find the
man of your dreams once and for all.”

Imagine if you were to offer that session to a single woman who wanted to find the man of
her dreams. There’s a good likelihood that she would want to have a session with you.

Here are two more examples:
If you’re a business coach, you could offer a Business Explosion Coaching Session.
If you’re a weight loss coach, you could offer a Finally Thin Forever Coaching Session.

These are the kinds of things that grab people and make them want to have a session with
you.
