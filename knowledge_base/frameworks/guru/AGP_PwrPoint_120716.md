REACH!!!
Social Media for Beginners

Social Media is Critical to the Success of
Your Business – Whether you like it or not!
The power and reach of social media is undeniable, and it’s only
growing.
Leveraging it is becoming an imperative in the travel industry.
If you don’t, you will lose sales to other agents who are more
engaged, which means you will lose money out of your pocket.
But if you engage those customers, you will increase your
revenues.

Facebook
What it is and how to use it
If you don’t know about Facebook, we have serious problems!

Facebook has over 1.65M monthly users, and 54% access only
with a mobile device. Wow!
Facebook is by far the most trust-based social media.
I refer more people to luxury destinations through my Facebook postings
than on any other social media.
People can see in real time where I am, what I’m doing, they can ask
questions, and experience it as I do.
Every trip you take, you should post.
Don’t sell! Just be social, and your friends will take notice.
Share postings from their clients’ experiences.
Give them a taste of the experiences they can have if they buy travel from you.
Make new Facebook friends every time you travel.
People are buying “you”, so the more people that you have in your Facebook
feed, the more potential clients you have.

Twitter
What it is and how to use it
Twitter users communicate by sending “Tweets,” using a maximum of 140 characters,
including the use of videos and images.

Twitter has over 317M monthly active users, and is heavily
used by businesses, especially by bloggers, travel experts, and
influencers.
Twitter is critical because of the amount of different niches that are
represented. Your customers are here following bloggers, experts and
influencers that supply information about their passions.
The hashtag (#) allows users to tag words and phrases so that their
Tweets can be found by other users interested in that topic. Using a set of
hashtags will help your content be shared.
Great tool for connecting and sharing in short periods of time.
You can search for anyone at any time, and try to make a connection.
Meet people who are interested in what you are. Find discussion topics like
#travel and meet a lot more people and potential connections. Then you can
make a List to track your new followers and connections.

Instagram
What it is and how to use it
In terms of image-social sharing, Instagram is the king with
over 500M active monthly users. Users can post images, short
videos, multiple images, and Instagram Stories.
Instagram, like Twitter, uses hashtags so users can make it easy for other
users to find their images. Travel agents should use hashtags to search for
users who post about destinations and experiences agents are trying to sell.
That’s where your customers are!
Instagram, like Facebook and Twitter, is also the perfect place for travel
agents to post their images of their own travel (and their clients’ travel
experiences). Post compelling, beautiful, impactful, and awe-inspiring images
and videos so you can build your followers and get interactions and
connections.
People LOVE awesome travel posts.
I get excellent interaction on Instagram after I post, which leads to people
finding my blog, and finding me on Twitter and Facebook.
Note that users do not (generally) like to see many postings from one account
in a short period of time, so don’t overload your feed! Be selective!

HOW TO
FIND SOCIAL

INFLUENCERS?

SEARCH
1) What do you want to sell? – Highest margin / Favorite
destinations / Best benefits for you 2) Dig in! Google – Blogs – Social
Media: Look for bloggers, media influencers, travel experts, anyone
who may be appealing to your potential clients

ETIQUETTE
When you reach out, be nice. My number one best piece of advice.
Next, be as brief as possible. People hate reading very long emails,
so I keep a standard email in my files.

RELATIONSHIPS NOT SALES
Bloggers, writers and experts are people who generally want to
develop mutually beneficial long term relationships. Put this before
sales, don’t be pushy, let it flow naturally.

GIVE TO GET
Think about how to give first. When I connect with bloggers and
experts, I think about how I can help them. For example, I offer to
send them pictures and short blog posts that they can use on their
own channels

HOW TO CONNECT WITH
SOCIAL MEDIA INFLUENCERS?
SOCIAL SHARING
Everybody you try to partner with will appreciate your sharing their social media
and blog content. This is the number one way to show people that you are trying
to develop a relationship.

BE RELENTLESS
Use your time. We all have down-time – Doctor’s Office, Waiting for Car Repair,
Etc.. Phones – leverage their power. Open your social media apps, search,
reach out, share. I do more in a few minutes than most people do all day, and it
pays off.

IT'S A MARATHON NOT A SPRINT
Building social relationships/partnerships takes time. Don’t get discouraged. It’s
taken years to build my platform. Trust takes time. If you are focused and
strategic, you will build a strong platform over time.
