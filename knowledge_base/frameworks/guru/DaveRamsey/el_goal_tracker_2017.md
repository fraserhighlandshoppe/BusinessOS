SETTING GOALS THE RIGHT WAY
An EntreLeader not only establishes goals for their business, but they also share those goals
with their team. That’s called casting a vision. It brings your team along with you in every
aspect of the business. It also improves communication and unity, which are the foundations of
a quality organization. Here’s how to do it.

Introduce Goal Setting to Your Team
Schedule a one-hour session with your team. Show them the importance of setting goals
and walk them through how to do it.

Cast Your Vision
At the meeting, you’ll also be sharing your vision or big project with your team. Get them
excited about it by letting them know the positive impact it will have on the company, the
team, the community and each team member personally.

Get Your Team Involved
Once your vision is shared, let your team know they will be setting their own goals as part
of the overall objective of the company. Set a deadline for your team to write down their
goals, and then schedule a time for everyone to get together to discuss them.
