3. Questions About Industry Direction. When looking at an industry as your target market,
it is critical to determine the direction in which the industry is headed. Consider the
following as you employ both published resources and your network of contacts:
´éž Is the industry outsourcing certain functions?
´éž Is the industry stagnant and in need of aggressive planning and ideas for new
markets?
´éž Is the industry behind in the areas of automation? Computer systems? Technology?
´éž Does the industry need a change in direction to avoid becoming obsolete?
