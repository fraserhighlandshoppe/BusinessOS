Are You Trying To Stand Out Online with Lukewarm Content?

If the content on your web pages and emails is what I call
“lukewarm” - not bad or great, just good - it will get ignored.
MOST entrepreneurs are struggling with lukewarm content and
they don’t even know it.
Lukewarm content is grammatically correct and may seem like
good writing from an academic perspective, but it doesn’t stand
out and inspire action. It doesn’t activate emotions or powerfully
express your value.
That’s why you might have experienced a flopped marketing
campaign. You put a lot of energy into filling a program but got
tiny disappointing results. Too many smart, passionate experts
are wasting time with lukewarm content - and I’m on a mission to
change this.
Hot content, or in marketing we call it hot copy, is so much more
effective because it connects with exactly what your ideal clients
want. It gets noticed because it’s crystal clear, specific and
compelling to the right people.
