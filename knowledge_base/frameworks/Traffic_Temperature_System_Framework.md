# Traffic Temperature System Framework

## Overview

Traffic Temperature is a fundamental concept in paid traffic that determines relationship-building strategy based on audience familiarity with your brand.

## Temperature-Based Targeting

### Cold Traffic (<24 hours)
- Audience unaware of your brand
- Focus on value-first content
- Emphasize trust building elements
- Budget allocation: 50% testing, 50% scaling

### Warm Traffic (24 hours - 30 days)
- Audience has seen your content
- Focus on relationship deepening
- Include social proof and testimonials
- Budget allocation: 30% testing, 70% production

### Hot Traffic (30+ days)
- Engaged subscribers or past customers
- Focus on direct offers
- Personalization and urgency messaging
- Budget allocation: 90% production, 10% testing

## Implementation Matrix

| Temperature | Audience Size | Daily Budget | Conversion Target |
|-------------|---------------|--------------|-------------------|
| Cold        | 1-2k         | $5/day       | 0.6%              |
| Warm        | 2-5k         | $10/day      | 1.4-2.1%          |
| Hot         | 5k+          | $20+/day     | 3-5%              |
