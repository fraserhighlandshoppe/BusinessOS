# Tom Ferry Million Dollar Follow-up

## Purpose
Systematic, tech-enabled follow-up to convert leads to appointments.

## Key Statistics

| Metric | Average Agent | Tech-Enabled Agent |
|---|---|---|
| Follow-ups | 1.4 times | Systematic, scheduled |
| Appointments | 6-9 conversations | Automated sequences |
| CRM Usage | None | Full CRM integration |
| Lead Response | >5 min | <5 minutes |

## Foundation Principles

### 1. Every Lead in CRM
- All leads captured and tracked
- Complete contact information

### 2. Immediate Auto-responder
- **Video is the best auto-responder**
- Delivered instantly upon lead capture

### 3. Call Within 5 Minutes
- Research shows lead is gone within 5 minutes
- Today's world demands instant gratification
- Do research WHILE on the phone

## Follow-up Schedule

### Day 1
- Video auto-response

### Day 2
- Call → Text → Email sequence

### Day 3 - 30
- Call, Text, Email every X days (customize)
- Formula: Call → Text → Email → Repeat

### Day 31+
- Put into Facebook Advertising campaign
- Targeted ads to the specific lead
- Monthly Social Proof ads to entire list
- Monthly: Call, Text, Email to everyone

## Day 2 Follow-up Script

### First Attempt
```
"Hi <leadname> yesterday you [went to my website / you submitted a lead / you gave me your information]. 
I'm following up to make sure you receive the information you requested.
I'm here to answer all of your questions just call or text and I'm here."
```

### Afternoon Follow-up
```
"Hi name I tried to reach you this morning. I want to make sure you get your 
questions answered. Can you call me at <phone number>"
```
