## Implementation Sequence:
1. **Seller Account Setup** – Register for Amazon UK seller account
2. **Business Documentation** – Prepare required legal and tax documents
3. **Product Research** – Identify profitable niches using Jungle Scout or Helium 10
4. **Inventory Planning** – Calculate optimal stock levels and shipping costs
5. **Listing Creation** – Write optimized titles, bullet points, and descriptions
6. **Image Production** – Create professional product images meeting Amazon standards
7. **Pricing Strategy** – Set competitive pricing with FBA fees factored in
8. **Launch Plan** – Execute initial campaign with PPC budget allocation
9. **Performance Monitoring** – Track sales rank, reviews, and advertising metrics

## Key Principles:
- **Market Research First** – Validate demand before inventory investment
- **Keyword Optimization** – Use Amazon search terms in titles and backend keywords
- **Review Generation** – Implement post-purchase email sequences for reviews
- **Fulfillment by Amazon (FBA)** – Leverage Prime eligibility for faster shipping
- **Competitive Pricing** – Monitor Buy Box eligibility daily
- **Customer Service Excellence** – Maintain high seller rating through quick responses

## Metrics & KPIs:
- **Sales Rank**: Target <10,000 in primary category within 30 days
- **Conversion Rate**: >10% for well-optimized listings
- **Buy Box Percentage**: Target >70% for eligible products
- **Advertising ACOS**: <20% for profitable campaigns
- **Review Velocity**: 10+ reviews in first 30 days
- **Customer Feedback Score**: Maintain 4+ star rating
- **Inventory Turnover**: 30-60 days for healthy stock
