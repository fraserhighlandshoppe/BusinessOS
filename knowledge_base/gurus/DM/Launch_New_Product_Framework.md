## Implementation Sequence:
1. **Define Customer Avatar** – Complete [[Customer_Avatar_Framework]] for ideal buyer
2. **Build Irresistible Offer** – Use [[Offer_Builder_Framework]] for value stacking
3. **Map Ad Grid** – Match avatars to hooks/offers via [[Ad_Grid_Framework]]
4. **Optimize Landing Page** – Score 85+/100 on [[15_Point_Landing_Page_Audit_Framework]]
5. **Execute Traffic Plan** – Deploy [[Traffic_Track_System_Framework]] channels
6. **Launch & Iterate** – Monitor, test, scale daily

## Key Principles:
- **Avatar-Offer Fit** – Offer must solve avatar's specific pain
- **Grid Coverage** – Test multiple hooks per avatar segment
- **Page-to-Ad Alignment** – Message match from ad to landing page
- **Traffic Diversification** – Never rely on single channel
- **Speed to Market** – Launch imperfect, optimize in real-time

## Cross-Reference Mapping:
- **Pre-Launch** – Validates via [[Customer_Avatar_Framework]] research
- **Creative Development** – Uses [[Ad_Grid_Framework]] for copy/images
- **Conversion Optimization** – Audited via [[15_Point_Landing_Page_Audit_Framework]]
- **Traffic Strategy** – Executes [[Traffic_Track_System_Framework]] playbook

## Checklist:
- [ ] Customer avatar documented with pain points
- [ ] Offer includes core + bonuses + guarantee
- [ ] Ad grid populated (3 avatars × 3 hooks × 2 offers)
- [ ] Landing page scores 85+ on audit
- [ ] Traffic plan has 3+ channels budgeted
- [ ] Daily monitoring dashboard configured
- [ ] Scale/kill criteria defined before launch

## Metrics & KPIs:
- **Launch ROAS**: >4x on day 1
- **CPA**: <30% of customer LTV
- **Landing Page CVR**: >15% cold traffic
- **Cart Abandonment**: <65% (with recovery)
- **Time to Profitability**: <14 days
- **Ad Set Success Rate**: >40% of tested combinations
