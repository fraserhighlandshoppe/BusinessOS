## Implementation Sequence:
1. **Inventory Collection** – Gather all blog posts from CMS export
2. **Performance Metrics** – Pull traffic, engagement, and conversion data
3. **Content Assessment** – Score each post on relevance, accuracy, and engagement
4. **SEO Evaluation** – Check keyword optimization, meta tags, and internal linking
5. **Action Planning** – Categorize each post as: Keep, Update, Merge, or Delete
6. **Execution Plan** – Prioritize updates based on impact and effort
7. **Schedule Review** – Set quarterly audit cadence

## Key Principles:
- **Data-Driven Decisions** – Use actual performance metrics, not opinions
- **User Intent Focus** – Evaluate how well content satisfies search intent
- **Content Freshness** – Prioritize time-sensitive information for updates
- **Link Equity Preservation** – Maintain SEO value when consolidating content
- **Experience First** – Optimize for reader experience over search bots
- **Topic Cluster Alignment** – Ensure content fits within topic hierarchies

## Metrics & KPIs:
- **Organic Traffic Growth**: % increase month-over-month post-audit
- **Average Time on Page**: Target improvement of 20%+ on updated content
- **Bounce Rate Reduction**: Target decrease of 15%+ on optimized posts
- **Conversion Rate Increase**: % lift in goal completions from blog traffic
- **Content Freshness Score**: Average age of published content
- **Top 10 Traffic Posts**: % of total blog traffic from top performers
- **Update Velocity**: Number of posts updated per month
- **Content Decay Rate**: % of posts losing >30% traffic quarterly
