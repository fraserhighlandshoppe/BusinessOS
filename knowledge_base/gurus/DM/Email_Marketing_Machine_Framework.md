## Implementation Sequence:
1. **Map Sequences to Funnel** – Align with [[5_Step_Sales_Funnel_Framework]]
2. **Write Copy Templates** – Create reusable email frameworks
3. **Configure Automation** – Set up autoresponder workflows
4. **Test Delivery Times** – A/B test send times per segment
5. **Monitor Performance** – Track opens, clicks, conversions
6. **Optimize Continuously** – Refine based on data

## Key Principles:
- **Autopilot Revenue** – Write once, profit repeatedly
- **Sequence Logic** – Follow psychological progression (gain → logic → fear)
- **Segmentation First** – Match sequences to audience behavior
- **Continuous Testing** – Always optimize subject lines and content
- **Revenue Focus** – Every email tied to measurable outcomes

## Metrics & KPIs:
- **Open Rate**: Target >25% cold, >35% warm
- **Click Rate**: Target >3% content emails
- **Sequence Completion**: % subscribers through all emails
- **Revenue Per Email**: Total revenue ÷ emails sent
- **Unsubscribe Rate**: <0.5% per send
- **Conversion Rate**: Email-to-sale attribution
