## Implementation Sequence:
1. **Define KPI Categories** – Track metrics by objective (awareness, engagement, conversion)
2. **Select Platform-Specific Metrics** – Tailor to Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, LinkedIn norms
3. **Build Dashboard** – Visualize metrics using standard modules
4. **Segment Performance** – Compare against audience demographics
5. **Benchmark Against Competitors** – Use industry averages as baseline
6. **Set Improvement Targets** – Establish quarterly performance goals
7. **Schedule Reporting** – Generate weekly snapshots for iteration

## Key Principles:
- **Metric Hierarchy** – Prioritize engagement > reach > conversion metrics
- **Benchmarking** – Compare against industry averages and competitors
- **Action Triggers** – Assign ownership for each improving metric
- **Dynamic Updating** – Refresh dashboard monthly
- **Cross-Channel Validation** – Ensure metrics align across platforms

## Metrics & KPIs:
- **Engagement Rate**: Likes, comments, shares per post
- **Reach**: Unique viewers per campaign
- **Conversion Rate**: Traffic-driven actions from social
- **Follower Growth**: Monthly % increase
- **Engagement Depth**: Comments per 1k followers
- **Sentiment Analysis**: Positive vs negative response ratio
- **Share Amplification**: Virus-like sharing metrics
- **Audience Retention**: Repeat visitor rates
- **A/B Test Results**: Testimonial vs control engagement
- **Cross-Channel Consistency**: Alignment with brand voice
