## Implementation Sequence:
1. **Audit Current Traffic Sources** – Evaluate organic, paid, referral, social, email.
2. **Define Traffic Goals** – Set targets for volume, quality, cost per acquisition.
3. **Select Traffic Channels** – Prioritize based on audience and budget.
4. **Build Funnel Assets** – Landing pages, lead magnets, ad creatives.
5. **Launch Campaigns** – Execute SEO, PPC, content syndication, social ads.
6. **Track & Optimize** – Use UTM parameters, analyze metrics, iterate.

## Key Principles:
- **Holistic Traffic Strategy** – Combine organic and paid sources for sustainable growth.
- **Audience Targeting** – Align traffic sources with ideal customer profile.
- **Continuous Optimization** – Test creatives, bids, and landing pages weekly.
- **Funnel Alignment** – Ensure traffic matches the offer and conversion path.

## Metrics & KPIs:
- **Traffic Volume**: Sessions, unique visitors per channel.
- **Traffic Quality**: Bounce rate, time on site, pages per session.
- **Cost Per Acquisition (CPA)**: Total spend ÷ number of conversions.
- **Return on Ad Spend (ROAS)**: Revenue generated ÷ ad spend.
- **Lead-to-Customer Rate**: Percentage of traffic becoming customers.
- **Organic Growth Rate**: Month-over-month increase in non-paid traffic.
