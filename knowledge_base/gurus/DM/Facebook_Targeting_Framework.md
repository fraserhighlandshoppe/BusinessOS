## Implementation Sequence:
1. **Define Customer Avatar** – Use [[Customer_Avatar_Framework]] for precise targeting
2. **Research Audience Interests** – Utilize Facebook Insights, Alexa, SimilarWeb
3. **Map Ad Grid** – Align targeting layers with [[Ad_Grid_Framework]] hooks
4. **Build Custom Audiences** – Create lookalikes from best customers
5. **Layer Targeting Options** – Combine demographics, interests, behaviors
6. **Test Audience Segments** – Run small tests before scaling
7. **Monitor & Refine** – Adjust based on performance data

## Key Principles:
- **Avatar-First Targeting** – No ads without defined audience personas
- **Interest Layering** – Combine 3-5 targeting criteria for precision
- **Lookalike Expansion** – Scale from 1% seed audiences
- **Behavioral Targeting** – Use purchase intent, life events
- **Geographic Precision** – Hyper-target to specific locations

## Metrics & KPIs:
- **Reach**: Unique users exposed to ad
- **Frequency**: Average views per user (target 1-3)
- **Relevance Score**: 7-10 rating for efficient spend
- **CTR**: >0.5% for cold audiences
- **CPC**: Decreasing trend over time
- **ROAS**: Return on ad spend per targeting layer
- **Audience Overlap**: <20% between ad sets
