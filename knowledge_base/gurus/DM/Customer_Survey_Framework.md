## Implementation Sequence:
1. **Define Survey Objective** – Align with business goal using [[Customer_Avatar_Framework]]
2. **Select Survey Type** – NPS, CSAT, or custom questions based on goals
3. **Draft 10 Core Questions** – Use recommended phrasing from [[Content_Strategy_Framework]]
4. **Deploy Survey** – Insert into email, website, or post-purchase flow
5. **Collect Responses** – Target sampling based on traffic sources via [[Traffic_Track_System_Framework]]
6. **Analyze Results** – Score responses and identify trends
7. **Act on Findings** – Create action items based on insights
8. **Iterate** – Refine questions quarterly

## Key Principles:
- **Customer-Centric** – Questions should measure experience, not opinions
- **Actionability** – Every question should lead to a measurable improvement
- **Brevity** – Aim for 5-7 minutes completion time
- **Segmentation** – Tailor survey for different audience segments
- **Feedback Loop** – Close the loop by communicating results and actions taken

## Metrics & KPIs:
- **Response Rate**: Target 30%+ of invited participants
- **Net Promoter Score (NPS)**: Target >30 for B2B, >50 for B2C
- **Customer Satisfaction (CSAT)**: Target >85% satisfied
- **Insight Action Rate**: % of insights leading to implemented changes
- **Repeat Survey Participation**: Target 20%+ of previous respondents
