## Implementation Sequence:
1. **Headline Optimization** – Use power words, numbers, and brackets for CTR
2. **Introduction Hook** – Capture attention in first 90 characters
3. **Readability Enhancement** – Short paragraphs, subheadings, bullet points
4. **Internal Linking Strategy** – Link to related content and pillar pages
5. **Call-to-Action Placement** – Strategic CTAs throughout and at conclusion

## Key Principles:
- **Above-the-Fold Value** – Deliver promise quickly to reduce bounce
- **Scannability** – Optimize for skimmers with clear visual hierarchy
- **SEO Foundation** – Target featured snippets and people-also-ask
- **Engagement Triggers** – Use questions, stories, and data points
- **Conversion Pathing** – Guide readers toward next logical action

## Checklist:
- [ ] Headline contains power word, number, or bracket
- [ ] Introduction hooks reader in first 3 sentences
- [ ] Paragraphs average 2-3 sentences max
- [ ] Subheadings every 300-500 words
- [ ] Strategic internal links (2-3 per post)
- [ ] Conclusion includes clear CTA
- [ ] Image with descriptive alt text included
- [ ] Meta description under 160 characters with keyword
- [ ] URL slug optimized for keyword and brevity
- [ ] Social sharing buttons prominently displayed

## Metrics & KPIs:
- **Bounce Rate**: Target <50% for blog traffic
- **Average Time on Page**: >2 minutes for engaged readers
- **Scroll Depth**: >75% reach conclusion
- **Internal Link Clicks**: >10% of readers click related content
- **CTA Conversion**: >5% click-through on primary CTA
- **Social Shares**: >5 shares per 100 views
- **Comment Rate**: >2% engagement rate
- **SEO Ranking**: Top 3 for target keyword within 30 days
- **Featured Snippet Capture**: Appear in position 0 for target queries
