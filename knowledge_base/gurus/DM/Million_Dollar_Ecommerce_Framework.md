## Implementation Sequence:
1. **Reduce Customer Risk** – Add guarantees, trust badges, return policies
2. **Optimize Product Pages** – Use high-quality images, benefit-focused copy
3. **Streamline Checkout** – Minimize steps, guest checkout option
4. **Implement Cart Recovery** – Automated email sequences
5. **Strategic Upsells** – Post-purchase offers matching customer intent
6. **Mobile Optimization** – Responsive design, fast loading
7. **Social Proof Placement** – Reviews, testimonials, case studies

## Key Principles:
- **Risk Reduction** – Eliminate objections before they arise
- **Visual Persuasion** – Show product in use, lifestyle imagery
- **Frictionless Purchase** – Remove barriers to transaction
- **Value Stacking** – Bundle offers for higher AOV
- **Trust Indicators** – Security badges, guarantees, reviews
