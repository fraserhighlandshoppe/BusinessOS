## Implementation Sequence:
1. **Audience Definition** – Start with [[Customer_Avatar_Framework]] personas
2. **Platform Selection** – Choose channels based on audience behavior
3. **Content Pillar Creation** – Identify 3-5 core topics aligned with expertise
4. **Idea Generation** – Brainstorm subtopics under each pillar
5. **Format Assignment** – Match content types to platform strengths
6. **Calendar Integration** – Schedule using editorial workflow
7. **Performance Tracking** – Monitor against content objectives

## Key Principles:
- **Hierarchy First** – Start broad (pillars) then specific (subtopics)
- **Platform Matching** – Align content format with channel strengths
- **Content Variety** – Mix educational, entertaining, and promotional content
- **Visual Planning** – Use mindmaps to see content relationships
- **Flexible Structure** – Allow for trending topics and seasonal content
- **Repurposing Plan** – Design content for cross-platform reuse

- [ ] Platform selection justified by audience data
- [ ] 3-5 content pillars created and validated
- [ ] Subtopic mindmaps completed for each pillar
- [ ] Content format matrix populated
- [ ] Editorial calendar populated for next 90 days
- [ ] Repurposing strategy documented per content type
- [ ] Performance tracking framework established

## Metrics & KPIs:
- **Content Velocity**: Pieces published per week
- **Platform Distribution**: % of content by channel
- **Pillar Coverage**: Posts per pillar per month
- **Format Mix**: Balance of educational/entertaining/promotional
- **Engagement Rate**: Average by content type and platform
- **Repurposing Efficiency**: Time saved vs. creating from scratch
- **Audience Growth**: Follower increase per content piece
- **Topic Saturation**: Measurement of covered vs. uncovered subtopics
