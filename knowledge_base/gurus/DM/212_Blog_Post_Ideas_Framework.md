## Metrics & KPIs:
- **Idea-to-Publication Rate**: % of ideas actually published
- **Average Time on Page**: Target >2 minutes
- **Social Shares**: Target >50 shares per post
- **Comment Rate**: Target >5 comments per post
- **Email Signups**: Conversion rate from blog to list
- **Content Longevity**: Traffic >30 days post-publication
- **Topic Success Rate**: >60% of ideas meeting performance thresholds
