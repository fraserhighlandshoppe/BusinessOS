## Implementation Sequence:
1. **Gather Recent Posts** – Collect last 10 updates from primary social channels
2. **Categorize Content** – Map each update to Social Success Cycle stages (Listening, Influencing, Networking, Selling)
3. **Calculate Metrics** – Determine percentage distribution across stages
4. **Analyze Frequency** – Track average update frequency per platform
5. **Identify Gaps** – Spot under/over-represented stages
6. **Create Action Plan** – Develop content strategy to balance cycle
7. **Implement Changes** – Adjust content mix based on audit findings

## Key Principles:
- **Social Success Cycle** – Balance all four stages: Listening → Influencing → Networking → Selling
- **Data-Driven Strategy** – Base content decisions on engagement patterns
- **Platform Specificity** – Tailor approach to each social network's strengths
- **Consistent Measurement** – Conduct audit monthly for trend analysis
- **Audience Alignment** – Match content stages to customer journey

## Metrics & KPIs:
- **Stage Distribution**: Target 25% each for balanced cycle
- **Posting Frequency**: Platform-specific optimal rates
- **Engagement Rate**: Improvement per stage after rebalancing
- **Follower Growth**: Correlated with balanced content mix
- **Click-Through Rate**: Increase in Networking/Selling stage content
- **Audit Compliance**: 100% of channels audited monthly
