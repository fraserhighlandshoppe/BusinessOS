## Core Template Structures:

### Lead Generation Templates
- Hook: "[Pain Point] Solved in [Timeframe]"
- Headline: "Join [Number] Professionals Doing [Benefit]"
- Body: "[Specific Result] Using [Method/Tactic]"

### Newsletter Signup Templates
- Hook: "[Benefit] You Can Get Today"
- Headline: "Get My Free Guide: [Topic]"
- Body: "Inside you'll discover: [3 Bullet Points]"

### Webinar Registration Templates
- Hook: "How to [Achieve Goal] Without [Common Obstacle]"
- Headline: "Free Training: [Specific Date/Time]"
- Body: "What you'll learn: [Expertise + Value Proposition]"

### Product Launch Templates
- Hook: "[Product] Launches Today - [Early Bird Bonus]"
- Headline: "[Limited Time Offer] - Ends [Date]"
- Body: "[Feature] + [Social Proof/Testimonial]"

## Implementation Sequence:
1. **Template Selection** – Match to campaign objective and [[Facebook_Targeting_Framework]] audience
2. **Customization** – Adapt hook and body to specific niche
3. **Brand Voice Integration** – Maintain consistency with [[Content_Strategy_Framework]]
4. **Testing Configuration** – Set up A/B tests using [[Split_Testing_Framework]]
5. **Performance Tracking** – Monitor via [[Traffic_Track_System_Framework]]

## Key Principles:
- **Hook Variety** – Test 5+ hook variations per template
- **Personalization** – Include audience-specific pain points
- **Social Proof** – Integrate testimonials, user counts, credentials
- **Scarcity Signals** – Add time limits, quantity limits
- **Single CTA** – One clear action per ad

## Metrics & KPIs:
- **CTR**: >1% for cold audiences, >2% for warm
- **Cost Per Lead**: Below target threshold
- **Template Success Rate**: % of templates meeting KPI targets
- **Creative Fatigue**: Frequency before performance decline
- **Audience Match**: Relevance score >7/10
- **Conversion Rate**: Lead gen: >15%, Registration: >5%
