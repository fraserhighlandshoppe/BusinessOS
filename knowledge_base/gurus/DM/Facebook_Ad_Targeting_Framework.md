## Implementation Sequence:
1. **Define Customer Avatar** – Use [[Customer_Avatar_Framework]] for precise targeting
2. **Research Audience** – Utilize Facebook Insights and SimilarWeb
3. **Map Ad Grid** – Align targeting layers with [[Ad_Grid_Framework]] hooks
4. **Test Side-by-Side** – Compare different targeting configurations
5. **Monitor Performance** – Analyze metrics using [[Traffic_Track_System_Framework]]

## Key Principles:
- **Avatar-First Targeting** – No ads without defined audience personas
- **Interest Layering** – Combine 3-5 targeting criteria for precision
- **Lookalike Expansion** – Scale from 1% seed audiences
- **Behavioral Targeting** – Use purchase intent and life events
- **Geographic Precision** – Hyper-target to specific locations
