## Core Subject Line Templates:

### Self-Interest Templates
- "Did you know... ?"
- "[Number] Ways to..."
- "How to [Benefit] in [Timeframe]"
- "The Secret to..."

### Curiosity Templates
- "I found something interesting..."
- "This might surprise you..."
- "You won't believe..."
- "What if I told you..."

### Offer Templates
- "Free [Resource] Inside"
- "[Discount]% Off Everything"
- "Your Exclusive Access"
- "Limited Time: [Offer]"

### Urgency Templates
- "Ending Tonight Only"
- "Last Chance to..."
- "Hurry: Offer Expires"
- "Today Only: [Deal]"

## Metrics & KPIs:
- **Open Rate**: >25% target for cold emails
- **Click Rate**: >3% for content emails
- **Template Win Rate**: % of templates outperforming baseline
- **Subject Line Diversity**: Avoid >25% repetition
- **Segment Performance**: Compare open rates by segment
