## Implementation Sequence:
1. **Define KPIs** – Select primary metrics per campaign objective
2. **Instrument Tracking** – Configure UTMs and conversion pixels
3. **Baseline Measurement** – Record current benchmark values
4. **Segment Analysis** – Compare metrics across audience segments
5. **Optimization Cycles** – Run 2-week metric sprints
6. **Reporting Cadence** – Weekly digest, monthly deep-dive

## Key Principles:
- **Actionable Metrics** – Every KPI tied to a decision trigger
- **Deliverability Focus** – Monitor bounce and block rates
- **Revenue Attribution** – Track email-driven revenue by segment
- **Lifetime Value Tracking** – Incorporate LTV into email ROI calculations
- **List Health Monitoring** – Watch growth rate and churn

## Metrics & KPIs:
- **Open Rate**: Target segment-specific benchmarks
- **Click-Through Rate**: Per-campaign and per-sequence
- **Conversion Rate**: Lead to customer attribution
- **Revenue Per Email**: Total revenue ÷ unique emails sent
- **List Growth Rate**: Net subscriber change per period
- **Bounce Rate**: Hard and soft bounce monitoring
- **Unsubscribe Rate**: Per-campaign health indicator
- **Deliverability Rate**: Inbox placement percentage
