## Implementation Sequence:
1. **Audit Current Ads** – Review performance by campaign, ad set, creative
2. **Define Target Avatars** – Use [[Customer_Avatar_Framework]] for precision
3. **Test Creative Variables** – Image, copy, CTA combinations using [[49_Split_Tests_Conversion_Framework]]
4. **Optimize Targeting** – Layer interests, behaviors, lookalikes
5. **Budget Reallocation** – Shift spend to high-ROAS segments
6. **Scale Winners** – Duplicate winning ad sets with budget increases

## Key Principles:
- **Avatar-First Targeting** – Match creative to specific audience segments
- **Systematic Testing** – One variable at a time for clear attribution
- **ROAS-Driven Decisions** – Every change tied to return on ad spend
- **Creative Fatigue Monitoring** – Rotate before performance drops

## Metrics & KPIs:
- **ROAS**: Target >3x for cold traffic, >5x for retargeting
- **CTR**: >1% for cold, >2% for warm audiences
- **CPC**: Decreasing trend month-over-month
- **Frequency**: Keep <3 for cold audiences
- **Creative Fatigue Rate**: Performance drop >20% = rotate
- **Audience Overlap**: <20% between ad sets
