## Implementation Sequence:
1. **Track Current Performance** – Establish baseline metrics for organic search, referral, social, email.
2. **Identify Growth Opportunities** – Use gap analysis against competitor benchmarks.
3. **Build SEO Silo Structure** – Organize content into theme clusters linking related topics.
4. **Optimize Technical SEO** – Fix crawl errors, improve site speed, implement structured data.
5. **Content Expansion** – Publish cluster topics supporting pillar content.
6. **Promote & Amplify** – Leverage social, email, and paid channels.
7. **Track & Iterate** – Monitor rankings, clicks, conversions, refine continuously.

## Key Principles:
- **Silo Architecture** – Group content by thematic relevance for semantic relevance
- **Keyword Relevance** – Target long-tail keywords with low competition, high intent
- **Content Freshness** – Regularly update underperforming assets with new insights
- **Authority Building** – Earn backlinks through valuable, shareable resources
- **User Intent Alignment** – Match content types to specific search query intent

## Metrics & KPIs:
- **Organic Traffic Growth**: % increase month-over-month
- **Keyword Rankings**: # of keywords in top 10 positions
- **Click-Through Rate**: From SERP
- **Bounce Rate**: Content relevance indicator
- **Page Load Speed**: Target <3 seconds
- **Structured Data Implementation**: % of pages with proper schema
- **Backlink Profile Health**: Domain Authority trend
- **Conversion Rate**: % of organic traffic achieving goals
