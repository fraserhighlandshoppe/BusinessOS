## Core Questions (Extracted Knowledge)
1. **What problem are you trying to solve?** - Reveals pain points and use cases
2. **How did you hear about us?** - Identifies effective marketing channels
3. **What was your biggest concern before buying?** - Uncovers objection patterns
4. **On a scale of 1-10, how satisfied are you?** - Quantifies satisfaction
5. **What's one thing we could improve?** - Direct improvement feedback
6. **Would you recommend us to a friend?** - Measures advocacy/NPS potential
7. **How likely are you to buy again?** - Purchase intent indicator
8. **What outcome were you hoping to achieve?** - Aligns expectations with results
9. **What questions did you have before deciding?** - FAQ extraction
10. **What would make this product/service perfect?** - Feature prioritization

## Implementation Notes
- Keep surveys under 5 minutes
- Mix multiple choice with open-ended
- Always include demographic segmentation
- Ask permission for follow-up contact
