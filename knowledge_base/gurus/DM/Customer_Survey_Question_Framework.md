## Implementation Sequence:
1. **Define Research Objective** – Align survey goals with [[Customer_Avatar_Framework]] personas
2. **Select Question Types** – Choose between NPS, CSAT, and open-ended questions
3. **Select Optimal Questions** – Use [[Survey_Questions_Knowledge_Bank]] as template
4. **Design Distribution Channel** – Email, website pop-up, or in-app
5. **Collect Responses** – Target specific audience segments
6. **Analyze Results** – Interpret data using qualitative coding methods
7. **Create Action Plan** – Translate insights into operational improvements

## Key Principles:
- **Brevity** – Keep survey under 5 minutes completion time
- **Relevance** – Questions must align with customer journey stages
- **Actionability** – Each question should lead to measurable improvements
- **Anonymity Assurance** – Ensure respondents know responses are confidential
- **Follow-up Schedule** – Implement quarterly review cycles

## Metrics & KPIs:
- **Response Rate**: Target 30%+ of invited participants
- **Completion Rate**: Target >80% of started surveys
- **Question-Specific Success**: Each question should yield actionable insights
- **Insight-to-Action Rate**: % of insights leading to implemented changes
- **Net Promoter Score (NPS)**: Track for overall sentiment measurement
- **CSAT (Customer Satisfaction)**: Track for immediate satisfaction gauge
- **Feature Request Rate**: Count of suggestions for new functionality
