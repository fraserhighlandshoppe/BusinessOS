# Traffic Generation AI Skill

## Skill Overview
- **Skill Name**: Traffic Generation
- **Domain**: Marketing
- **Guru**: Digital Marketer
- **Source**: Ten Dollar A Day Traffic Plan

## Core Framework: The 6/3/1 Ratio

### Budget Allocation
- **$6**: Cold traffic (60%) - New audience introduction
- **$3**: Warm traffic (30%) - Engaged prospects
- **$1**: Hot traffic (10%) - Existing customers

### Traffic Types and Strategies

#### Cold Traffic (60% Budget)
**Goal**: Introduce brand to new audience
**Objectives**:
- Build awareness
- Establish authority
- Collect pixels for retargeting
- Segment audience for future offers

**Content Types**:
- Blog posts
- Social media updates
- Podcasts
- Content videos
- Lead magnets
- Quizzes and surveys
- Whitepapers

**Campaign Structure**:
1. Interest targeting (500K-1M audience size)
2. Ad copy matches landing page
3. Focus on value delivery
4. Success metric: Cost per pixel

#### Warm Traffic (30% Budget)
**Goal**: Convert interest into leads
**Audience**: People who showed interest (email subscribers, social followers, website visitors)
**Stage**: Evaluation - interested but not ready to purchase

**Offer Types**:
- Lead magnets (email exchange)
- Quiz/survey results (opt-in required)
- Free/paid webinars
- Flash sales ($7-10)
- Product demos
- Branding videos
- Books
- Free trials

#### Hot Traffic (10% Budget)
**Goal**: Reactivate and upsell existing customers
**Audience**: Past purchasers

**Goals**:
- Activation (remind inactive customers)
- High-dollar sales (increase customer value)

**Offer Types**:
- Events
- Paid webinars
- High-dollar offers
- Done-for-you services

## Implementation Steps

### Step 1: Audience Research
1. Identify authorities and thought leaders in niche
2. Determine media consumption habits
3. Map audience interests and behaviors
4. Create detailed customer avatar

### Step 2: Content Creation
1. Write backwards from call-to-action
2. Choose topic supporting CTA
3. Select post type (list, how-to, aggregator, crowdsource)
4. Edit for consumption (short paragraphs, bullet points)

### Step 3: Campaign Launch
1. Set budget according to 6/3/1 ratio
2. Create ad copy matching landing page
3. Implement pixel tracking
4. Launch and monitor cost per pixel

### Step 4: Optimization
1. Analyze cost per pixel
2. Refine targeting based on performance
3. Adjust budget allocation
4. Scale winning campaigns

## Key Metrics
- Cost per pixel (cold traffic)
- Cost per lead (warm traffic)
- Cost per acquisition (hot traffic)
- Return on ad spend (ROAS)
