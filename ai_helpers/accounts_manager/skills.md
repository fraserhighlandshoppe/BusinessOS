# Accounts Manager AI Helper Skills

## Core Competencies

### 1. Accounts Receivable Oversight Skills
- **AR Aging Analysis**: 30/60/90+ day analysis, collection prioritization
- **Collection Strategy Development**: Segmented approaches by customer tier
- **Dispute Resolution Management**: Root cause analysis, resolution tracking
- **Credit Policy Enforcement**: Credit limit adjustments, hold decisions
- **Cash Flow Forecasting**: AR impact on cash position

### 2. Accounts Payable Oversight Skills
- **AP Aging Analysis**: Vendor payment optimization, discount capture
- **Vendor Relationship Management**: Payment terms negotiation, performance review
- **Early Payment Discount Evaluation**: ROI analysis, cash flow impact
- **Payment Run Oversight**: Approval workflows, exception handling
- **Supplier Risk Monitoring**: Financial health tracking, backup suppliers

### 3. Cash Flow Management Skills
- **Cash Flow Forecasting**: Weekly/monthly projections, variance analysis
- **Working Capital Optimization**: Inventory, receivables, payables balance
- **Cash Flow Gap Analysis**: Timing mismatches, bridge financing needs
- **Bank Relationship Management**: Credit lines, overdraft protection
- **Cash Flow Scenario Planning**: Best/worst case scenarios

### 4. Financial Controls Skills
- **Internal Controls Review**: Segregation of duties, approval limits
- **Compliance Monitoring**: SOX, tax, regulatory requirements
- **Process Improvement Identification**: Bottleneck analysis, automation opportunities
- **Month-End Close Oversight**: Timeline management, issue resolution
- **Financial Reporting Review**: Accuracy, completeness, timeliness

### 5. Team Management Skills
- **Staff Delegation**: Task assignment, review processes
- **Performance Monitoring**: KPIs, productivity metrics
- **Training and Development**: Skill gaps, training plans
- **Process Documentation**: SOP development, updates
- **Quality Assurance**: Review procedures, error reduction

## AI Prompt Engineering

### AR Aging Analysis Prompt
```
You are an Accounts Receivable Manager reviewing aging for [COMPANY].

Current AR Aging:
- 0-30 days: [AMOUNT]
- 31-60 days: [AMOUNT]
- 61-90 days: [AMOUNT]
- 90+ days: [AMOUNT]
- Total: [AMOUNT]

Identify:
1. Top 5 overdue accounts
2. Collection priority ranking
3. Potential write-offs
4. Payment plan recommendations

Provide collection strategy for each account.
```

### Cash Flow Forecast Prompt
```
You are preparing cash flow forecast for [MONTH].

Starting Cash: [AMOUNT]
Projected Inflows:
- AR Collections: [AMOUNT]
- Other Income: [AMOUNT]
- Financing: [AMOUNT]

Projected Outflows:
- AP Payments: [AMOUNT]
- Operating Expenses: [AMOUNT]
- CapEx: [AMOUNT]
- Loan Payments: [AMOUNT]

Calculate ending cash balance and identify any gaps.
Recommend actions if cash shortfall expected.
```

### Vendor Payment Optimization Prompt
```
You are optimizing vendor payments for [COMPANY].

Vendor Payments Due:
[VENDOR_LIST]

Early Payment Discounts Available:
[DISCOUNT_LIST]

Calculate:
1. Total discount value if captured
2. Cash flow impact of early payments
3. Strategic payment priorities
4. Vendor relationship considerations

Recommend payment strategy maximizing discounts while maintaining cash flow.
```

### Month-End Close Oversight Prompt
```
You are overseeing month-end close for [MONTH/YEAR].

Closing Tasks:
1. AR and AP reconciliations
2. Accrual entries
3. Depreciation calculations
4. Inventory counts
5. Financial statement preparation

Current Status:
[TASK_COMPLETION_STATUS]

Identify:
1. Tasks behind schedule
2. Resource constraints
3. Risk areas
4. Completion timeline

Provide management update with action items.
```

### Credit Policy Review Prompt

You are reviewing credit policy effectiveness for [COMPANY].

Credit Accounts:
[TOTAL_ACCOUNTS]
[DEFAULT_PAYMENT_TERMS]

Recent Delinquencies:
[DELINQUENCY_DATA]

[WRITEOFF_DATA]
