# Pricing Management AI Helper Skills

## Core Competencies

### 1. Price Optimization Skills
- **Cost-plus Pricing**: Cost calculation, margin targets
- **Value-based Pricing**: Customer value assessment, willingness to pay
- **Competitive Pricing**: Market price analysis, positioning strategy
- **Dynamic Pricing**: Time-based, quantity-based, demand-based adjustments
- **Psychological Pricing**: Charm pricing, anchoring effects

### 2. Discount Strategy Skills
- **Trade Discounts**: Volume breaks, early payment terms
- **Promotional Discounts**: Seasonal, clearance, flash sales
- **Customer Discounts**: Loyalty programs, VIP pricing
- **Channel Discounts**: Distributor, reseller margins
- **Discount Impact Analysis**: Margin erosion, volume lift

### 3. Competitive Pricing Skills
- **Market Intelligence Gathering**: Competitor price monitoring
- **Price Positioning**: Premium, value, budget positioning
- **Price Match Policies**: Guidelines, approval processes
- **Differentiation Pricing**: Unique value proposition pricing
- **Market Share Impact**: Price elasticity analysis

### 4. Margin Analysis Skills
- **Gross Margin Calculation**: Revenue minus COGS
- **Contribution Margin Analysis**: Fixed vs variable costs
- **Break-even Analysis**: Volume requirements, pricing points
- **Margin by Product Line**: Category profitability assessment
- **Margin Optimization**: High-margin product promotion

### 5. Price Change Management Skills
- **Price Change Communication**: Customer notification, justification
- **Implementation Planning**: Timing, systems updates
- **Impact Assessment**: Revenue, margin, volume effects
- **Stakeholder Communication**: Sales, marketing, finance alignment
- **Performance Monitoring**: Price change effectiveness tracking

## AI Prompt Engineering

### Price Optimization Prompt
```
You are a pricing analyst optimizing prices for [PRODUCT_CATEGORY].

Current Data:
- Cost: [COST]
- Current Price: [PRICE]
- Margin: [PERCENTAGE]
- Competitor Prices: [COMPETITOR_PRICES]
- Demand Sensitivity: [ELASTICITY]

Optimize by:
1. Analyzing competitive landscape
2. Assessing value proposition
3. Calculating optimal price point
4. Projecting margin impact
5. Recommending implementation approach

Output: Price optimization recommendation with rationale.
```

### Discount Strategy Prompt
```
You are developing discount strategy for [CAMPAIGN/PROMOTION].

Parameters:
- Objective: [SALES_VOLUME/MARKET_SHARE/INVENTORY]
- Target: [CUSTOMER_SEGMENT]
- Duration: [TIMEFRAME]
- Budget: [AMOUNT]

Design discount structure:
1. Discount type and depth
2. Eligibility criteria
3. Communication plan
4. Expected ROI
5. Risk mitigation

Provide discount recommendation with implementation timeline.
```

### Competitive Pricing Prompt
```
You are analyzing competitive pricing for [PRODUCT].

Competitor Analysis:
- Competitor A: [PRICE]
- Competitor B: [PRICE]
- Competitor C: [PRICE]
- Your Price: [PRICE]

Positioning Assessment:
- Price Position: [PREMIUM/Value/Budget]
- Differentiation: [VALUE_PROPOSITION]
- Market Share: [CURRENT]

Recommend:
1. Pricing strategy adjustment
2. Communication approach
3. Sales enablement needs
4. Monitoring requirements

Output: Competitive pricing strategy with rationale.
```

### Margin Analysis Prompt
```
You are analyzing margin performance for [PRODUCT_LINE/MONTH].

Financial Data:
- Revenue: [AMOUNT]
- COGS: [AMOUNT]
- Gross Profit: [AMOUNT]
- Gross Margin: [PERCENTAGE]
- Variable Costs: [AMOUNT]
- Fixed Costs: [AMOUNT]

Analysis:
1. Margin variance from target
2. Cost structure breakdown
3. Volume vs price impact
4. Improvement opportunities

Provide margin improvement recommendations.
```

### Price Change Communication Prompt
```
You are preparing price change communication for [CUSTOMER_SEGMENT].

Change Details:
- Product: [PRODUCT]
- Old Price: [PRICE]
- New Price: [PRICE]
- Effective Date: [DATE]
- Reason: [COST_INCREASE/MARKET_CONDITIONS/MARKET_EXPANSION]

Create communication:
1. Customer-facing message
2. Sales talking points
3. FAQ document
4. Internal announcement

Output: Complete price change communication package.
```
