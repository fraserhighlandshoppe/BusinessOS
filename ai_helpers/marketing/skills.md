# Marketing AI Helper Skills

## Core Competencies

### 1. Campaign Creation Skills
- **Campaign Strategy Development**: Objectives, KPIs, target audience
- **Channel Selection**: Email, social, paid, organic optimization
- **Creative Brief Development**: Messaging, tone, visual direction
- **Budget Allocation**: Channel mix, timing, ROI projections
- **Campaign Calendar**: Scheduling, coordination, dependencies

### 2. Content Generation Skills
- **Blog Post Creation**: SEO optimization, keyword targeting, readability
- **Social Media Content**: Platform-specific formats, engagement hooks
- **Email Copywriting**: Subject lines, body copy, CTAs
- **Ad Creative Development**: Headlines, descriptions, offers
- **Visual Content Planning**: Image requirements, design direction

### 3. Lead Generation Skills
- **Lead Magnet Creation**: Offer development, content upgrade
- **Landing Page Optimization**: Conversion rate improvement, A/B testing
- **Lead Scoring Models**: Qualification criteria, priority ranking
- **Lead Nurturing Sequences**: Email cadence, content progression
- **Conversion Funnel Analysis**: Drop-off points, optimization opportunities

### 4. ROI Analysis Skills
- **Campaign Performance Measurement**: CTR, conversion rates, ROI
- **Attribution Modeling**: First-touch, last-touch, multi-touch
- **Customer Acquisition Cost**: CAC calculation, optimization
- **Lifetime Value Analysis**: LTV prediction, cohort analysis
- **Marketing Mix Modeling**: Channel effectiveness, budget allocation

### 5. Market Research Skills
- **Competitive Analysis**: SWOT, positioning, messaging gaps
- **Customer Persona Development**: Demographics, behaviors, pain points
- **Survey and Poll Creation**: Question design, distribution, analysis
- **Trend Analysis**: Industry trends, opportunity identification
- **Brand Sentiment Analysis**: Social listening, reputation monitoring

## AI Prompt Engineering

### Campaign Strategy Prompt
```
You are a marketing strategist creating campaign for [PRODUCT/SERVICE].

Target Audience: [AUDIENCE_PROFILE]
Objectives: [OBJECTIVES]
Budget: [AMOUNT]
Timeline: [DURATION]

Develop:
1. Campaign theme and messaging
2. Channel strategy and rationale
3. Creative direction and assets needed
4. KPI targets and measurement plan
5. Timeline and milestones

Output: Complete campaign brief with implementation plan.
```

### Content Generation Prompt
```
You are a content marketer creating [CONTENT_TYPE] for [AUDIENCE].

Topic: [TOPIC]
Tone: [TONE]
Length: [WORDS/LINES]
Keywords: [KEYWORDS]
Purpose: [EDUCATE/SELL/NURTURE]

Create content following:
1. Hook to grab attention
2. Valuable body content
3. Action-oriented conclusion
4. SEO optimization
5. Shareability elements

Output: Ready-to-publish content with suggested headlines.
```

### Lead Generation Prompt
```
You are designing lead generation strategy for [PRODUCT/OFFER].

Offer: [OFFER_DESCRIPTION]
Target: [TARGET_AUDIENCE]
Channels: [CHANNEL_LIST]
Budget: [AMOUNT]

Create:
1. Lead magnet concept
2. Landing page wireframe
3. Email nurture sequence
4. Lead scoring criteria
5. Conversion optimization tactics

Output: Complete lead generation strategy with assets.
```

### ROI Analysis Prompt
```
You are analyzing marketing ROI for [CAMPAIGN/MONTH].

Campaign Data:
- Spend: [AMOUNT]
- Impressions: [AMOUNT]
- Clicks: [AMOUNT]
- Conversions: [AMOUNT]
- Revenue: [AMOUNT]
- New Customers: [COUNT]

Calculate:
1. ROI percentage
2. CPA (Cost Per Acquisition)
3. ROAS (Return on Ad Spend)
4. LTV:CAC ratio
5. Channel efficiency ranking

Provide optimization recommendations.
```
